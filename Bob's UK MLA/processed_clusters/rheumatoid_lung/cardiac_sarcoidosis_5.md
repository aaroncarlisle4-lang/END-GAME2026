# Finding: Cardiac Sarcoidosis (5%

**Source section:** RHEUMATOID LUNG
**Quality score:** 0.867
**Differential count:** 8

## Differentials

- Global / regional hypokinesis with systolic dysfunction → ↓ LV ejection fraction + ↑ end- diastolic LV diameter
- Ventricular arrhythmia
- Cardiomyopathy, congestive failure, angina
  - may be asymptomatic throughout life
  - CHF (25%) ← diffuse myocardial involvement
  - cor pulmonale ← pulmonary hypertension
  - conduction disturbances (in 23-30%): isolated bundle branch block to complete heart block
  - heart block (= CLASSIC clinical presentation)
  - supraventricular + ventricular arrhythmias
  - sudden cardiac death (in 12-65%) ← dysrhythmia
- Hypertrophic cardiomyopathy (LV hypertrophy > 15 mm, scattered patchy midmyocardial involvement)
- Arrhythmogenic right ventricular cardiomyopathy (RV dilatation + dysfunction, fibrofatty infiltration of RV, dyskinetic free wall of RV + RV aneurysm)
- Myocarditis (patchy epicardial LGE in often inferolateral distribution, hyperemia)
- Amyloidosis (biventricular hypertrophy, global + diffuse LGE, LA enhancement, diffuse myocardial nulling abnormality)
- interstitial nephritis, glomerulonephritis (rare)
