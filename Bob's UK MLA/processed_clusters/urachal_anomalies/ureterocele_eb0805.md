**Source section:** URACHAL ANOMALIES
**Quality score:** 0.533

## Differentials

- Wall thickening ← edema from impacted stone / infection
- hypoplastic / absent ipsilateral trigone

- Multicystic dysplastic kidney (the further the orifice from normal site of insertion, the more dysplastic the kidney!)
