# Finding: Gas in Biliary Tree = Pneumobilia

**Source section:** GALLBLADDER POLYP
**Quality score:** 0.3
**Differential count:** 6

## Differentials

- Incompetent sphincter of Oddi (after sphincterotomy / passage of a gallstone)
- Gallstone ileus
- Emphysematous cholecystitis (actually in gallbladder)
- Trauma
- Ulcer (duodenal ulcer perforating into CBD)
- Postoperative (eg, cholecystoenterostomy)

## Context

√  gas outlines choledochus ± gallbladder √  peripheral branches of bile ducts not filled Pneumobilia is a common finding with patent biliary stents. It may not be present when the stent is occluded ← no communication with the duodenum.
