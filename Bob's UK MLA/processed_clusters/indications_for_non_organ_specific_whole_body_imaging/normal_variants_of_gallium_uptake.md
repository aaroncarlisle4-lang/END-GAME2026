# Finding: Normal Variants of Gallium Uptake

**Source section:** Indications for Non-organ-specific Whole Body Imaging
**Quality score:** 0.6
**Differential count:** 9

## Differentials

- Breasts: increased uptake under stimulus of menarche, estrogens, pregnancy, lactation, phenothiazine medication, renal failure, hypothalamic lesion
- Liver: suppressed uptake by chemotherapeutic agents / high levels of circulating iron / irradiation / severe acute liver disease
- Lung: prominent uptake after lymphangiography
- Spleen: increased uptake in splenomegaly
- Thymus: uptake in children
- Salivary glands: uptake within first 6 months after radiation therapy to neck (may persist for years)
- Epiphyseal plates in children
- Previous steroid therapy, chemotherapy, and radiation therapy may decrease Ga uptake
- Healing surgical incision
