# Finding: Bilateral Enlargement of Salivary Glands

**Source section:** Parotid Gland Enlargement
**Quality score:** 0.567

- Sarcoidosis
- Lymphoma

## Context

Salivary Gland Neoplasm Frequency:   90-95% occur in parotid gland, 5% in submandibular + sublingual glands; only 1% of all pediatric tumors! ◊  Most salivary gland neoplasms are benign (70%-80%) and found in parotid glands (80- 90%)! ◊  The smaller the salivary gland the higher the rate of malignancy: parotid (20-25%) < submandibular (40-50%) < sublingual + minor salivary glands in tongue / floor of mouth / retromolar region (50-81%) ◊  Spread to regional lymph nodes is infrequent!
