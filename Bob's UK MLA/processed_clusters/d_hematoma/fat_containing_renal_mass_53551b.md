# Finding: Fat-containing Renal Mass

**Source section:** D. HEMATOMA
**Quality score:** 0.733
**Differential count:** 7

## Differentials

- Angiomyolipoma
- Lipoma, liposarcoma
- Teratoma
- Wilms tumor
- Xanthogranulomatous pyelonephritis
- Oncocytoma engulfing renal sinus fat
- Renal cell carcinoma (a)  invasion of perirenal fat (b)  intratumoral metaplasia into fatty marrow (in 32% of RCCs < 3 cm in diameter) ◊  If a lesion contains fat + calcium RCC is likely, NOT angiomyolipoma!
