# Finding: Monostotic Fibrous Dysplasia (70-80%

**Source section:** FIBROMA OF TENDON SHEATH
**Quality score:** 0.467
**Differential count:** 5

## Differentials

- usually asymptomatic until 2nd-3rd decade
  - incidental discovery: obvious deformity / dull aching pain
- pain ← pathologic fracture Location:  ribs (6-28%), proximal femur (23%), tibia, craniofacial bones (10-25%)[frontal, sphenoid, maxillary, ethmoid], humerus
- No conversion to polyostotic form
- No increase in size over time
- Disease becomes inactive at puberty
