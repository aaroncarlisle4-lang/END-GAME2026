# Finding: Hip Dislocation

**Source section:** DISLOCATION
**Quality score:** 0.667
**Differential count:** 11

## Differentials

- POSTERIOR HIP DISLOCATION (80-85%)
- adducted lower extremity flexed at hip
- ANTERIOR HIP DISLOCATION (5-10%)
- superoanterior / pubic hip dislocation
  - lower extremity in external rotation
- CENTRAL ACETABULAR FRACTURE-DISLOCATION
- 50-75% not clinically diagnosed initially ← self-reduction!
- Temporary immobilization + rehabilitation: successful in 75%
- Surgery: fixation of osteochondral fragments if > 1 cm2, medial capsule repair, lateral retinacular release, vastus medialis et lateralis rearrangement, medial retinaculum reefing
- Shoulder Dislocation
- Sternoclavicular Dislocation (3%)
- POSTERIOR STERNOCLAVICULAR DISLOCATION
