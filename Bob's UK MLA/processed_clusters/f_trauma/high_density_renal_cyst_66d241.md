# Finding: High-density Renal Cyst

**Source section:** F. TRAUMA
**Quality score:** 0.867
**Differential count:** 8

## Differentials

- Proteinaceous content (20-40 HU)
- Hemorrhage (> 40 HU)
- Infection
- Calcification
- Communication with calyx
- Streak artifact
- clear straw-colored serous fluid
- almost always asymptomatic

## Context

=  completely homogeneous cyst content ≥ 20 HU (often 50-90 HU) without enhancement √  simple cyst on US √  complex cyst on US √  pseudoenhancement (see above) ◊  Cyst wall cannot be evaluated in high-attenuation cysts! ◊  Considered a Bosniak Class II lesion if: ≤ 3 cm in size, partially exophytic, round, sharply marginated, homogeneous, nonenhancing ◊  A homogeneous renal mass with attenuation > 70 HU on NECT is benign with a 99% probability! Cyst of Renal Pelvis =  generic term for spherical fluid-filled mass intimately attached to renal pelvis without connection to pelvicaliceal system Pre
