# Finding: Ovarian Mass with Low Resistive Flow

**Source section:** ADNEXA
**Quality score:** 0.6
**Differential count:** 6

## Differentials

- Inflammatory mass
- Endometrioma
- Mesothelial cyst
- Benign tumor: serous / mucinous cystadenoma, mature cystic teratoma, cystadenofibroma
- Malignant ovarian tumor ◊  Some malignancies have high resistive blood flow!

## Context

Low resistance flow = RI < 0.5 or PI ≤ 1.0
