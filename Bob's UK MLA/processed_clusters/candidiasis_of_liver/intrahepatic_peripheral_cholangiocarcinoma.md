# Finding: Intrahepatic Peripheral Cholangiocarcinoma

**Source section:** CANDIDIASIS OF LIVER
**Quality score:** 0.6
**Differential count:** 6

## Differentials

- NO jaundice
- abdominal pain, discomfort, anorexia, weight loss
  - pruritus, jaundice (late symptom)
- Invasion of right / left hepatic duct with extension to 2nd-order biliary radicles
- Atrophy of 1 hepatic lobe + involvement of contra- lateral portal vein branch / 2nd-order biliary radicle
- Vascular encasement / invasion of main portal vein or main hepatic artery
- Metastatic disease: lymph node / distant
