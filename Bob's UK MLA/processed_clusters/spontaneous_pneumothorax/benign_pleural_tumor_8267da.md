# Finding: Benign Pleural Tumor

**Source section:** Spontaneous Pneumothorax
**Quality score:** 0.8
**Differential count:** 9

## Differentials

- Loculated pleural effusion ("vanishing tumor")
- Organized empyema
- Local benign mesothelioma
- Subpleural lipoma: may erode adjacent rib
- Mesothelial cyst
- Neural tumor: schwannoma, neurofibroma
- Solitary fibrous tumor of pleura (rare)
- Fibrin bodies =  3-4 cm large tumorlike concentrations of fibrin forming in serofibrinous pleural effusions; usually near lung base
- Hematoma
