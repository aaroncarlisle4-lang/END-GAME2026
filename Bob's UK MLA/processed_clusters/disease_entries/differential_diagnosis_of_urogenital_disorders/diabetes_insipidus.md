# Disease: DIABETES INSIPIDUS

**Source section:** DIFFERENTIAL DIAGNOSIS OF UROGENITAL DISORDERS

## Definition

characterized by daily production of very large volume of dilute urine (specific gravity < HYPOTHALAMIC DIABETES INSIPIDUS VASOPRESSIN-SENSITIVE DIABETES INSIPIDUS vasopressin (ADH) production is reduced to < 10% compulsive intake of large amounts of fluid → inhibits normal vasopressin production poor reabsorption of water in collecting ducts ← end-organ resistance to vasopressin

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** atrophic supraoptic nucleus

## Imaging Findings

### General

- bilateral hydroureteronephrosis

## Clinical

- **Rx:** thiazide diuretics, low-salt diet, encouragement of frequent micturition, indomethacin
