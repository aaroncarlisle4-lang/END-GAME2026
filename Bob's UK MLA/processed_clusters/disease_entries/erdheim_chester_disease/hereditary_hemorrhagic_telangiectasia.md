# Disease: HEREDITARY HEMORRHAGIC TELANGIECTASIA

**Source section:** ERDHEIM-CHESTER DISEASE

## Definition

RENDU-OSLER-WEBER SYNDROME group of autosomal dominant inherited disorders that result in a variety of systemic dilatation of small mucosal blood vessels

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 1-20÷100,000; M = F
- **Age:** manifestation usually during adulthood
- **Path:** direct connections between arteries + veins with absence of capillaries (telangiectases
- **Histo:** absence of cellular intervals in vascular endothelial cells
- **Location:** lips, tongue, floor of oral cavity, palate, fingers, face, conjunctiva, trunk, arms,

## Imaging Findings
