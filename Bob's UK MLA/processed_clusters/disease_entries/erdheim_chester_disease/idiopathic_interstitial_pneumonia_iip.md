## Key Facts

- **Path:** temporally homogeneous organizing diffuse alveolar damage; little mature collagen
- **Histo:** extensive diffuse alveolar damage
- **Mean age:** 50 years (wide range); M = F
- **Location:** bilateral symmetric mainly in lower lung zones
- **Frequency:** 14-36% (second most common IIP)
- **Age:** 40-50 years (1 decade younger than IPF); M = F
- **Prevalence:** 20-30% of all chronic infiltrative lung disease
- **Distribution:** lower lung lobe predominance, peripheral; more diffuse + uniform

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** death within 1-6 months (60-90%); recovery in 12%; frequently progression
- **Rx:** supportive with oxygen supplementation; corticosteroids in early phase
- **N.B.:** pleural effusion and > 11 mm large / growing nodules concerning for coexisting
- **Cx:** malignant lymphoma (in 5%)
