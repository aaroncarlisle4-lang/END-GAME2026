# Disease: EXTRINSIC ALLERGIC ALVEOLITIS

**Source section:** ERDHEIM-CHESTER DISEASE

## Definition

HYPERSENSITIVITY PNEUMONITIS characterized by an inappropriate host response to repeatedly inhaled organic allergens often bacteria < 1 µm in diameter with morphologic characteristics of fungi; found in soil, renewed exposure to antigen with improvement between attacks usually within enclosed less intense but continuous exposure to inhaled antigens, usually in domestic environment prolonged insidious dust exposure
