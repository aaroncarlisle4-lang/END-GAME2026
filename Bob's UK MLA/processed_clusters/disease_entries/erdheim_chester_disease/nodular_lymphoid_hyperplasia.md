# Disease: NODULAR LYMPHOID HYPERPLASIA

**Source section:** ERDHEIM-CHESTER DISEASE

## Definition

PSEUDOLYMPHOMA = LOCALIZED MASSLIKE FORM OF LIP reactive benign lesion = uncommon localized lymphoid hyperplasia; no progression to

## Key Facts

- **Path:** usually single involvement of small area of lung
- **Histo:** aggregates of polymorphous large + small lymphoid cells (B and T cells); multiple
- **Location:** subpleural / ± peribronchial

## Imaging Findings

### CXR

- well-demarcated dense infiltrate(s) as nodule / mass / masslike consolidation

### CT

- infiltrate typically in central location extending to visceral pleura
- prominent central air bronchogram
- NO invasion of adjacent bronchus / pleural space
- NO hilar / mediastinal lymphadenopathy
- NO pleural effusion

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** occasionally progression to non-Hodgkin lymphoma
- **Rx:** most patients respond well to steroids initially
