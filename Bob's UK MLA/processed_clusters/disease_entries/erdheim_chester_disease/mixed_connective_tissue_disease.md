# Disease: MIXED CONNECTIVE TISSUE DISEASE

**Source section:** ERDHEIM-CHESTER DISEASE

## Definition

OVERLAP SYNDROME disorder that shares distinctive features of ≥ 2 different connective tissue diseases in same

## Imaging Findings

### General

- interstitial lung disease (21-66%):
- groundglass opacities predominantly in lower lung fields
- pulmonary arterial hypertension (in 10-45%):
- pleural thickening / effusion (<10%)
- pulmonary vasculitis, thromboembolism
- alveolar hemorrhage
- respiratory muscle dysfunction

## Clinical

- **Cx:** aspiration pneumonia ← esophageal dysmotility
