# Disease: NEAR DROWNING

**Source section:** ERDHEIM-CHESTER DISEASE

## Definition

asphyxiation ← water inhalation followed by survival for a minimum of 24 hour laryngospasm + swallowing of water into the stomach

## Imaging Findings

### General

- NO roentgenographic abnormality
- Kerley lines, peribronchial cuffing
- patchy perihilar alveolar airspace consolidation
- pressure edema
- permeability edema ← hypoxia + diffuse alveolar damage
- no roentgenographic abnormality
- central extensive fluffy areas of increased opacity (alveolar edema indistinguishable from
- tendency for opacities to coalesce
- hyaline membrane formation = considerable loss of protein from blood

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** resolution within 24-48 hours (under therapy)
- **Cx:** ARDS, aspiration of gastric fluid, infection by fresh-water saprophytic bacteria
