# Disease: SCINTIGRAPHY OF NEUROENDOCRINE TUMORS

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL SCINTIGRAPHY

## Definition

14-amino acid peptide that inhibits peptide + hormone synthesis by neuroendocrine cells 8-amino acid analog of somatostatin

## Key Facts

- **Location:** hypothalamus, pituitary gland, thyroid gland, adrenal medulla, GI tract
- **Distribution:** kidneys, spleen, gallbladder, liver, urinary bladder, intestines, thyroid,
