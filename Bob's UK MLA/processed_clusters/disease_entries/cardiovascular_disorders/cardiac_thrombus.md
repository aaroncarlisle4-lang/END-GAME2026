# Disease: CARDIAC THROMBUS

**Source section:** CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Associated with:** mitral valve disease
- **Incidence:** 4-7% with suspected pulmonary embolism

## Imaging Findings

### General

- atrial dilatation
- irregular / lobulated border
- microcavitations
- laminated appearance
- hyperechoic mass (DDx: crista terminalis, myxoma)
- absence of mobility
- multiple lesions favor thrombus
- usually NO enhancement:
- peripheral enhancement ← neovascularization in chronic thrombus
- homogeneous attenuation on CT
- heterogeneous signal on SE MR images
- low-signal intensity on GRE MR images

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** 80-100% mortality in untreated patients
- **Rx:** anticoagulation, thrombectomy, thrombolysis, surgical resection
