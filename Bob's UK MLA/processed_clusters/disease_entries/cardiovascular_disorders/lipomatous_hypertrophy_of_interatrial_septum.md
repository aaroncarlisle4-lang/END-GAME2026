# Disease: LIPOMATOUS HYPERTROPHY OF INTERATRIAL SEPTUM

**Source section:** CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

LHIAS = excessive deposition of brown fat in interatrial septum

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 3%; ↑ with older age
- **Associated with:** obesity, pulmonary emphysema
- **Histo:** unencapsulated infiltration of adipocytes (brown fat) between atrial muscle cells, NOT

## Imaging Findings

### General

- fatty mass of dumbbell shape ← sparing of fossa ovalis
- no contrast enhancement
- increase in mediastinal and epicardial fat
- ± focally increased FDG activity = mean SUV of 1.84 (range, 0.48-3.48) ← metabolically active

## Clinical

- **Cx:** supraventricular arrhythmias, vena caval obstruction
