# Disease: TRICUSPID REGURGITATION / INSUFFICIENCY

**Source section:** CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Imaging Findings

### General

- normal / reduced pulmonary vascularity
- cardiomegaly ← RA + RV hypertrophy and enlargement
- distension of IVC > SVC
- systolic reversal of hepatic vein flow with normal sinus rhythm
- dilatation of RA
- flattening / leftward bowing of interventricular septum during diastole
- vena contracta (= smallest stream diameter) width > 0.7 cm
- color flow jet area > 10 cm2
- proximal isovelocity surface area radius > 0.9 cm
- decreased S wave = S wave no longer as deep as the D wave, gradually rising with degree
- one large a-S-v complex in severe incompetence

### CT

- RV displaced to the left
- interventricular septum bowed to the left
- hepatic venous congestion
- emphysema, pulmonary hypertension, left heart disease

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** if untreated → RV dysfunction, congestive hepatopathy, cardiac cirrhosis
