# Disease: PULMONARY ARTERY PSEUDOANEURYSM

**Source section:** CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

tear / disruption of layers of vessel wall with extravasation of blood contained by adventitia /

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** rare
- **Associated with:** patent ductus arteriosus

## Imaging Findings

### CXR

- stable / increasing focal lung mass

### CT

- enhancing round lung mass isointense to central pulmonary a.

## Clinical

- **Cx:** 100% mortality with rupture
