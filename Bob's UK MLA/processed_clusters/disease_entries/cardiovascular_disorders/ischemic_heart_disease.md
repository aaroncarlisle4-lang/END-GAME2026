# Disease: ISCHEMIC HEART DISEASE

**Source section:** CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

CORONARY ARTERY DISEASE (CAD) myocardial scar / severe myocardial ischemia perfusion abnormality on poststress images normalizes on resting images 20-30% improvement in regional activity using a reference profile (= database normalized to specific gender, band of pixels with negative counts around hot objects during SPECT

## Key Facts

- **Incidence:** 1.5 million annually; leading cause of death in industrial nations
