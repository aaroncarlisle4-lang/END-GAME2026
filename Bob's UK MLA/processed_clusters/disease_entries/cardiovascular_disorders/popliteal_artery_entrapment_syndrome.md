# Disease: POPLITEAL ARTERY ENTRAPMENT SYNDROME

**Source section:** CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

vascular compression syndrome of popliteal artery (PopA)

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 0.165% of young males entering military service

## Imaging Findings

### General

- posterior tibial pulse obliterated during active plantar flexion against resistance /
- ankle-arm index reduced during active muscle contraction
- Doppler waveforms of posterior tibial artery diminished during muscle contractions
- medial deviation of artery (29%), popliteal stenosis (11%), poststenotic dilatation (8%)
- arteriography with typical medial deviation of popliteal artery before + after gastrocnemius
- popliteal artery thrombosis / occlusion

## Clinical

- **Cx:** popliteal artery aneurysm
