# Disease: CORONARY ARTERY FISTULA

**Source section:** CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

direct precapillary connection between branch of coronary a. + lumen of heart chamber /

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 0.05-0.25% of coronary angiographies; 0.002% of general population

## Imaging Findings

### General

- may have normal CXR (in small shunts)
- cardiomegaly + shunt vascularity (in large shunts)

### ANGIO

- dilated tortuous coronary artery with anomalous connection
- single (most) / multiple communications / network of fine vessels

## Clinical

- **Cx:** myocardial ischemia / sudden cardiac death (in large shunt), CHF, endocarditis,
- **Rx:** observation, surgical ligation, transcatheter embolization
