# Disease: SUPERIOR VENA CAVA SYNDROME

**Source section:** CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

obstruction of SVC with development of collateral pathways

## Imaging Findings

### General

- superior mediastinal widening (64%)
- encasement / compression / occlusion of SVC
- dilated cervical + superficial thoracic veins (80%)
- SVC thrombus

### NUC

- increased tracer uptake in quadrate lobe + posterior aspect of medial segment of left lobe
