# Disease: EOSINOPHILIC HEART DISEASE

**Source section:** CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

manifestation of hypereosinophilic syndrome

## Imaging Findings

### MR

- ventricular wall thickening
- regional wall motion abnormalities
- extensive myocardial hyperintensity on T2WI
- CHARACTERISTIC 3-layered appearance of ventricular wall:
- outer normal nulled (black) myocardium
- adjacent enhancing hyperintense subendocardium ← fibrosis
- innermost nonenhancing mural thrombus
- pericardial effusion with variable degree of pericardial enhancement ← pericarditis (rare)
- endocardial thickening, cardiac mural thrombus
- restrictive ventricular diastolic (filling) dysfunction ← reduced size, atrial enlargement,
- nonenhancing intracardiac mural thrombus
- fibrotic endocardial thickening
