# Disease: BACTERIAL ENDOCARDITIS

**Source section:** CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Incidence:** 1.7-6.2÷100,000 annually in developed countries

## Imaging Findings

### General

- exaggerated valve motion (= disintegration of suture line + regurgitation)

### CT

- peripheral cavitary (septic) lung nodules → assess for right-sided valvular vegetations
- usually discrete focal echodensities with sharp edges; may show fuzzy / shaggy
- may appear as shaggy echoes that prolapse when the valve is closed (DDx to mitral
- routinely depicts vegetations > 1 cm in diameter
- depicts perivalvular abscess + pseudoaneurysm
- septic + peripheral emboli

## Clinical

- **Cx:** bundle-branch block, fistulation, refractory sepsis
