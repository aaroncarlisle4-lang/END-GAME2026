# Disease: SPLENIC ARTERY ANEURYSM

**Source section:** CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

most frequent of visceral artery aneurysms

## Key Facts

- **Etiology:** medial degeneration with superimposed atherosclerosis, congenital, mycotic,
- **Location:** intra- / extrasplenic

## Imaging Findings

### General

- calcified wall of aneurysm (²/³)

## Clinical

- **Cx:** rupture of aneurysm (6-9%, higher during pregnancy) especially if > 1.5 cm in diameter
