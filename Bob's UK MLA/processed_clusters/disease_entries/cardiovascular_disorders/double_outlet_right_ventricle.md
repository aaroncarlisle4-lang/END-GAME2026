# Disease: DOUBLE-OUTLET RIGHT VENTRICLE

**Source section:** CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

DORV = TAUSSIG-BING HEART most of the aorta + pulmonary artery arise from the RV ← maldevelopment of conotruncus

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 127÷1,000,000 live births
- **Associated with:** VSD (100%), pulmonary stenosis / atresia (75%), mitral stenosis / atresia,

## Imaging Findings

### General

- NO continuity between mitral valve + adjacent semilunar valve (PATHOGNOMONIC)
- aorta posterior / parallel / anterior to pulmonary artery:
- aorta and pulmonary artery side by side with aorta in D-malposition on the right (most
- aorta overriding the interventricular septum with predominant connection to RV
- normal / hypoplastic / absent LV
