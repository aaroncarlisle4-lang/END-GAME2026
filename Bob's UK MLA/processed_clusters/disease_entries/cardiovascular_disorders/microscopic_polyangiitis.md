# Disease: MICROSCOPIC POLYANGIITIS

**Source section:** CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

MICROSCOPIC POLYARTERITIS = HYPERSENSITIVITY VASCULITIS = LEUKOCYTOCLASTIC pauci-immune necrotizing small-vessel angiitis without granulomatous inflammation

## Key Facts

- **Path:** necrotizing arteritis identical to polyarteritis nodosa but in vessels smaller than arteries
- **Location:** skin, mucous membranes, lung, brain, heart, GI tract, kidney, muscle

## Imaging Findings

### Kidney

- glomerulonephritis (90%)

### Lung

- pulmonary infiltrates ← capillaritis

### CNS (involved in 37-72%)

- mononeuritis multiplex
- cerebral hemorrhage, nonhemorrhagic cerebral infarction
- pachymeningitis
- variable degrees of small-vessel disease involving white and gray matter

## Clinical

- **Rx:** removal of offending agent
