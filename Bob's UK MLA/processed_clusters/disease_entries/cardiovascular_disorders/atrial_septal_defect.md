# Disease: ATRIAL SEPTAL DEFECT

**Source section:** CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

secundum type ASD serves essential compensatory function in: persistent opening at site of foramen ovale defect of atrioventricular endocardial cushion CORONARY SINUS DEFECT anomalous drainage of right pulmonary vein into RA (= to right of leftward deviated septum ASD + mitral stenosis

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 8-10-14% of all CHD; M÷F = 1÷3
- **Age:** presentation frequently > age 40 secondary to benign course
- **Location:** in the center of the atrial chamber at fossa ovalis
- **Size:** large defect of 1-3 cm in diameter
- **Associated with:** heterotaxy, polysplenia, complex CHDs
