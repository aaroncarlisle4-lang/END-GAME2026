# Disease: BICUSPID AORTIC VALVE

**Source section:** CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

single fused commissure at birth resulting in 2 separate cusps of unequal size (usually fused L

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 1-2% in general population
- **Genetics:** autosomal dominant with incomplete penetrance
- **Histo:** cystic medial degeneration; ↑ levels of matrix metalloproteinases; ↓ levels of fibrillin-1
- **Associated with:** dilatation of aortic root (50%) + ascending aorta + transverse arch aortic;

## Clinical

- **Cx:** stenosis, regurgitation, endocarditis, aneurysmal dilatation of ascending aorta, aortic
