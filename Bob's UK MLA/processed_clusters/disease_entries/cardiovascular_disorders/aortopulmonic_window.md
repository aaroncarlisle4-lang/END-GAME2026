# Disease: AORTOPULMONIC WINDOW

**Source section:** CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

AORTOPULMONARY WINDOW large round / oval communication between left wall of ascending aorta + right wall of proximal communication near semilunar valves (70-96%) distal communication near pulmonary bifurcation at level of right pulmonary artery total absence of aortopulmonary septum (5%)

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 0.1% of all CHD
- **Associated with:** other congenital cardiac anomalies (in up to 44%)

## Imaging Findings

### CXR

- shunt vascularity
- cardiomegaly ← LA + LV enlarged
- diminutive aortic knob
- prominent pulmonary trunk ← arterial pHTN
- defect several mm above aortic valve
- pulmonary valve identified (DDx to truncus arteriosus)

## Clinical

- **Rx:** surgery (pulmonary artery flap, pericardial patch); nonsurgical (Amplatzer device
