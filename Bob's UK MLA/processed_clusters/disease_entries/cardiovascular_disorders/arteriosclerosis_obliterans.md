# Disease: ARTERIOSCLEROSIS OBLITERANS

**Source section:** CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

ASO = HARDENING OF THE ARTERIES

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 2.7% (in 1.0% of < 60 years of age, in 3.4% of ≥ 65 years of age); 2.4 million
- **Etiology:** unknown
- **Histo:** deposition of lipids, blood products, carbohydrates, begins as disruption of intimal
- **Age:** 50-70 years; M > F (after menopause)
