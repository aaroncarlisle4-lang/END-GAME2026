# Disease: COR TRIATRIATUM

**Source section:** CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

rare congenital anomaly in which a fibromuscular septum with a single stenotic / fenestrated /

## Imaging Findings

### General

- pulmonary venous distention + interstitial edema + dilatation of pulmonary trunk + pulmonary
- enlarged RA + RV
- mild enlargement of LA

### ANGIO

- dividing membrane on levophase of pulmonary arteriogram

### MR

- accessory chamber (representing the common pulmonary vein) communicates with LA
- accessory chamber lies always above level of LA appendage

## Clinical

- **Rx:** surgical excision of obstructing membrane
