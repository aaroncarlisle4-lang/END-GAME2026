# Disease: IDIOPATHIC DILATATION OF PULMONARY TRUNK

**Source section:** CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

CONGENITAL ANEURYSM OF PULMONARY ARTERY

## Key Facts

- **Age:** adolescence; M < F

## Imaging Findings

### General

- dilated main pulmonary artery causes a round bulge at the mediastinal border simulating a
- normal peripheral pulmonary vascularity
- normal pulmonary arterial pulsations
- NO lateralization of pulmonary flow

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** nonprogressive
