# Disease: TROUSSEAU SYNDROME

**Source section:** CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

PARANEOPLASTIC THROMBOEMBOLISM

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 1-11%; higher in terminally ill cancer patients

## Imaging Findings

### General

- deep vein thrombosis
- pulmonary embolism
- nonbacterial thrombotic endocarditis   (echocardiography)
