# Disease: AZYGOS CONTINUATION OF IVC

**Source section:** CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

INTERRUPTED IVC WITH AZYGOS / HEMIAZYGOS CONTINUATION = ABSENCE OF THE HEPATIC

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 0.6%
- **Etiology:** formation failure of right subcardinal-hepatic venous anastomosis → atrophy of right
