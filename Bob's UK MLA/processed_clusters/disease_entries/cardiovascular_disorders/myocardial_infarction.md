# Disease: MYOCARDIAL INFARCTION

**Source section:** CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

MI = myocardial cell death ← prolonged ischemia overwhelming cellular repair mechanisms saccular protrusion of LV wall of mechanically weak necrotic / scarred fibrotic wall VENTRICULAR SEPTAL RUPTURE POSTMYOCARDIAL INFARCTION SYNDROME = LATE PERICARDITIS

## Key Facts

- **Incidence:** 1,500,000 annually in USA resulting in 500,000 deaths (50% occur in asymptomatic
- **Peak age:** 40-65 years; more severe + lethal in women
- **Location:** anterior + apical walls (70-85%)
- **Path:** myocardial rupture contained by parietal pericardium + thrombus + scar tissue =
- **Etiology:** autoimmune reaction to myocardial infarction

- **Cx:** myocardium is prone to rupture during 3rd-14th day post infarction
- **Prognosis:** 90-95% survival for hospital admissions
- **Rx:** emergency surgery (VSD patch repair + coronary artery bypass grafting)
