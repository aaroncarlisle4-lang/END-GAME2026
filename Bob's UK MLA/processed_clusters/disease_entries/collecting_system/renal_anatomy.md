# Disease: RENAL ANATOMY

**Source section:** COLLECTING SYSTEM

## Definition

central core of medullary tissue enveloped by 1.04 ± 0.22 branching of main renal artery prior to reaching hilum tiny vessels perfusing the renal capsule coursing tangentially to renal margin vessel with the greatest diameter vessel with smaller diameter segmental artery originating from aorta / iliac a. segmental artery arising from superior mesenteric artery / internal spermatic artery

## Imaging Findings

### General

- separation of upper + lower groups of calices
- indentation of cortical contour + echogenic line (= interrenicular septum = junctional
- ren lobatus (= interlobar surface grooves) present in fetus and infant, rare in adulthood
- assimilation of independent lobes > 28 weeks GA makes renal surface smoother
- cortex may be more echogenic than adjacent normal liver / spleen
- increase in corticomedullary differentiation
- hypoechoic pyramids appear relatively large
- renal sinus echogenicity less prominent

## Clinical

- **N.B.:** may be misinterpreted as dilated calices / renal cystic disease
