# Disease: ANALGESIC NEPHROPATHY

**Source section:** COLLECTING SYSTEM

## Definition

renal damage from ingestion of salicylates in combination with phenacetin / acetaminophen in

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** USA (2-10%), Australia (20%)
- **Age:** middle-aged; M÷F = 1÷4

## Imaging Findings

### General

- papillary necrosis
- scarring of renal parenchyma ("wavy outline"); bilateral in 66%, unilateral in 5%
- renal atrophy
- papillary urothelial tumors in calices / pelvis (mostly TCC / squamous cell carcinoma), in 5%
