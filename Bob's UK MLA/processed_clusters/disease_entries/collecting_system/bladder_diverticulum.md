# Disease: BLADDER DIVERTICULUM

**Source section:** COLLECTING SYSTEM

## Definition

cavity formed by herniation of bladder mucosa through muscular wall, joined to bladder CONGENITAL / IDIOPATHIC DIVERTICULUM

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 1.7% in children

## Imaging Findings

### General

- hypoechoic fluid-filled structure continuous with bladder wall
- may appear complex ← thickened wall / debris / soft-tissue component
- may contain calculi
- in 3% single diverticulum
- in 50% multiple diverticula
