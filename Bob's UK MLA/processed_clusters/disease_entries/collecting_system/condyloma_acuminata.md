# Disease: CONDYLOMA ACUMINATA

**Source section:** COLLECTING SYSTEM

## Definition

VENEREAL WART = PENILE SQUAMOUS PAPILLOMA

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** squamous papilloma
- **Prevalence:** 0.5-5% of male patients with cutaneous disease

## Imaging Findings

### Skin

- multiple papillary filling defects in anterior urethra
- ± extension into bladder (rare)

## Clinical

- **N.B.:** retrograde urethrography NOT recommended as it may cause retrograde seeding!
- **Rx:** instillation of podophyllin, thiotepa, 5-fluorouracil into urethra
