# Disease: EXTRAGONADAL GERM CELL TUMOR

**Source section:** COLLECTING SYSTEM

## Definition

PRIMARY EXTRAGONADAL GERM CELL TUMOR

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 1.0-2.5% of germ cell tumors; M > F
- **Histo:** seminoma (rare in retroperitoneum) / nonseminomatous germ cell tumor (embryonal

## Imaging Findings

### General

- large lobulated well-defined homogeneous solid mass with ringlike / speckled
- hypointense fibrous septa on T2WI
- contrast enhancement of septa
- heterogeneous tumor with areas of hemorrhage + necrosis
- heterogeneous contrast enhancement
- ± flow voids ← hypervascularity
- ± invasion of adjacent structures

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** worse compared to gonadal location
