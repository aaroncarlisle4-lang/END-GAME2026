# Disease: ALKALINE-ENCRUSTED CYSTITIS AND PYELITIS

**Source section:** COLLECTING SYSTEM

## Definition

chronic severe urinary tract infection affecting urothelial lining

## Key Facts

- **Path:** mucosal inflammation + encrustation of urothelial lining with struvite + calcium

## Imaging Findings

### NECT

- diffuse thin / coarse superficial linear urothelial calcifications

## Clinical

- **Cx:** septic shock, graft failure
- **Rx:** antibiotics, oral acidification of urine
