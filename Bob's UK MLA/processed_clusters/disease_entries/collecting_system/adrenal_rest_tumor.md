# Disease: ADRENAL REST TUMOR

**Source section:** COLLECTING SYSTEM

## Definition

ADRENOCORTICAL REST

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 7-15% of newborns; 1.6% of adults
- **Location:** near testicular hilum; usually bilateral

## Imaging Findings

### US

- multiple hypoechoic nodules
- may become hyperechoic with acoustic shadowing

### MR

- nodules isointense relative to normal testis on T1WI
- nodules hypointense relative to normal testis on T2WI
- diffuse enhancement after contrast administration
