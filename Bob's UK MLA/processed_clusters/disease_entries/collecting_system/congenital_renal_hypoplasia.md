# Disease: CONGENITAL RENAL HYPOPLASIA

**Source section:** COLLECTING SYSTEM

## Definition

miniaturization with reduction in number of renal lobes, number of calices and papillae,

## Imaging Findings

### General

- unilateral small kidney
- decreased number of papillae + calices (5 or less)
- hypertrophied contralateral kidney
- absent renal artery
- hypoplastic disorganized renal veins
