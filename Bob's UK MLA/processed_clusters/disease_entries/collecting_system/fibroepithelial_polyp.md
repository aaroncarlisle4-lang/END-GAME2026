# Disease: FIBROEPITHELIAL POLYP

**Source section:** COLLECTING SYSTEM

## Definition

FIBROUS POLYP = FIBROEPITHELIOMA = VASCULAR FIBROUS POLYP = POLYPOID FIBROMA

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** mesodermal tumor with fibrovascular stroma covered by normal transitional cell
- **Age:** in child / young adult 20-40 years

## Imaging Findings

### General

- mobile elongated cylindrical filling defect on a thin stalk
- multilocular wormlike appearance with smooth margins
- (occasionally) frondlike appearance

### Ureter

- polyp may extend through bladder neck into bladder
- extension into midpoint of bulbar urethra during voiding phase of cystourethrography

## Clinical

- **Cx:** bladder outlet obstruction
