# Disease: ACQUIRED CYSTIC KIDNEY DISEASE

**Source section:** COLLECTING SYSTEM

## Definition

ACQUIRED CYSTIC DISEASE OF UREMIA development of numerous fluid-filled renal cysts in end-stage kidney disease without

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** in 8-13% before dialysis
- **Histo:** cysts lined by flattened cuboidal epithelium with papillary proliferations within renal

## Imaging Findings

### General

- small hypoplastic end-stage kidneys (< 280 g)
- multiple ½-3-cm cysts bilaterally (early = small, late = large): must have at least 3 cysts in
- occasionally progressive renal enlargement due to cysts

### US

- atrophic echogenic kidneys
- cysts of varying size and complexity

## Clinical

- **Cx:** spontaneous hemorrhage into cyst (macrohematuria / retroperitoneal hemorrhage from cyst
