# Disease: ACUTE CORTICAL NECROSIS

**Source section:** COLLECTING SYSTEM

## Definition

rare form of acute renal failure

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** patchy / universal necrosis of renal cortex + proximal convoluted structures ←
- **Distribution:** diffuse / multifocal; mostly bilateral

## Imaging Findings

### General

- diffusely enlarged smooth kidneys
- absent / faint nephrogram

### CT

- enhancing interlobar and arcuate arteries adjacent to nonenhancing cortex (arterial
- "reversed rim" sign = enhancement of medulla + nonenhancement of hypoattenuating
- rim of subcapsular cortical enhancement ← collateral blood flow from cortical vessels
- enhancement of juxtamedullary zone of cortex

### US

- loss of normal corticomedullary region with hypoechoic outer rim of cortex
- hyperechoic cortex with acoustic shadowing

### NUC

- severely impaired renal perfusion
- small kidney (after a few months)
- "tramline" / punctate calcifications along margins of viable and necrotic tissue (as early

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** poor chance of recovery
