# Disease: ADENOMATOID TUMOR OF SCROTUM

**Source section:** COLLECTING SYSTEM

## Definition

benign slow-growing mesothelial neoplasm

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 30% of all extratesticular masses;
- **Age:** 2nd-4th decade
- **Histo:** epithelial-like cells + stroma of hyalinized / loose collagen with varying amounts of
- **Location:** epididymis > tunica vaginalis, spermatic cord (rare)
- **Size:** 0.4-5.0 cm (usually < 2 cm)

## Imaging Findings

### General

- smooth round paratesticular mass at periphery of testis:
- well-marginated solid hypoechoic mass with echogenicity equal to / greater than testis
- indentation of the testicular contour

### MR

- isointense relative to testis on T1WI
- slightly hypointense relative to testis on T2WI
- enhancement similar to testicular enhancement
