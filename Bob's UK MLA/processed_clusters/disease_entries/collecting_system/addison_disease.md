# Disease: ADDISON DISEASE

**Source section:** COLLECTING SYSTEM

## Definition

PRIMARY ADRENAL INSUFFICIENCY ADDISONIAN CRISIS = ADRENAL APOPLEXY

## Imaging Findings

### General

- bilateral adrenal enlargement with areas of increased attenuation
- diminutive glands ← idiopathic atrophy + chronic inflammation
- calcifications (in 25% of chronic course)

## Clinical

- **Cx:** catastrophic hypotension + shock
