# Disease: SEPTUM PELLUCIDUM

**Source section:** ANATOMY OF THE NERVOUS SYSTEM

## Definition

1.5-3.0 mm thin vertical membrane of triangular shape (when viewed from side) persistence / failure of fusion of potential space (= slitlike cavity = 5th ventricle) between "6th ventricle" fluid-filled cavity located posterior to a vertical plane formed by columns of fornix

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** in 80% of term infants; in 15% of adults
- **Location:** posterior to genu of corpus callosum, inferior to body of corpus callosum,

## Imaging Findings

### General

- extends to foramen of Monro
- may dilate + cause obstructive hydrocephalus (rare)
