# Disease: BASAL NUCLEI

**Source section:** ANATOMY OF THE NERVOUS SYSTEM

## Definition

BASAL GANGLIA (earlier incorrect designation)

## Key Facts

- **Location:** between lateral ventricle + insular cortex

## Imaging Findings

### General

- isointense to cortical gray matter on all pulse sequences
- no enhancement
- may exhibit dilated Virchow-Robin perivascular spaces
- may contain bilateral symmetric age-related calcifications
- slightly hypointense relative to putamen ← progressive iron deposition with age
- isointense to cortical gray matter on all pulse sequences
- no enhancement
