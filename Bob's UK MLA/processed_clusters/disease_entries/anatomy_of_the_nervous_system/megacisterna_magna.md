# Disease: MEGACISTERNA MAGNA

**Source section:** ANATOMY OF THE NERVOUS SYSTEM

## Definition

GIANT CISTERNA MAGNA = ENLARGED CISTERNA MAGNA large retrocerebellar CSF space with normal vermis + cerebellar hemispheres

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 1% of all brains

## Imaging Findings

### General

- intact vermis + normal 4th ventricle
- paired linear echoes that join + descend toward base of posterior fossa = dural folds =
- NO enlarged posterior fossa, NO mass effect on cerebellum
