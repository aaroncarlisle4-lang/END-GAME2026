# Disease: SINUSOIDAL OBSTRUCTION SYNDROME

**Source section:** AND SPLENIC DISORDERS

## Definition

SOS = venoocclusive disease blockage of small hepatic venules

## Imaging Findings

### General

- hepatomegaly and hepatic congestion

### CT

- heterogeneous hepatic attenuation

### CECT

- heterogeneous enhancement ← altered perfusion in areas of increased sinusoidal pressure
- small obstructed hepatic venules NOT visible at imaging

### MR

- heterogeneous liver with areas of increased signal intensity on T2WI ← edema
