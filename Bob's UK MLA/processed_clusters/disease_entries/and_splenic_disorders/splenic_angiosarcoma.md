# Disease: SPLENIC ANGIOSARCOMA

**Source section:** AND SPLENIC DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** rare, < 100 cases in literature
- **Age:** 50-60 years

## Imaging Findings

### General

- multiple nodules of varying size → usually enlarging spleen
- solitary complex mass with variable contrast enhancement
- metastasizes to liver (in 70%)
- spontaneous rupture (in 33%)

### MR

- focal / diffuse hypointense foci on T1WI + T2WI ← iron deposition from hemorrhage

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** 20% survival rate after 6 months
