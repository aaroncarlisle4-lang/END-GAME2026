# Disease: PORTAL VEIN

**Source section:** AND SPLENIC DISORDERS

## Definition

waveform pulsatility with > ²/³ change from peak velocity to minimum velocity waveform below the baseline accumulation of lymph around the portal tract ill-defined hypodensity at porta hepatis / hypodensity parallel to portal vein

## Key Facts

- **Location:** predominantly in left lobe = nondependent position when patient supine
- **Associated with:** portal venous hypertension, congenital abnormalities, umbilical vein

## Imaging Findings

### General

- dilated hepatic veins
- decreased S wave in hepatic venous Doppler
- tall a and v waves in hepatic venous Doppler
- compressed hepatic veins
- cavernous transformation = development of collateral vessels in / around occluded
- "thread & streak" sign = color signal with arterial (pulsatile) waveform within
- focal segment of vessel narrowing ± poststenotic dilatation
- inhomogeneous peripheral subcapsular increase in arterial inflow during hepatic arterial
- secondary portal hypertension → increased number and size of collaterals

### US

- focal color aliasing
- accelerated flow across stenosis = 4-fold velocity gradient
- spectral broadening of waveform (= poststenotic turbulence)
- branching linear gas densities:
- in periphery of liver extending to within 2 cm of liver capsule
- predominantly within more anteriorly located left lobe of liver
- pneumatosis of intestinal wall
- intensely hyperechoic foci (= reverberation artifacts)within lumen of portal vein +
- sharp high-amplitude bidirectional spikes (overloading of Doppler receiver from strong

### CT

- tubular branching areas of decreased attenuation (similar to air) in periphery 2 cm deep
- gas in superior / inferior mesenteric veins
- gas in small mesenteric veins at mesenteric border of bowel

### MR

- susceptibility artifacts in portal vein

## Clinical

- **Cx:** portal venous hypertension with massive intractable ascites + recurrent variceal
- **Rx:** balloon angioplasty, stent placement
- **Prognosis:** 75-90% mortality rate within 1 week of Dx
