# Disease: BILE DUCTS

**Source section:** AND SPLENIC DISORDERS

## Definition

intrahepatic biliary system runs parallel to portal venous supply of liver SUBVESICAL / ACCESSORY BILE DUCTS small ducts from liver bed draining directly into GB TRIANGLE OF CALOT anatomic space bordered by CHD medially, cystic duct inferiorly, and cystic artery

## Key Facts

- **Genetics:** PKHD1 (polycystic kidney hepatic disease 1) gene codes for
- **Pathology:** mutation of PKHD1 gene → abnormal cilia → abnormal ductal plate formation
- **Prevalence:** 2.4% of autopsies; 13-18.5% of operative cholangiograms
- **Incidence:** 2%

## Imaging Findings

### Cystic duct

- distal cystic duct posterior to CBD (in 95%), anterior to CBD (in 5%)
- almost horizontal course
- more vertical course
- pneumobilia
- cystic duct parallels extrahepatic bile duct (implies common fibrous sheath)
- gallbladder drains directly into common bile duct

## Clinical

- **N.B.:** important for donor of right hepatic lobe transplant
- **Cx:** during cholecystectomy
