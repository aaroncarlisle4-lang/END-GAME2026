# Disease: SPLENIC HAMARTOMA

**Source section:** AND SPLENIC DISORDERS

## Definition

SPLENOMA rare typically single nonneoplastic lesion composed of a mixture of normal splenic elements

## Key Facts

- **Etiology:** congenital

## Imaging Findings

### CT

- attenuation equal to / hypodense to splenic tissue
- prolonged heterogeneous enhancement

### MR

- heterogeneously hyperintense on T2WI
- diffuse heterogeneous enhancement, more homogeneous on delayed images
