# Disease: GLYCOGEN STORAGE DISEASE

**Source section:** AND SPLENIC DISORDERS

## Definition

autosomal recessive diseases with varying severity and clinical syndromes (13 different abnormal metabolism with enlargement of myocardial cells ← glycogen deposition; similar

## Key Facts

- **Etiology:** defect in glucose-6-phosphatase with excess deposition of glycogen in liver,

## Imaging Findings

### US

- increased liver echogenicity (glycogen / fat)
- nephromegaly

### CT

- increased (glycogen) / normal / decreased (fat) parenchymal attenuation

### Liver

- hepatomegaly

### Kidney

- nephromegaly
- increased cortical + medullary echogenicity ← nephrocalcinosis, nephrolithiasis
- amyloidosis
- high renal blood flow
- massive cardiomegaly with CHF
- hepatomegaly

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** death in infancy, may survive into adulthood with early therapy
- **Cx:** hepatic adenoma, cirrhosis
