# Disease: PAPILLARY ADENOMA OF BILE DUCTS

**Source section:** AND SPLENIC DISORDERS

## Definition

very rare benign tumor of biliary tract

## Key Facts

- **Path:** usually solitary tumor / biliary papillomatosis with papillary fronds extending into
- **Histo:** columnar epithelium supported by connective tissue from lamina propria ± mucin
- **Location:** common bile duct > right / left hepatic duct

## Imaging Findings

### General

- usually small intraductal mass
- visualized at cross-sectional imaging only if large enough
- segmental biliary ductal dilatation

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** high rate of recurrence after surgical resection
- **Cx:** malignant transformation (rare in solitary adenoma, significant risk in papillomatosis))
