## Imaging Findings

### US

- well-circumscribed homogeneous hypo- / hyperechoic nodule, if visible at all

### CT

- hypo- (typically) or isoattenuating nodules
- typically no enhancement / diffuse mild enhancement
- heterogeneous hepatic parenchyma

### MR

- hypo- to iso- to hyperintense on T1WI
- iso- to minimally hypointense on T2WI (= difficult to visualize!)
- ± ↓ SI on fat-suppressed T1WI ← intracellular fat
- decreased enhancement relative to pancreas
- multiple small similar-sized enhancing nodules
- fading to isointensity
- progressive enhancement
- hyper- / isointense on 1-hour delayed images after gadobenate dimeglumine = Gd
