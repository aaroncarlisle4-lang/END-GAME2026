# Disease: GALLBLADDER ADENOMA

**Source section:** AND SPLENIC DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Incidence:** 0.15% of cholecystectomy specimens, 4-7% of all gallbladder polyps

## Imaging Findings

### General

- sessile / pedunculated mass:
- solitary (in ²/³); 2-5 in number (in ⅓)
- up to 20 mm in size
- internal vascularity at color Doppler interrogation
- typically enhancing (DDx adenocarcinoma)

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** dysplasia-to-carcinoma sequence
