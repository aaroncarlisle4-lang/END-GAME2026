## Definition

## Key Facts

- **Path:** conglomeration of communicating cysts covered by a rim of normal pancreatic
- **Histo:** mucinous transformation of pancreatic ductal epithelium forming innumerable papillary
- **Mean age:** 65 years; M > F
- **Location:** frequently multifocal; 5-10% involve entire pancreas
- **Age:** 57 (range, 34-75) years; M÷F = 1÷1

## Clinical

- **N.B.:** escape of contrast material due to excess of mucin / patent papillary orifice hinders
- **Prognosis:** low-grade malignancy with better prognosis than pancreatic adenocarcinoma
