# Disease: MUCINOUS CYSTADENOCARCINOMA OF PANCREAS

**Source section:** AND SPLENIC DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Etiology:** adenoma-carcinoma sequence
- **Histo:** invasive elements surrounded by ovarian-type stroma
- **Age:** older than patients with mucinous cystadenoma

## Imaging Findings

### MR

- > 4-cm large complex cystic pancreatic lesion
- intracystic enhancing soft tissue
- soft-tissue nodularity

## Clinical

- **Rx:** any enhancing soft tissue within a cystic pancreatic neoplasm is an indication for resection.
