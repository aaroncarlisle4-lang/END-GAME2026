## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 9-30-50% of all cholecystectomy specimens;
- **Associated with:** gallstones in 90%
- **Path:** hyperplasia of mucosa + muscularis propria with mucosal outpouching of epithelium-
- **Age:** > 35 years (develop with increasing age); M÷F = 1÷3
- **Prevalence:** 4%; most common (50%) fixed filling defect of gallbladder
- **Location:** commonly in middle ⅓ of gallbladder
