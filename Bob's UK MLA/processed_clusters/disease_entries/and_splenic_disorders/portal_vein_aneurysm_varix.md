# Disease: PORTAL VEIN ANEURYSM / VARIX

**Source section:** AND SPLENIC DISORDERS

## Definition

rare localized saccular / fusiform portal venous dilatation with a portal vein diameter of > 20

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 3% of all venous aneurysms; most common type of visceral venous aneurysm
- **Prevalence:** 0.6-4.3÷1000 patients

## Imaging Findings

### General

- often solitary (80%) and fusiform (97%-100%)

## Clinical

- **Cx:** portal hypertension, spontaneous thrombosis (28%), venous varix rupture,
