# Disease: PANCREATIC DUCTAL ADENOCARCINOMA

**Source section:** AND SPLENIC DISORDERS

## Definition

DUCT CELL ADENOCARCINOMA = PANCREATIC ADENOCARCINOMA

## Key Facts

- **Incidence:** 12÷100,000 / year (in USA 43,920 new diagnoses in 2012 and 36,800 deaths = 4th
- **Path:** scirrhous infiltrative adenocarcinoma with a dense cellularity + sparse vascularity
- **Peak age:** 7th (range, 4th-8th) decade; M÷F = 2÷1; extremely rare in children
- **Stage:** I  = confined to pancreas
