# Disease: BILIARY CYSTADENOCARCINOMA

**Source section:** AND SPLENIC DISORDERS

## Definition

BILE DUCT CYSTADENOCARCINOMA rare malignant multilocular cystic tumor originating from biliary cystadenoma

## Imaging Findings

### General

- nodularity with septations are suggestive of malignancy
- coarse calcifications
