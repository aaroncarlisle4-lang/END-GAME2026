# Disease: CONGENITAL HEPATIC FIBROSIS

**Source section:** AND SPLENIC DISORDERS

## Definition

congenital cirrhosis with rapid + fatal progression

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** fibrous tissue within hepatic parenchyma with excess numbers of distorted terminal
- **Age:** early childhood - 6th decade;

## Imaging Findings

### General

- "lollipop-tree" = ectasia of peripheral biliary radicles
- atrophy of right lobe + normal / enlarged medial segment of left lobe + hypertrophy of left
- splenomegaly
- portosystemic varices
- enlarged hepatic artery associated with large multiacinar regenerative nodules

## Clinical

- **Cx:** cirrhosis, portal hypertension, hepatocellular carcinoma, cholangiocellular carcinoma
