## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 1-4%; autopsy incidence 0.4-7.3%; increased with multiparity
- **Age:** rarely seen in young children; M÷F = 1÷2-5
- **Path:** large vascular channels filled with slowly circulating blood; lined by single layer of
- **Size:** < 4 cm (90%)
- **Associated with:** coexistent smaller < 5 cm hemangioma in 13%
- **Histo:** hemorrhage, thrombosis, extensive hyalinization, liquefaction, fibrosis; central cleft
