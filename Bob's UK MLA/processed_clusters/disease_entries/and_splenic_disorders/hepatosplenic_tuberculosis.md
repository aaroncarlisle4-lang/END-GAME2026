# Disease: HEPATOSPLENIC TUBERCULOSIS

**Source section:** AND SPLENIC DISORDERS

## Imaging Findings

### micronodular-miliary involvement

- innumerable 0.5-2.0-mm nodules
- hyperechoic liver

### macronodular involvement (uncommon)

- hypoattenuating nodules + irregular ill-defined margins
- minimal central + definite peripheral enhancement
- T1-hypointense + T2-hyperintense nodules
- tuberculomas eventually calcify
