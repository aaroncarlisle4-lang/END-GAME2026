# Disease: PANCREATIC TRANSPLANTATION

**Source section:** AND SPLENIC DISORDERS

## Definition

major long-term cause of graft failure > first 6 months

## Key Facts

- **Path:** autoimmune vasculitis → small vessel occlusion (decreased perfusion) →
- **Frequency:** up to 35%

## Imaging Findings

### General

- venous > arterial thrombosis
- all imaging modalities are unreliable
- graft enlargement + heterogeneity
- poor margination of transplant
- dilated pancreatic duct
- resistive indices not helpful
- shrunken / disappearing transplant
- nonspecific enlargement + organ heterogeneity
- peritransplant fluid
- mural thickening of adjacent bowel

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** 40% survival rate > 1 year
- **Rx:** prompt pancreatectomy
- **Cx:** multiple episodes culminate in chronic rejection
