# Disease: HEPATIC VENO-OCCLUSIVE DISEASE

**Source section:** AND SPLENIC DISORDERS

## Definition

occlusion of small centrilobular veins without involvement of major hepatic veins

## Key Facts

- **Etiology:** radiation and chemotherapy in bone-marrow transplant patients; bush tea (alkaloid)

## Imaging Findings

### General

- main hepatic veins + IVC normal
- bidirectional / reversed portal venous flow
- gallbladder wall thickening
