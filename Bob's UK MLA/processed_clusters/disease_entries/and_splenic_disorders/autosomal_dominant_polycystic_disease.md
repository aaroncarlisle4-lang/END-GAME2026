# Disease: AUTOSOMAL DOMINANT POLYCYSTIC DISEASE

**Source section:** AND SPLENIC DISORDERS

## Definition

POLYCYSTIC LIVER DISEASE part of spectrum of fibropolycystic liver disease

## Key Facts

- **Etiology:** biliary ductal plate malformation at the level of the small intrahepatic bile ducts with
- **Associated with:** polycystic kidney disease (in 50%)

## Imaging Findings

### General

- enlarged diffusely cystic liver: cysts of 1 mm - 12 cm in diameter
- calcifications of cyst wall ← hemorrhage / infection
- ± diffuse dilatation of intra- and extrahepatic bile ducts

## Clinical

- **Cx:** infection, compression, bleeding, rupture of cysts
