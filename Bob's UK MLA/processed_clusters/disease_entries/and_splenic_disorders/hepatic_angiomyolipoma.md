# Disease: HEPATIC ANGIOMYOLIPOMA

**Source section:** AND SPLENIC DISORDERS

## Definition

rare benign mesenchymal tumor

## Key Facts

- **Associated with:** tuberous sclerosis
- **Histo:** smooth muscle cells, fat, proliferating blood vessels

## Imaging Findings

### General

- intratumoral fat is DIAGNOSTIC
- soft-tissue component may enhance

### US

- circumscribed hyperechoic mass (DDx: hemangioma)
- sound attenuation + subtle acoustic shadowing
- relative hypervascularity

### CT

- areas of macroscopic fat
- marked enhancement with visualization of large central vessels during arterial phase

### MR

- hyperintense areas of macroscopic fat on T1WI
- marked decrease in SI with T1 fat suppression technique
- intense enhancement during arterial phase with dark areas of macroscopic fat on fat-

## Clinical

- **Cx:** intratumoral hemorrhage
