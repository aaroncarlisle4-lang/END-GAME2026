# Disease: GLOBOID CELL LEUKODYSTROPHY

**Source section:** CENTRAL NEUROCYTOMA

## Definition

KRABBE DISEASE autosomal recessive neurodegenerative disorder characterized by severe myelin loss +

## Key Facts

- **Age:** 3-6 months

## Imaging Findings

### General

- symmetric hyperintensity / hyperdensity in periventricular white matter, thalami, basal
- decreased attenuation of white matter
- brain atrophy with enlargement of ventricles
- elevated choline + creatine + myo-inositol associated with moderate N-acetylaspartate

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** death within first few years of life
