# Disease: CHOROID PLEXUS NEOPLASM

**Source section:** CENTRAL NEUROCYTOMA

## Key Facts

- **Incidence:** 0.5-0.6% of all primary intracranial tumors; 2-5% of pediatric brain tumors; 5% of
- **Age:** up to 20% < 1 year of age; in 75% < 2 years of age; in 86% < 5 years of age; M >> F
- **Path:** large aggregation of choroidal fronds producing great quantities of CSF; occasionally
- **Location:** anywhere within choroid plexus epithelium
