# Disease: DEMENTIA WITH LEWY BODIES

**Source section:** CENTRAL NEUROCYTOMA

## Definition

2nd most common neurodegenerative disorder in patients > 65 years

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** loss of dopaminergic neurons in substantia nigra

## Imaging Findings

### PET

- hypometabolism in both parietal + posterior temporal lobes and posterior cingulate gyrus
- ± involvement of occipital lobes (spared in Alzheimer's)
