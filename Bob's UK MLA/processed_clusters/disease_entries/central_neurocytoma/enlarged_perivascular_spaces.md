# Disease: ENLARGED PERIVASCULAR SPACES

**Source section:** CENTRAL NEUROCYTOMA

## Definition

ENLARGED / ATYPICAL VIRCHOW-ROBIN SPACES small invaginations of subarachnoid space following pia mater along perforating nutrient end giant sievelike Virchow-Robin spaces clusters of variable sized cysts with bizarre cystic configuration and mass effect

## Key Facts

- **Location:** inferior basal ganglia (lenticulostriate arteries); inferior third of putamen = état

## Imaging Findings

### General

- no restricted diffusion
- no enhancement
- normal signal intensity of surrounding parenchyma
- occasionally central high SI on T1WI ← inflow effect
- small 1-2-mm round lesions isointense to CSF
- moderate 2-5 mm round lesions isointense to CSF
- clusters of variably sized bizarre cystic spaces bordering ventricles / subarachnoid space
- ± hydrocephalus
- ± surrounding reactive gliosis

## Clinical

- **Cx:** hydrocephalus
