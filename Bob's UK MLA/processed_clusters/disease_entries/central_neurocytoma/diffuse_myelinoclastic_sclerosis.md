# Disease: DIFFUSE (MYELINOCLASTIC) SCLEROSIS

**Source section:** CENTRAL NEUROCYTOMA

## Definition

MYELINOCLASTIC DIFFUSE SCLEROSIS = SCHILDER DISEASE rare demyelinating disorder with episodic recurrence + remission

## Key Facts

- **Age:** children (5-14 years) > adults; M÷F = 1÷1
- **Histo:** selective confluent demyelination with relative axonal sparing, perivascular
- **Location:** centrum semiovale in both hemispheres

## Imaging Findings

### General

- large well defined bilateral low-attenuation white matter lesions with mass effect
- enhancement with IV contrast material

## Clinical

- **Rx:** usually responsive to corticosteroids
