# Disease: HALLERVORDEN-SPATZ DISEASE

**Source section:** CENTRAL NEUROCYTOMA

## Definition

PANTOTHENATE KINASE-ASSOCIATED NEURODEGENERATION rare familial neurodegenerative metabolic disorder with abnormal iron retention in basal

## Key Facts

- **Age:** 2nd decade of life
- **Histo:** hyperpigmentation and symmetrical destruction of globus pallidus + substantia nigra

## Imaging Findings

### CT

- low- (= tissue destruction) / high-density (= dystrophic calcification) foci in globus

### MR

- "eye-of-the-tiger" sign = high-signal-intensity center surrounded by the more typical
- initially bilateral hypointense globus pallidus on T2WI (= iron accumulation)
- later central hyperintense foci on T2WI (= tissue destruction + gliosis)
