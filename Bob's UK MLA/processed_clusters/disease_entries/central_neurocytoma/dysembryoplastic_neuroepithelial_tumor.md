# Disease: DYSEMBRYOPLASTIC NEUROEPITHELIAL TUMOR

**Source section:** CENTRAL NEUROCYTOMA

## Definition

benign tumor of neuroepithelial origin arising from cortical / deep gray matter

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** specific glioneuronal element in a columnar pattern oriented perpendicular to cortical
- **Age:** usually < 20 years; M > F

## Imaging Findings

### CT

- hypoattenuating mass ± calcifications
- remodeling of inner table of skull

### MR

- cortical mass without surrounding vasogenic edema:
- hypointense on T1WI + hyperintense on T2WI
- "soap bubble" / megagyrus appearance at cortical margin = enlargement of cortical
- contrast enhancement (in 33%)

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** partial resection stops seizure activity; rarely recurs
