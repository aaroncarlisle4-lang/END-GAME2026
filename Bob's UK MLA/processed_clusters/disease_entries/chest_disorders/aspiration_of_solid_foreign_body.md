# Disease: ASPIRATION OF SOLID FOREIGN BODY

**Source section:** CHEST DISORDERS

## Definition

75% of foreign bodies aspirated into airways below larynx chronic inflammatory reaction to repeated aspiration of foreign particles into bronchioles

## Key Facts

- **Age:** peak prevalence 6 month-3 years; in 50% < 3 years; in 70% < 15 years
- **Location:** R main-stem bronchus (almost exclusively in lower lobes), larynx (3%),
- **Histo:** resembling diffuse panbronchiolitis
