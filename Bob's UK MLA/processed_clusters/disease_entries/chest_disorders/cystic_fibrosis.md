# Disease: CYSTIC FIBROSIS

**Source section:** CHEST DISORDERS

## Definition

CF = MUCOVISCIDOSIS = FIBROCYSTIC DISEASE common autosomal recessive multisystem disease of exocrine gland dysfunction characterized multiple regenerative nodules with diffuse involvement

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 1÷2,500 livebirths; almost exclusively in Caucasians affecting 70,000 persons
- **Genetics:** mutation of cystic fibrosis transmembrane regulator gene (CFTR) on long arm of
- **Location:** predilection for apical + posterior segments of upper lobes
- **Histo:** mucus-containing cysts in gallbladder wall
