# Disease: DROWNED LUNG

**Source section:** CHEST DISORDERS

## Definition

abnormal increase in lobar / rarely whole lung volume

## Imaging Findings

### General

- bulging interlobar fissure
- little / no mediastinal shift to contralateral side
- obliteration of bronchial lumen leading to obstructed lobe
- absence of air bronchogram
- hilar mass + regional lymphadenopathy

### CT

- postobstructive consolidation
- mucus bronchogram = bronchi filled with low-attenuation material

### CECT

- delineates enhancing tumor + lymph nodes from consolidated lung of low attenuation (=

### MR

- T2WI differentiates drowned lung from obstructing tumor

### PET

- high tumor activity centrally

## Clinical

- **Cx:** necrosis, cavitation, secondary infection
