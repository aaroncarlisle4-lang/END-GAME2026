## Definition

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 1.5-6% of all primary lung cancers (increasing incidence to ? 20-25%)
- **Etiology:** development from type II alveolar epithelial cells
- **Age:** 40-70 years; M÷F = 1÷1 (strikingly high in women)
- **Path:** peripheral neoplasm arising beyond a recognizable bronchus with tendency to spread
- **Histo:** subtype of well-differentiated adenocarcinoma; cuboidal / columnar cells grow along
- **Location:** peripherally, beyond a recognizable bronchus
