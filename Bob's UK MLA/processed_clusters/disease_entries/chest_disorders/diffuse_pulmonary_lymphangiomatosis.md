# Disease: DIFFUSE PULMONARY LYMPHANGIOMATOSIS

**Source section:** CHEST DISORDERS

## Definition

rare condition consisting of proliferation + dilatation of communicating lymphatic channels in

## Key Facts

- **Age:** teenager, young adult
- **Path:** resembles lymphangioma
- **Location:** predominantly upper lobes of lung

## Imaging Findings

### CT

- smooth symmetric thickening of interlobular septa + peribronchovascular bundles
- diffuse increased attenuation of mediastinal fat
- mild perihilar infiltration
- diffuse pleural thickening
- pleural effusion
- pericardial effusion
