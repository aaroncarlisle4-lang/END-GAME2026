# Disease: CONGENITAL PULMONARY VENOLOBAR SYNDROME

**Source section:** CHEST DISORDERS

## Definition

SCIMITAR SYNDROME unique form of congenital lung underdevelopment affecting one / more lobes in a constellation uncommon variant of hypogenetic lung syndrome in which RLL crosses midline between
