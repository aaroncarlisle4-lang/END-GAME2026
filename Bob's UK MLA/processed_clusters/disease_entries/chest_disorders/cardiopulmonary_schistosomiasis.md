# Disease: CARDIOPULMONARY SCHISTOSOMIASIS

**Source section:** CHEST DISORDERS

## Definition

form of parasitic embolism

## Key Facts

- **Path:** intra- and perivascular granulomas, intimal hyperplasia, medial hypertrophy, concentric
- **Age:** 25-35 (range, 1-93) years

## Imaging Findings

### CXR

- cardiomegaly
- central pulmonary arterial enlargement
- tiny scattered lung nodules occasionally

### HRCT

- nodules of 2-15 mm, interstitial thickening
- larger nodules surrounded by ground-glass-opacity halo
- dilatation of right atrium + right ventricle + central pulmonary arteries

## Clinical

- **Cx:** cor pulmonale (2-33%)
- **Rx:** praziquantel, oxamniquine
