# Disease: GASTRIC ULCER

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Incidence:** 5÷10,000; 100,000 annually (USA)
- **Age:** adulthood; M÷F = 7÷1
