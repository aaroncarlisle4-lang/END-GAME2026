# Disease: MECONIUM ILEUS

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

small bowel obstruction ← desiccated meconium pellets impacted in distal ileum

## Key Facts

- **Age:** may develop in utero (in 15%)

## Imaging Findings

### General

- numerous dilated small bowel loops without air-fluid levels (fluid not present)
- "bubbly" / "frothy" appearance of intestinal contents
- "soap-bubble" / "applesauce" appearance in RLQ (in 50-66%) ← admixture of gas with
- multiple round / oval filling defects in distal ileum + colon
- functional microcolon ← unused colon in antenatal obstruction
- unusual echogenic intraluminal areas in small bowel (DDx: normal transient inspissated
- usually polyhydramnios
- fluid-filled dilated small bowel
