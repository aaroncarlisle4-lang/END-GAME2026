# Disease: TAILGUT CYST

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

RETRORECTAL CYSTIC HAMARTOMA

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** several types of epithelia + elements of intestinal epithelium, smooth muscle within cyst

## Imaging Findings

### General

- well-defined thin-walled multicystic / unilocular cyst within perirectal space adhering to
- clear fluid / mucoid fluid with internal echoes
- mild enhancement of uncomplicated cyst
- thick enhancing wall ± surrounding inflammation / air-fluid level ← secondary infection

## Clinical

- **Rx:** complete surgical excision
