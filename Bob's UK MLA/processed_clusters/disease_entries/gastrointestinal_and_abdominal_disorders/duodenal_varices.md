# Disease: DUODENAL VARICES

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

dilated collateral veins ← portal hypertension (posterior superior pancreaticoduodenal vein)

## Imaging Findings

### General

- lobulated filling defects (best demonstrated in prone position, maximal luminal distension
- commonly associated with fundal + esophageal varices
