# Disease: CROHN DISEASE

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

REGIONAL ENTERITIS chronic relapsing inflammatory disorder with prolonged unpredictable course characterized GRANULOMATOUS COLITIS = CROHN COLITIS PENETRATING CROHN DISEASE

## Key Facts

- **Associated with:** erythema nodosum, pyoderma gangrenosum
- **Location:** duodenal bulb, 2nd portion of duodenum, terminal ileum
- **Frequency:** 35% at some time during course of disease with a 20-40% lifetime risk
