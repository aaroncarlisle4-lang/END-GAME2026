# Disease: LYMPHOGRANULOMA VENEREUM

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

LGV = sexually transmitted disease caused by virus Chlamydia trachomatis producing a

## Key Facts

- **Location:** rectum ± extension to sigmoid + descending colon

## Imaging Findings

### General

- narrowing + shortening + straightening of rectosigmoid
- widening of retrorectal space
- irregularity of mucosa + ulcerations
- paracolic abscess
- fistula to pericolic area, rectum, vagina (common)

## Clinical

- **Rx:** tetracyclines effective in acute phase before scarring has occurred
