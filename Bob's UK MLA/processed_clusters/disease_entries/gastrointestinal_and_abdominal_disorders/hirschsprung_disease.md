**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Etiology:** arrest of craniocaudal migration of neural crest cells along vagal trunks during 5th-
- **Prevalence:** 1÷5,000-8,000 live births; 15-20% of all neonatal bowel obstructions; usually
- **Age:** during first 6 weeks of life of a full-term infant (70-80%); extremely rare in premature
- **Associated with:** trisomy 21 (in 2%)
- **Location:** at varying distances proximal to anus, usually rectosigmoid (in 80%) and rectum
