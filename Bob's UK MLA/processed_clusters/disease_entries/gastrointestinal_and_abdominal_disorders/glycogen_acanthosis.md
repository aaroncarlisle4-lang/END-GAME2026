# Disease: GLYCOGEN ACANTHOSIS

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

benign degenerative condition with accumulation of cellular glycogen within squamous

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** in up to 15% of endoscoped patients
- **Etiology:** unknown
- **Age:** middle-aged / elderly individuals
- **Histo:** hyperplasia + hypertrophy of squamous mucosal cells ← increased glycogen; no
- **Location:** middle (common) / upper esophagus, in random distribution

## Imaging Findings

### General

- multiple 1-3-mm rounded nodules / plaques
