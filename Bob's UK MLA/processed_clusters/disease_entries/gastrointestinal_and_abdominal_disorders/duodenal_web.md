# Disease: DUODENAL WEB

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

failure of recanalization process in primitive foregut between 9th-11th week GA

## Imaging Findings

### General

- fluid- / contrast-filled blind-ending saccular structure
- thin enhancing diverticular wall

## Clinical

- **Cx:** pancreatitis ← reflux of duodenal contents through papilla of Vater
