# Disease: DIAPHRAGM DISEASE

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

small bowel webs ← NSAIDs

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** in 10% of patients receiving long-term NSAIDs
- **Path:** foci of submucosal fibrosis with interruption of adjacent muscularis mucosae
- **Location:** ileum > jejunum

## Imaging Findings

### General

- multiple concentric diaphragm-like strictures
