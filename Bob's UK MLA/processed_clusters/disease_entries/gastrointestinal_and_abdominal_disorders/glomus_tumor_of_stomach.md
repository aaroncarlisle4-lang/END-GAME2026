# Disease: GLOMUS TUMOR OF STOMACH

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

rare benign vascular tumor that originates from glomus body

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 2% of all benign gastric tumors
- **Path:** dilated irregularly shaped thin-walled vessels (= modified capillaries) covered by nests
- **Histo:** sheets of monotonous round cells interspersed with numerous delicate vessels
- **Location:** gastric antrum
- **Size:** 1-4 cm in diameter

## Imaging Findings

### General

- solitary smooth intramural / intraluminal subepithelial mass ± mucosal ulceration
- change in shape + size during fluoroscopy (similar to lipoma)
- tiny flecks of calcifications (occasionally)
