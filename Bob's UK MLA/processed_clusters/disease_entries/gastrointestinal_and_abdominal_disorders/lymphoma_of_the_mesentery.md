# Disease: LYMPHOMA OF THE MESENTERY

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Associated with:** retroperitoneal lymphadenopathy

## Imaging Findings

### General

- "sandwich" sign = mesenteric lymphadenopathy surrounding mesenteric vessels:
- round / oval soft-tissue opacities
- irregular masses / lobulated cake-like heterogeneous mass with areas of low attenuation ←
- mildly enhancing
- "misty mesentery" = mesenteric fat stranding (especially after chemotherapy)
- direct extension into small bowel
- displacement of small
