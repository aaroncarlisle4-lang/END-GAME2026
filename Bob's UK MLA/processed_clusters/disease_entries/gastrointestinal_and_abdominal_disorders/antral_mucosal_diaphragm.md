# Disease: ANTRAL-MUCOSAL DIAPHRAGM

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

ANTRAL WEB

## Key Facts

- **Associated with:** gastric ulcer (30-50%)
- **Location:** usually 1.5 cm from pylorus (range 0-7 cm)

## Imaging Findings

### General

- constant symmetric band of 2-3 mm thickness traversing the antrum perpendicular to long axis
- "double bulb" appearance (in profile)
- concentric / eccentric orifice
- normal peristaltic activity
