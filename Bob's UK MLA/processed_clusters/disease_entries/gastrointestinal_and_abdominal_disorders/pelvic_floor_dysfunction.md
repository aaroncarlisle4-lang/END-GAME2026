## Imaging Findings

### General

- linear distance measured during straining
- hiatal enlargement > 6 cm
- pelvic floor descent > 2 cm
- 90° at rest and during voluntary contraction (squeeze maneuver)
- more obtuse during defecation straining (void)
- uncovers urge incontinence from detrusor instability
- uncovers stress urinary incontinence masked by urethral hypermobility
