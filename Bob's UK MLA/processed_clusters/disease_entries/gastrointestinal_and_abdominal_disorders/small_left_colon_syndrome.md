# Disease: SMALL LEFT COLON SYNDROME

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Age:** newborn infant
- **Associated with:** maternal diabetes mellitus (most common), maternal substance abuse; NOT

## Imaging Findings

### General

- colonic caliber becomes abruptly diminutive distal to splenic flexure
- bowel dilatation proximal to splenic flexure
- ± meconium plug (as a result and NOT the cause of obstruction)

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** gradual resolution of functional immaturity over days to weeks
