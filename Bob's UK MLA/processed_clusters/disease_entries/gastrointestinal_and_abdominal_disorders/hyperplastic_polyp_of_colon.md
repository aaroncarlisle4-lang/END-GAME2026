# Disease: HYPERPLASTIC POLYP OF COLON

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

intestinal metaplasia consisting of mucous glands lined by a single layer of columnar

## Key Facts

- **Path:** infolding of epithelium into the glandular lumen
- **Location:** rectosigmoid

## Imaging Findings

### General

- smooth rounded sessile elevation
- usually < 5 mm in diameter
