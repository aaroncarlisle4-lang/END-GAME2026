# Disease: DUODENAL ATRESIA

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

most common cause of congenital duodenal obstruction; 2nd most common site of

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 1÷10,000; M÷F = 1÷1
- **Etiology:** defective vacuolization of duodenum between 6th and 11th weeks of fetal life; rarely

## Imaging Findings

### General

- "double bubble" sign = gas-fluid levels in duodenal bulb + gastric fundus
- total absence of intestinal gas in small / large bowel
- colon of normal caliber
- "double bubble" sign = simultaneous distension of stomach + 1st portion of duodenum;
- increased gastric peristalsis
- polyhydramnios in 3rd trimester (100%)

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** 36% mortality in neonates
- **Cx:** prematurity (40%) ← preterm labor ← polyhydramnios
