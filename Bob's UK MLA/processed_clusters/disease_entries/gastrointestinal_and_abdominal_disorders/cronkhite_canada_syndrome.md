# Disease: CRONKHITE-CANADA SYNDROME

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

nonneoplastic nonhereditary polyps (as in juvenile polyposis) associated with ectodermal

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** > 100 cases described
- **Histo:** hamartomatous polyps resemble juvenile / retention polyps = multiple cystic spaces
- **Age:** 62 (range, 42-75) years; M < F
- **Location:** stomach (100%); small bowel (> 50%); colon (100%)

## Imaging Findings

### General

- multiple polyps
- thickened gastric rugae

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** rapidly fatal in women within 6-18 months ← cachexia; tendency toward remission
