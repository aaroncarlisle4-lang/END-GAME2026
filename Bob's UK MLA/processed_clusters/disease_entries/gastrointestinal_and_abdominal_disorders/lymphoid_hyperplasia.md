# Disease: LYMPHOID HYPERPLASIA

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** normal variant in 13% of BE examinations
- **Histo:** hyperplastic lymph follicles in lamina propria (Peyer patches) ← probably
- **Associated with:** splenomegaly, large tonsils, eczematous dermatitis, achlorhydria, pernicious

## Imaging Findings

### General

- mucosa studded with innumerable 1-3-mm small uniform polypoid lesions
- lesions may be umbilicated (uncommon)
