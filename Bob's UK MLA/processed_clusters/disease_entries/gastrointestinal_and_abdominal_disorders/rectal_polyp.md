# Disease: RECTAL POLYP

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 7-50% (more common in older patients)
- **Path:** hyperplastic÷adenomatous polyps = 1÷4
- **Size:** usually < 5 mm

## Imaging Findings

### General

- tends to flatten out with air distention
- not visible adjacent to rectal balloon
- sessile > pedunculated > flat
- carpetlike nodular appearance (for villous adenoma)
- underdistended / redundant rectal mucosa may simulate villous adenoma
