# Disease: GLIOMATOSIS PERITONEI

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

benign mature glial implants throughout peritoneum

## Key Facts

- **Associated with:** solid / immature ovarian teratomas, ventriculoperitoneal shunts
- **Histo:** benign mature glial tissue with delicate fibrillar processes and scattered supporting

## Imaging Findings

### CT

- peritoneal nodules + masses of soft-tissue density
- omental caking
- ascites
- adnexal / pelvic mass suggestive of teratoma

### MR

- multilobular masses of homogeneously high SI on T2WI
