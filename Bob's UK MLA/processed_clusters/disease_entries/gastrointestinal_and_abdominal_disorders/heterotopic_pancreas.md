# Disease: HETEROTOPIC PANCREAS

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

pancreatic tissue at aberrant sites that lack a vascular / ductal connection to main pancreatic

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 1-15% (autoptic)

## Imaging Findings

### General

- flat ovoid intramural subepithelial nodule of < 3 cm
- intraluminal growth ± central umbilication (= orifice of rudimentary pancreatic duct)

### CT

- mass with irregular border
- usually homogeneously enhancing mass with attenuation similar to pancreas
- occasionally poor enhancement (if pancreatic acini remain a minor component of the mass)
