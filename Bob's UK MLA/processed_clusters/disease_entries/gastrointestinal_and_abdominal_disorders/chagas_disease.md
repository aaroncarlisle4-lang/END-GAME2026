# Disease: CHAGAS DISEASE

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

damage of ganglion cells by neurotoxin liberated from protozoa Trypanosoma cruzi resulting

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** decreased number of cells in medullary dorsal motor nucleus + Wallerian degeneration
- **Peak age:** 30-50 years; M÷F = 1÷1

## Imaging Findings

### Esophagus: changes as in achalasia

- severe cardiomegaly ← dilated cardiomyopathy
- pulmonary edema, septal lines, pleural effusion ← chronic heart failure

## Clinical

- **Cx:** impacted feces, sigmoid volvulus
