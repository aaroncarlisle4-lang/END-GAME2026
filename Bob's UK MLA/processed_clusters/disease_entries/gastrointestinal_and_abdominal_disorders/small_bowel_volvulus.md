# Disease: SMALL BOWEL VOLVULUS

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

rare life-threatening SURGICAL EMERGENCY

## Imaging Findings

### General

- poor / absent enhancement of bowel wall
- "spoke wheel" sign = radial peripheral distribution of distended fluid-filled small-bowel
- two collapsed adjacent bowel loops = site of constriction (35%)
- U-shaped configuration of distended fluid-filled small-bowel loops = radial arrangement of
- "triangular" sign = fusiform tapering of collapsed loop at site of constriction on longitudinal
- "whirl" sign = swirling appearance of twisted mesentery (10%)
