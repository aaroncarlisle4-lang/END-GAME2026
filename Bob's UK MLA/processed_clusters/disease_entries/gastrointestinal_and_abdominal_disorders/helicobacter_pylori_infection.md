# Disease: HELICOBACTER PYLORI INFECTION

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** increasing with age; in > 50% of Americans > 60 years of age
- **Path:** surface epithelial damage + inflammation with mucosal infiltration by neutrophils,
- **Location:** gastric antrum > proximal half of stomach

## Imaging Findings

### General

- gastritis (75% prevalence of H. pylori):
- thickened gastric folds
- polypoid gastritis mimicking malignant tumor
- enlarged areae gastricae
- gastric ulcer (60-80% prevalence of H. pylori)
- duodenal ulcer (90-100% prevalence of H. pylori)
