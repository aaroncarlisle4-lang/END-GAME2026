# Disease: DUODENAL HEMATOMA

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Age:** 80% in children / young adults
- **Associated with:** pancreatic injury in 50-98%, mesenteric and abdominal wall hematoma,
- **Path:** mural hematoma with blood dissecting along submucosal compartment ← separation of
- **Location:** 2nd / 3rd portion of duodenum

## Imaging Findings

### General

- circumferential / eccentric duodenal narrowing ← extrinsic compression
- large filling defect completely occupying + dilating lumen

### CT

- hyperattenuating (= acute trauma) thickened duodenal wall
- eccentric mass protruding into the bowel lumen

## Clinical

- **Cx:** bowel obstruction, perforation
- **Rx:** conservative
