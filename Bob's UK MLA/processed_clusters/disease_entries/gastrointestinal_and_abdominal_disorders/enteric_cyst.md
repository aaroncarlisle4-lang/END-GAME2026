# Disease: ENTERIC CYST

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

cyst lined by gastrointestinal mucosa without bowel wall

## Key Facts

- **Etiology:** migration of small bowel / colonic diverticulum into mesentery / mesocolon
- **Path:** unilocular thin smooth-walled cyst with serous contents lined by enteric epithelium +

## Imaging Findings

### US

- hypoechoic cystic mass, occasionally with septations
