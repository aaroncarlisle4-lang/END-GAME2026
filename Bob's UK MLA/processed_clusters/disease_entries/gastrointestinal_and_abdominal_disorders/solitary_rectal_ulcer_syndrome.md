# Disease: SOLITARY RECTAL ULCER SYNDROME

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

MUCOSAL PROLAPSE SYNDROME rare condition frequently associated with chronic constipation and pelvic floor dyssynergia

## Key Facts

- **Age:** young patients (especially women)
- **Path:** small / large, single / multiple shallow ulcers; 25% broad- based, 18% patchy granular /
- **Histo:** obliteration of lamina propria mucosae by fibromuscular proliferation of muscularis

## Imaging Findings

### General

- central ulceration on anterior rectal wall → regional inflammation → mass effect in
- polypoid lesion / nodules (polypoid type)
- flat granular mucosa (flat type)
- thickened valves of Houston without ulcer
- stricture
- failure of anorectal angle to open while straining
- excessive perineal descent
