# Disease: INFLAMMATORY FIBROID POLYP

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

VANEK TUMOR = EOSINOPHILIC GRANULOMA = SUBMUCOSAL FIBROMA = FIBROUS POLYPOID unusual benign chronic inflammatory lesion

## Key Facts

- **Path:** arises in submucosa
- **Histo:** spindle cells arranged in whorls around blood vessels with prominent inflammatory
- **Size:** 2-5 cm in diameter

## Imaging Findings

### General

- intraluminal subepithelial polypoid mass / pedunculated polyp with smooth / slightly

## Clinical

- **Cx:** intermittent gastric outlet obstruction
- **Rx:** endoscopic resection
