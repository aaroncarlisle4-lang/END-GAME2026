# Disease: BLUNT ABDOMINAL TRAUMA

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

manifestation of tenuous hemodynamic instability even after aggressive resuscitation efforts liver laceration or hematoma extending into major hepatic vein / IVC areas of low attenuation paralleling portal vein + its branches 2° to AP diameter < ¼ of IVC width not caused by external compression

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 29-34% of patients with abdominal visceral injury have NO hemoperitoneum ←
- **Location:** paracolic gutters, pelvis
- **Associated with:** other solid visceral / bowel injuries (29%); lower rib fractures in 44%,
- **Frequency:** < 10% of childhood trauma
