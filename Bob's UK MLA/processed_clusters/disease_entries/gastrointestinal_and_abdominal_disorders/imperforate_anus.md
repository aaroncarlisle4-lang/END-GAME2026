**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Imaging Findings

### General

### US

- ≤ 15 mm distance between anal dimple + distal rectal pouch on transperineal images →
- dilated colon in lower pelvis with U- / S-shaped configuration ± intraluminal calcifications
- normal amniotic fluid (unless also TEF)
- absence of anal characteristics (= hypoechoic circular rim with central echogenic stripe)

## Clinical

- **Rx:** readily reparable
- **Cx:** associated malformations more common + more severe
