# Disease: KILLIAN-JAMIESON DIVERTICULUM

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

rare pulsion diverticulum through muscular gap in anterolateral wall of proximal cervical

## Key Facts

- **Age:** usually > 60 years (same as for Zenker diverticulum)

## Imaging Findings

### General

- predominantly left lateral + anterior outpouching from cervical esophagus

## Clinical

- **Cx:** overflow aspiration
