# Disease: BANNAYAN-RILEY-RUVALCABA SYNDROME

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

RUVALCABA-MYHRE-SMITH SYNDROME

## Imaging Findings

### General

- hamartomatous intestinal polyps (in 45%): usually in distal ileum + colon
- macrocephaly
- subcutaneous and visceral lipomas + hemangiomas
