# Disease: HETEROTOPIC MESENTERIC OSSIFICATION

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

OSSEOUS METAPLASIA of mesentery and peritoneum

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** core of reactive myofibroblasts with hemorrhage + fat necrosis, osteoblastic activity,
- **Location:** mesentery with extension to peritoneal surface

## Imaging Findings

### General

- multiple linear-branching structures of high-attenuation
- trabecular architecture
