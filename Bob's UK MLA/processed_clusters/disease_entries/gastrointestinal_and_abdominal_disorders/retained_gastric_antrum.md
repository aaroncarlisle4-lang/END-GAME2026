# Disease: RETAINED GASTRIC ANTRUM

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Associated with:** gastric ulcers in 30-50%

## Imaging Findings

### General

- duodenogastric reflux of barium through pylorus (DIAGNOSTIC)
- giant marginal ulcer / several marginal ulcers usually on jejunal side of anastomosis (large
- large amount of secretions
- edematous mucosa of jejunal anastomotic segment
- lacy / cobweblike small bowel pattern (hypersecretion)

## Clinical

- **Cx:** gastrojejunocolic fistula
