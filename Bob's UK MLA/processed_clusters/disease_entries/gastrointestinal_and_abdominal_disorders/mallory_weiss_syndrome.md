# Disease: MALLORY-WEISS SYNDROME

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

mucosal ± submucosal partial thickness tear of distal esophagus ± gastric cardia with

## Key Facts

- **Age:** 30-60 years; M > F
- **Location:** at / above / below (76%) esophagogastric junction

## Imaging Findings

### General

- 1-4 cm longitudinal collection of barium (single tear in 77%, multiple tears in 23%)
- extravasation of barium

### ANGIO

- bleeding site at gastric cardia

## Clinical

- **Rx:** supportive
