# Disease: COLONIC ATRESIA

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** less common than ileal atresia

## Imaging Findings

### General

- massive dilatation of colon proximal to obstruction
- mottled pattern of gas + feces proximal to point of atresia
- functional microcolon
- obstruction to retrograde flow of barium

### US

- dilated hyperechoic distal small bowel + proximal colon ← retained meconium
