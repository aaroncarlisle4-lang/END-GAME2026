# Disease: COLITIS CYSTICA PROFUNDA

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

rare benign chronic condition characterized by dilated sub- mucosal mucin-filled cysts lined

## Key Facts

- **Etiology:** probably related to chronic inflammation
- **Associated with:** solitary rectal ulcer syndrome (as localized polypoid variant)
- **Age:** primarily disease of young adults

## Imaging Findings

### General

- nodular polypoid / cauliflower-like lesions < 2 cm in size, containing no gas
- spiculations mimicking ulcers (barium-filled clefts between nodules)
