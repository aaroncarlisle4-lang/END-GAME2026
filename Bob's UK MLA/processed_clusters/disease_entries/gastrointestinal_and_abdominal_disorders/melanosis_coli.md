# Disease: MELANOSIS COLI

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

benign brown-black discoloration of colonic mucosa

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 10% of autopsies

## Imaging Findings

### General

- nonspecific colonic wall thickening (in severe cases)

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** no malignant potential
