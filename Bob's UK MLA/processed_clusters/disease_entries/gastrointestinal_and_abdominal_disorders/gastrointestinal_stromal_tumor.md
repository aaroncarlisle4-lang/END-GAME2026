# Disease: GASTROINTESTINAL STROMAL TUMOR

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

GIST = SPINDLE CELL / EPITHELIOID TUMOR

## Key Facts

- **Incidence:** < 1% of all gastrointestinal tumors; 7-20÷1,000,000 annually; 4500-6000 new
- **Histo:** spindle cell (70-80%) / epithelioid (20-30%) morphology / mixture of both
- **Location:** usually solitary
- **Associated with:** neurofibromatosis type 1, familial GIST, Carney triad
- **Frequency:** 2-3% of all gastric tumors; most common location of GIST
- **Age:** 31-82 years; M÷F = 1÷2
