# Disease: CATHARTIC COLON

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

prolonged use of stimulant-irritant cathartics (> 15 years) → chronically increased muscular

## Key Facts

- **Location:** involvement of colon proximal to splenic flexure

## Imaging Findings

### General

- effaced mucosa with flattened smooth surface
- diminished / absent haustrations
- "pseudostrictures" = typically smoothly tapered areas of narrowing ← sustained tonus of
- poor evacuation of barium
- flattened + gaping ileocecal valve
- shortened but distensible ascending colon
