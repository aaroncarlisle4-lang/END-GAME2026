# Disease: PERITONEAL METASTASES

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

PERITONEAL CARCINOMATOSIS relatively common intraabdominal spread of malignant tumors

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** adenocarcinoma with abundant mucin + signet ring morphology (most frequent)

- loculated fluid collections in peritoneal cavity
- enhancement of peritoneum

- occasionally sheetlike growth pattern encasing intraperitoneal viscera (DDx: malignant
