# Disease: WALDENSTRÖM MACROGLOBULINEMIA

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

low-grade lymphoid malignancy (= plasma cell dyscrasia) composed of mature plasmacytoid

## Key Facts

- **Incidence:** 0.53÷100,000 annually; frequency 10-15% of multiple myeloma
- **Histo:** macroglobulin proteinaceous hyaline material fills lacteals in lamina propria of small
- **Mean age:** 63 years; M > F

### General

### Bone marrow involvement (91-98%)

- compression fractures of spine (48%)
- diffuse demineralization of spine
- lytic lesions on bone surveys (in up to 20%)
- marrow iso- / hypointense to muscle on T1WI
- enhancement of abnormal marrow on T1WI

### Lymph nodes

- lymphadenopathy (43%)

### Liver & spleen

- hepatosplenomegaly

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** may occasionally progress to full-blown lymphoma
