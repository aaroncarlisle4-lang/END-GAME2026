# Disease: ESOPHAGEAL WEB

**Source section:** GASTROINTESTINAL AND ABDOMINAL DISORDERS

## Definition

complete / incomplete circumferential narrowing caused by 1-2-mm thick (vertical length)

## Key Facts

- **Age:** middle-aged females
- **Path:** hyperkeratosis + chronic inflammation of submucosa
- **Histo:** mucosal membrane covered by squamous epithelium on superior + inferior surfaces

## Imaging Findings

### General

- visualized during maximal distension (in ¹/¹0 of a second)
- arises at right angles from anterior esophageal wall
- thin delicate membrane of uniform thickness of < 3 mm

## Clinical

- **Cx:** high risk of upper esophageal + hypopharyngeal (postcricoid) carcinoma
