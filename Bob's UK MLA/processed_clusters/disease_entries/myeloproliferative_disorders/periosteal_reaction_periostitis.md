# Disease: PERIOSTEAL REACTION / PERIOSTITIS

**Source section:** MYELOPROLIFERATIVE DISORDERS

## Definition

reaction to periosteal irritant pleomorphic, rapidly progressing process undergoing constant change

## Key Facts

- **Location:** at site of tendon + ligament attachment

## Imaging Findings

### General

- even + uniform thickness > 1 mm
- persistent + unchanged for weeks
- bone proliferation (enthesophyte)
- calcification of tendon + ligament
- erosion
