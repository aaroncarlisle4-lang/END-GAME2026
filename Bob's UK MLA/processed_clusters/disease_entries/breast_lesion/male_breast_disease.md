# Disease: MALE BREAST DISEASE

**Source section:** BREAST LESION

## Key Facts

- **Incidence:** 0.17% of all male breast cancers
- **Mean age:** 59 years

## Imaging Findings

### General

- microcalcifications in only 13-30%
- axillary lymphadenopathy: unilateral in primary / bilateral in secondary breast
