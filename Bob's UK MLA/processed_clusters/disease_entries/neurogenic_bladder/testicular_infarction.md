# Disease: TESTICULAR INFARCTION

**Source section:** NEUROGENIC BLADDER

## Definition

extremely rare

## Key Facts

- **Age:** 20-40 years
- **Location:** often in periphery with extension to capsule

## Imaging Findings

### General

- focal hypoechoic wedge-shaped region without flow (most common)
- hyperechoic regions ← hemorrhage / fibrosis
- decrease in testicular size over time ← fibrosis
- diffusely hypoechoic small testis

### MR

- hypo- / (typically) isointense to normal testis on T1WI:
- may have high SI on T1WI ← hemorrhage
- hypointense to normal testis on T2WI
- avascular area + enhancing rim on contrast administration
