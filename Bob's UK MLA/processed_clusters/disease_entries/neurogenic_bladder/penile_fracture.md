# Disease: PENILE FRACTURE

**Source section:** NEUROGENIC BLADDER

## Definition

tear in tunica albuginea → rupture of corpus cavernosum

## Key Facts

- **Associated with:** urethral injury (in 10-20%)
- **Age:** younger man; often related to sports

## Imaging Findings

### MR

- focal disruption of T1- and T2-hypointense tunica albuginea
- adjacent T2-hyperintense hematoma
