## Imaging Findings

### General

- midline mass anterosuperior to vesical dome in the space of Retzius with predominantly
- tumor bulk outside bladder + invasion of bladder dome (88%)
- mean tumor size of 6 cm
- often peripheral curvilinear / punctate stippled psammomatous calcifications (50-72%)
- heterogeneous enhancement

### US

- midline fluid-filled cavity with mixed echogenicity + calcifications adjacent to anterior

### CT

- low-attenuation mass in 60% (mucin)

### MR

- markedly increased SI on T2WI ← mucin

## Clinical

- **Cx:** pseudomyxoma peritonei (in peritoneal carcinomatosis); metastasis to pelvic lymph nodes,
- **Prognosis:** 7-61% 5-year survival rate
- **Rx:** radical cystectomy; often local recurrence within 2 years
