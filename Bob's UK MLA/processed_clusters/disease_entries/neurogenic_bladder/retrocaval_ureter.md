# Disease: RETROCAVAL URETER

**Source section:** NEUROGENIC BLADDER

## Definition

CIRCUMCAVAL URETER

## Key Facts

- **Etiology:** abnormality in embryogenesis of IVC with persistence of right posterior cardinal vein
- **Prevalence:** 0.07%; M÷F = 3÷1

## Imaging Findings

### General

- proximal right ureter swings medially over pedicle of L3-4, passes behind IVC, and emerges
- varying degrees of hydronephrosis + proximal hydrouretero-nephrosis

## Clinical

- **Cx:** recurrent urinary tract infections
