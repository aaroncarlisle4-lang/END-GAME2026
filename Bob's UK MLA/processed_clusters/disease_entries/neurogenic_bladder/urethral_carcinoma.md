# Disease: URETHRAL CARCINOMA

**Source section:** NEUROGENIC BLADDER

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** < 1% of all urologic cancers
- **Age:** 6th-7th decade; M÷F = 1÷4
- **Histo:** SCC (60%), TCC (20%), adenocarcinoma (10%), undifferentiated ca. / sarcoma

## Imaging Findings

### General

- urethral stricture

### MR

- mass hypointense relative to normal corpora on T1WI + T2WI
- lobulated exophytic / deeply infiltrating lesion
- irregularly marginated hypo- / isoechoic mass
- T2-hypointense with heterogeneous enhancement
- lobulated iso- to hypointense on T1WI + T2WI
- heterogeneous contrast enhancement
- exophytic diverticular tumor
- T2-hyperintense + peripheral rim of low SI
- variably heterogeneous contrast enhancement
- mass hypointense on T1WI + hyperintense on T2WI

## Clinical

- **Spread:** direct extension + regional lymph nodes (superficial + deep inguinal + external iliac
- **Rx:** local surgical excision
- **Prognosis:** poor (in 50% metastatic at presentation); survival rate of 50% for distal tumor,
