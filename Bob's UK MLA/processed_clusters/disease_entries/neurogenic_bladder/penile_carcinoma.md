# Disease: PENILE CARCINOMA

**Source section:** NEUROGENIC BLADDER

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 0.4% of all male malignancies in US; 1% in Western countries; 10-20% of all
- **Histo:** squamous cell carcinoma (95%), sarcoma (epitheloid ~, Kaposi ~, leiomyo~,
- **Age:** 6th and 7th decade; < 25% are less than 40 years of age

## Imaging Findings

### MR

- solitary ill-defined infiltrating tumor:
- hypointense relative to corpora on T1WI + T2WI
- enhancement with Gd (less than corpora)

## Clinical

- **Rx:** circumcision, partial / total penectomy ± inguinal lymphadenectomy; radiation therapy
