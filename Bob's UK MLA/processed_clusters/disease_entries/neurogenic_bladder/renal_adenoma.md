# Disease: RENAL ADENOMA

**Source section:** NEUROGENIC BLADDER

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** in 7-15-23% of adults (autopsies); most common cortical lesion; increasing with
- **Age:** usually > 30 years; M÷F = 3÷1

## Imaging Findings

### General

- solitary in 75%, multiple in 25%
- usually < 3 cm in size; subcapsular cortical location
- impossible to differentiate from renal cell carcinoma

## Clinical

- **Cx:** premalignant (adenoma-carcinoma sequence)
- **Prognosis:** average growth rate of 0.4 (range, 0.2-3.5) cm annually; tumors growing < 0.25 cm
