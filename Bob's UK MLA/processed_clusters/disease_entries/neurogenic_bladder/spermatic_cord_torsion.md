# Disease: SPERMATIC CORD TORSION

**Source section:** NEUROGENIC BLADDER

## Definition

TESTICULAR TORSION = TWISTING OF SPERMATIC CORD symptoms present for < 24 hours MISSED TESTICULAR TORSION symptoms present for > 24 hours + less than 10 days TORSION OF TESTICULAR APPENDAGE

## Key Facts

- **Incidence:** 1÷4,000 to 1÷25,000 males of < 25 years annually; 10-fold risk in undescended
- **Age:** any, predominantly adolescence
- **Prevalence:** 31-67%
- **Location:** near superior pole of testis; L > R
