# Disease: PELVIC LIPOMATOSIS & FIBROLIPOMATOSIS

**Source section:** NEUROGENIC BLADDER

## Definition

nonmalignant overgrowth of adipose tissue with minimal fibrotic + inflammatory components

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 0.6÷100,000 to 1.7÷100,000
- **Mean age:** 48 (range, 9-80) years; M÷F = 10÷1 to 18÷1; racial predilection for blacks (67%);
- **Path:** unencapsulated mature homogeneous white fat separated by thin fibrous septa

## Imaging Findings

### General

- elongation + elevation of urinary bladder with symmetric inverted pear shape / teardrop
- elongation of posterior urethra
- medial / lateral displacement of lower ureters
- pelvic lucency (CT confirmatory)

### GI tract

- straightening + elongation + narrowing of rectum
- stretching + elevation of rectosigmoid and sigmoid colon out of pelvis
- increase in sacrorectal space > 10 mm

### Vessels

- stretching + thinning of femoral veins
