# Disease: URACHAL ANOMALIES

**Source section:** NEUROGENIC BLADDER

## Definition

cystic dilatation of urachus periodically emptying into bladder / umbilicus fistula between bladder and umbilicus gradually enlarging cyst ← closure of both ends of urachus urachus communicates only with bladder dome urachus patent only at umbilicus

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 1÷200,000 live births
- **Associated with:** urachal cyst

## Imaging Findings

### General

- cystic extraperitoneal mass
- no communication with bladder or umbilicus
- thickened tubular structure with echogenic center

## Clinical

- **Cx:** infection (23%), intestinal obstruction, hemorrhage into cyst, peritonitis from rupture,
