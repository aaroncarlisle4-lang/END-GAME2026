# Disease: SCHWANNOMA OF ADRENAL GLAND

**Source section:** NEUROGENIC BLADDER

## Definition

benign nerve sheath tumor of neural crest origin

## Key Facts

- **Location:** retroperitoneum (3%), adrenal gland (rare)
- **Age:** 20-40 years

## Imaging Findings

### CT

- heterogeneously enhancing hypoattenuating mass

### MR

- marked variability depending on degree of degeneration
- isointense signal to muscle on T1WI
- hyperintensity on T2WI
