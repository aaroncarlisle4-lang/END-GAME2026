# Disease: TESTICULAR MICROLITHIASIS

**Source section:** NEUROGENIC BLADDER

## Key Facts

- **Etiology:** defect in phagocytotic activity of Sertoli cells leaving degenerated intratubular debris
- **Prevalence:** 0.6%; 5% in asymptomatic men aged 18-35 years
- **Histo:** laminated concretions within lumen of seminiferous tubules

## Imaging Findings

### General

- 1-2-mm (> 5) hyperechoic nonshadowing foci scattered throughout the parenchyma of both
- may be asymmetrically distributed, unilateral, clustered in periphery

## Clinical

- **Cx:** concurrent germ cell tumor in up to 40% (21.6 x risk)
- **Prognosis:** the vast majority of patients with testicular microlithiasis will not develop
