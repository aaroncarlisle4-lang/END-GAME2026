# Disease: RENOMEDULLARY INTERSTITIAL CELL TUMOR

**Source section:** NEUROGENIC BLADDER

## Definition

MEDULLARY FIBROMA rare benign often incidental mesenchymal neoplasm of kidney

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 50% of autopsies in adults
- **Histo:** stellate spindle cells on basophilic stroma
- **Size:** < 5 mm (most)
- **Location:** renal pyramid

## Imaging Findings

### General

- small nonenhancing noncalcified hypoattenuating solid mass
- hypointense on T1WI + T2WI (← rich collagen content)
