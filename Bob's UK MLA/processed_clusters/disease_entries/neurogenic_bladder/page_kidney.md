# Disease: PAGE KIDNEY

**Source section:** NEUROGENIC BLADDER

## Definition

renin-angiotensin-mediated hypertension ← constrictive ischemic nephropathy (= reduction

## Imaging Findings

### General

- (compressive) subcapsular fluid collection:
- indentation / flattening of renal margin
- distortion of renal contour + thinning of renal parenchyma
- kidney surrounded by fibrotic band ± calcification
- stretching + splaying of intrarenal vessels
- enlarged + displaced capsular artery
- delayed nephrogram
- slow arterial washout
- elevated resistive index at spectral Doppler
- globally decreased renal function at perfusion scintigraphy
