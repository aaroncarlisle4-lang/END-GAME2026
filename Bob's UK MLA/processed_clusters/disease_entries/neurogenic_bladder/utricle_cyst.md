# Disease: UTRICLE CYST

**Source section:** NEUROGENIC BLADDER

## Definition

dilatation of prostatic utricle (sometimes believed to be a remnant of the müllerian duct)

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 1-5% of general population
- **Age:** 1st-2nd decade
- **Location:** arise in midline from verumontanum

## Imaging Findings

### General

- usually 8-10-mm long pear-shaped cyst posterior to urethra
- NO extension above base of prostate (DDx: müllerian duct cyst, usually larger with extension

## Clinical

- **Cx:** recurrent epididymitis, hematospermia, carcinomatous metaplasia
