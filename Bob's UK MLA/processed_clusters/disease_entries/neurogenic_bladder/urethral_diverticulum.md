# Disease: URETHRAL DIVERTICULUM

**Source section:** NEUROGENIC BLADDER

## Definition

protrusion of urethra into periurethral fascia

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 0.6-6% of women
- **Age:** 26-74 years; 6 x more common in black women (?)
- **Histo:** same as urethral mucosa

## Imaging Findings

### General

- rounded / elongated sac connected to urethra

### US

- echogenic focus with distinct acoustic shadow ← stone

### MR

- cystic T2-hyperintense lesion with various degrees of extensions around the
- oval / U-shaped "saddlebag" / circumferential
- single / multiple
- unilocular / multiseptated
- narrow / wide neck
- heterogeneous hyperintense signal compared with urine on T1WI if content is
- fluid-fluid level on T2WI if inflammation present
