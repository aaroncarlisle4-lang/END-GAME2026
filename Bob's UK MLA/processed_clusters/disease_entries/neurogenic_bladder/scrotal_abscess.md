# Disease: SCROTAL ABSCESS

**Source section:** NEUROGENIC BLADDER

## Imaging Findings

### NUC

- marked increase in perfusion = hot hemiscrotum with photon-deficient area representing the
- increased scrotal uptake with leukocyte imaging

### US

- hypoechoic / complex fluid collection (differentiation of intra- from extratesticular abscess
- intratesticular abscess with low-level internal echoes + shaggy wall
- focal area of absent color Doppler flow
- hypervascular surrounding testicular parenchyma
- skin thickening
- hydrocele

### MR

- hypointense on T1WI + hyperintense on T2WI ← fluid content
- hypointense rim on T2WI
- nonenhancing lesion + avid enhancement of surrounding tissue
- central hyperintensity on DWI with corresponding low signal on ADC map ← restricted
