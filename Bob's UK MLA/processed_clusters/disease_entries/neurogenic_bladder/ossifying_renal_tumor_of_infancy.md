# Disease: OSSIFYING RENAL TUMOR OF INFANCY

**Source section:** NEUROGENIC BLADDER

## Definition

extremely rare benign renal mass originating from urothelium

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** only 11 cases in literature
- **Age:** 6 days-14 months; M > F
- **Histo:** osteoid core, osteoblasts, spindle cells
- **Location:** L > R kidney; attached to renal papilla

## Imaging Findings

### General

- 2-3-cm polypoid mass:
- calcified (in 80%) resembling a staghorn calculus
- filling defect of collecting system
- partial obstruction of collecting system
- echogenic mass + shadowing
- poor enhancement on CT

## Clinical

- **Rx:** nephron-sparing resection curative
