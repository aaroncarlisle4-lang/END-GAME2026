# Disease: PYELOCALICEAL DIVERTICULUM

**Source section:** NEUROGENIC BLADDER

## Definition

PYELOGENIC CYST = PERICALICEAL CYST = CALICEAL DIVERTICULUM uroepithelium-lined urine-filled cavity within renal cortex communicating with renal

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 2.1-4.5÷1000 intravenous urograms

## Imaging Findings

### General

- appearance of simple cyst
- opacification during excretory phase may be delayed and remain so for prolonged period →
- mass effect on adjacent pelvicaliceal system if large enough

### US

- anechoic cyst
- ± layering mobile echogenic material ← formation of single / multiple stones (50%) / milk

## Clinical

- **Cx:** hemorrhage, recurrent infection, cyst rupture, malignancy (exceedingly rare)
