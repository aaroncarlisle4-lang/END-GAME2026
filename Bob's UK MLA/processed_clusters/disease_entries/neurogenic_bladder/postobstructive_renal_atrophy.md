# Disease: POSTOBSTRUCTIVE RENAL ATROPHY

**Source section:** NEUROGENIC BLADDER

## Definition

generalized papillary atrophy usually following successful surgical correction of urinary tract

## Imaging Findings

### General

- small smooth kidney, usually unilateral
- dilated calices with effaced papillae
- thinned cortex
