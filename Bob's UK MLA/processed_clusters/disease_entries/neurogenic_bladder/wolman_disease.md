# Disease: WOLMAN DISEASE

**Source section:** NEUROGENIC BLADDER

## Definition

PRIMARY FAMILIAL XANTHOMATOSIS rare autosomal recessive lipidosis with accumulation of cholesterol esters and triglycerides

## Key Facts

- **Etiology:** deficiency of lysosomal acid esterase / acid lipase

## Imaging Findings

### General

- hepatosplenomegaly
- extensive bilateral punctate calcifications (calcification of fatty-acid soaps) throughout
- enlarged fat-containing lymph nodes
- small bowel wall thickening ← infiltration of mucosa of small bowel by lipid-filled
- generalized osteoporosis

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** death occurs within first 6 months of life
