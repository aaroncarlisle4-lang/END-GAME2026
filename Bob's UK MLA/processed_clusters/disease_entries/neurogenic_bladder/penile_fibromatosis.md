# Disease: PENILE FIBROMATOSIS

**Source section:** NEUROGENIC BLADDER

## Definition

PEYRONIE DISEASE = INDURATIO PENIS PLASTICA benign acquired condition characterized by chronic inflammation → fibrosis + focal

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 3%
- **Age:** 40-60 years
- **Associated with:** palmar fibromatosis (Dupuytren contracture) in 16-20%, plantar

## Imaging Findings

### General

- focal hyperechoic thickening of tunica albuginea with shadowing ← calcification
- focal nonshadowing echogenic thickening of tunica
- nodular echogenic thickening of intercorporeal septum
- iso- / hypoechoic plaques (rare)
- hypointense focal thickening of tunica on T1WI + T2WI
- enhancement during active inflammation
