# Disease: PENA-SHOKEIR PHENOTYPE

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

autosomal recessive syndrome (45% sporadic, 55% familial) characterized by fetal akinesia

## Imaging Findings

### Limbs:   limited movement, knee + hip ankylosis (arthrogryposis), abnormal shape +

- craniofacial anomalies
- polyhydramnios
- IUGR
- short umbilical cord

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** still birth
