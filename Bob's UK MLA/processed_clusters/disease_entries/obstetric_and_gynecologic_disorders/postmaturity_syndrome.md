# Disease: POSTMATURITY SYNDROME

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

inability of aging placenta to support demands of fetus fetus undelivered by 42nd week MA

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** in 15% of all postterm gravidas

## Imaging Findings

### General

- grade 3 placenta (in 85%), grade 2 (in 15%), grade 1 (in 0%)
- decreased subcutaneous fat + wrinkling of skin
- long fingernails
- decreased vernix

## Clinical

- **Cx:** meconium aspiration, perinatal asphyxia, thermal instability
