# Disease: PLACENTA ACCRETA

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

defect in normal decidua basalis from prior trauma allows abnormal adherence + penetration

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 1÷2,500-7,000 deliveries related to increase in uterine surgery; in 5% of placenta
- **Associated with:** placenta previa (in 20%)

## Imaging Findings

- abnormal uterine bulging
