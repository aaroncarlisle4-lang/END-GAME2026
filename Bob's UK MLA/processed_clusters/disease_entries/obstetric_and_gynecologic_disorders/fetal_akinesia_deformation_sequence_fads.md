# Disease: FETAL AKINESIA DEFORMATION SEQUENCE (FADS

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Location:** generalized + symmetric; lower > upper limbs

## Imaging Findings

### General

- abnormal limb position, craniofacial deformation
- growth restriction, polyhydramnios
- abnormally short umbilical cord

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** usually lethal ← lung hypoplasia
