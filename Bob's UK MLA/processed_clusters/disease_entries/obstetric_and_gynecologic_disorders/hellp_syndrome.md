# Disease: HELLP SYNDROME

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

Hemolysis, Elevated Liver enzymes, Low Platelets

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 4-12% of patients with severe preeclampsia / eclampsia; higher in White women
- **Histo:** portal areas surrounded by deposited fibrin + hemorrhage + hepatocellular necrosis

## Imaging Findings

### General

- hepatomegaly
- fatty infiltration of liver (peak at 35th week)
- intraparenchymal hemorrhage of liver ← subcapsular hematoma / rupture into peritoneal
- hepatic edema ← early ischemia ← necrosis
- ascites + pleural effusions
- subcapsular hematoma of kidney
- vitreous hemorrhage
