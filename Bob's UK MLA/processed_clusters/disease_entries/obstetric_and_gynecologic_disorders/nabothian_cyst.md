# Disease: NABOTHIAN CYST

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

most common benign masslike lesion of cervix

## Key Facts

- **Incidence:** in women during reproductive years
- **Size:** < 1 cm (majority); may enlarge to become symptomatic

## Imaging Findings

### General

- simple anechoic cyst without vascularity (incidentally detected at imaging)
- may increase in echogenicity ← proteinaceous / hemorrhagic content
- "tunnel cluster" (in up to 8%) = multicystic dilatation of endocervical glands mimicking

## Clinical

- **Rx:** none (unless symptomatic)
