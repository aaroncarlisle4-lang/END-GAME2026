# Disease: BILIARY SCINTIGRAPHY

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

hepatic phase; 85% extracted by hepatocytes tracer enters anion pathway of bilirubin

## Imaging Findings

### Bowel

- gallbladder appearance within 60 minutes
- gallbladder visualization within 30 minutes in 90%
- small bowel activity within 90 min (80% within 60 min)
