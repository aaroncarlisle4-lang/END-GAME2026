# Disease: PARAOVARIAN CYSTADENOMA

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Location:** typically unilateral

## Imaging Findings

### General

- simple cyst
- one / more small nodules along a smooth inner wall (86%)
- ± septations

## Clinical

- **Cx:** malignant degeneration in 2-3%
