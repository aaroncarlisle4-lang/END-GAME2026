# Disease: LIVER SCINTIGRAPHY

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

LIVER-SPLEEN SCAN

## Imaging Findings

### General

- colloid shift away from liver in diffuse hepatic dysfunction / decreased hepatic
- ↑ bone marrow activity in hemolytic anemia
- ↑ splenic activity in hypersplenism of splenomegaly / cancer / systemic illness
