# Disease: UTERINE RUPTURE IN PREGNANCY

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

disruption of all layers surrounding the fetus (membranes, decidua, myometrium, serosa)

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 3-5% for classic cesarean sections; 1-2% for lower segment operations; 1% of

## Imaging Findings

### General

- full-thickness defect of uterine wall
- extrusion of fetus into the abdomen
- hemoperitoneum

## Clinical

- **Cx:** hypofibrinogenemia (triggered by excessive blood loss, trauma, amniotic fluid embolism)
