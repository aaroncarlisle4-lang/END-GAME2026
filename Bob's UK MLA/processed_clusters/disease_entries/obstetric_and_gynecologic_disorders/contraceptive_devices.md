# Disease: CONTRACEPTIVE DEVICES

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

improper position within uterine cavity

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 10%
- **Location:** cervix, vagina
- **Prevalence:** 2÷100 women annually

## Imaging Findings

### General

- double echogenic line with plastic IUD
- reverberation echoes with metal IUD
- 4-5 echogenic dots on SAG view
- horizontal line / dot on TRV view
- echogenic solid line on SAG view
- series of echoes / dot on TRV view
- dot in fundus + solid line in corpus on SAG view
- solid line in fundus + dot in corpus on TRV view
- embedment in endometrium / myometrium (18%)
- migration into peritoneal cavity → freely floating / encased in adhesions / adherent to
- IUD may not be visualized after 1st trimester → as uterus grows IUD is drawn into cavity
- ± ectopic pregnancy
- < 50% of inner coil within uterine cavity
