# Disease: NUCHAL CORD

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

umbilical cord encircling fetal neck: single loop (96%) > 2 loops (2-3%) > 3 or more loops

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 5-29% of pregnancies; increases with fetal age; frequently transient
- **Associated with:** increased cord length, small fetus, vertex presentation, polyhydramnios

## Imaging Findings

### General

- umbilical cord loop > 360° around fetal neck
- two adjacent cross sections of cord on longitudinal view of neck (diagnosis facilitated by
- "divot" sign = indentation of skin by nuchal cord suggests tight loop
- NO effect on fetal MCA + umbilical artery flow velocimetry
