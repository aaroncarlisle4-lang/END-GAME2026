# Disease: GARTNER DUCT CYST

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

secretory retention cyst of remnant mesonephric duct

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 1-2%
- **Histo:** lined by nonmucinous flat cuboidal / low columnar epithelium
- **Size:** usually < 2 cm

## Imaging Findings

### General

- well-defined round lesion with fluid contents:
- T2-hyperintense + T1-hypointense fluid
- hemorrhagic / proteinaceous contents hypointense on T2WI + hyperintense on T1WI
- may contain septa
- large cyst may displace ureter upward / protrude through introitus

## Clinical

- **Cx:** dyspareunia; interference with vaginal delivery
- **Rx:** cyst aspiration; tetracycline sclerotherapy; surgical excision
