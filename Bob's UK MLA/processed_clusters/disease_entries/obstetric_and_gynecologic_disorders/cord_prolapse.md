# Disease: CORD PROLAPSE

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

prolapse / descent of cord into endocervical canal after rupture

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 0.5% at delivery, 2-3% prenatally

## Clinical

- **Cx:** cord compression with high perinatal mortality
- **N.B.:** MEDICAL EMERGENCY! Alert obstetrician immediately!
