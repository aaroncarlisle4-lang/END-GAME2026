## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 1÷1,200 to 1÷2,000 pregnancies; < 5% of abortions
- **Path:** complex multicystic mass = "cluster of grapes"
- **Histo:** generalized hydropic swelling of all chorionic villi with prominent acellular space

## Imaging Findings

### MR

- predominantly hypointense heterogeneous mass distending uterine cavity on T1WI
- hyperintense mass on T2WI
- ± focal areas of hemorrhage + cystic spaces
- avid enhancement
- hypointense myometrium (without invasion)
- normal gestation with placenta + separate typical echogenic material of a hydatidiform
- ovarian theca lutein cysts
- hyperechoic tissue with punctate lucencies
- irregular focal hyperechoic region within myometrium
- bilateral theca lutein cysts, 4-8 cm in size
- myometrial invasion occasionally demonstrable
- nearly always coexistent fetus with severe abnormalities + IUGR
- placenta with numerous cystic spaces
- oligohydramnios

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** noninvasive in 85%, locally invasive in 13%, metastasizing in 2%
- **Rx:** dilatation + suction curettage (curative in 85%)
- **Spread:** lung, vagina
