# Disease: MASSIVE OVARIAN EDEMA

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

tumorlike condition with marked enlargement of one / both (occasionally) ovaries ←

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** rare; common in young woman during ovulation induction / pregnancy
- **Location:** right > left ovary
- **Age:** 6-33 (average 21) years

## Imaging Findings

### General

- solid / multicystic adnexal mass
- ovarian diameter of 5-40 (mean 11.5) cm

## Clinical

- **Rx:** oophorectomy / salpingo-oophorectomy / wedge resection with ovarian suspension
