- **Associated with:** prolonged labor, premature rupture of membranes, retained clots,
- **Path:** inflammation of liver capsule / diaphragm
- **Location:** anterior to liver (original description), hepatorenal fossa (= Morison space),

- **Prevalence:** in 8% of women with PID / endometriosis
