# Disease: FACIAL CLEFTING

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

failure of fusion of the 2 medial nasal prominences lack of fusion of mesenchymal masses of lateral palatine processes around 8th-9th weeks

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 0.5÷1,000 in blacks; 1÷1,000 live births in white population; 1.5÷1,000 in Asians;
- **Associated with:** anomalies in 20% (most frequently clubfoot); NO chromosomal
- **Location:** L > R
