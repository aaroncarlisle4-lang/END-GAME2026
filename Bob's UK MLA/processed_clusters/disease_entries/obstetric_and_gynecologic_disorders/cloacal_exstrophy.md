**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

exstrophy of all structures forming cloaca (rectum, bladder, lower GU tract) similar to

## Imaging Findings

### General

- low abdominal wall defect
- absence of visible bladder
- omphalocele (initial observation) as craniad part of large complex anterior wall defect
- "elephant trunk" = bowel herniation between everted bladder halves
