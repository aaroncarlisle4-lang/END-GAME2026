# Disease: OMPHALOMESENTERIC DUCT CYST

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Etiology:** persistence + dilatation of an omphalomesenteric / vitelline duct segment → joining
- **Histo:** cyst lined by columnar mucin-secreting GI epithelium
- **Location:** usually in close proximity to fetus

## Imaging Findings

### General

- umbilical cord cyst up to 6 cm in diameter
- beneath amniotic surface of cord (= eccentric)
