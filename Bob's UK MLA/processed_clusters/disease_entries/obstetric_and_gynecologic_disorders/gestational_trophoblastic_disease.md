# Disease: GESTATIONAL TROPHOBLASTIC DISEASE

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

group of disorders characterized by abnormal proliferation of trophoblastic tissue with fertilization of one egg by two sperm = chromosomes completely / predominantly of

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** < 1% of all gynecologic malignancies
- **Associated with:** molar pregnancy (most), post abortion, ectopic pregnancy, term pregnancy
