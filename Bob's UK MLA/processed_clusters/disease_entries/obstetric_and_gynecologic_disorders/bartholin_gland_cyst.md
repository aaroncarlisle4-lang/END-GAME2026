# Disease: BARTHOLIN GLAND CYST

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

retention cyst

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** lined by mucinous transitional cell / columnar epithelium
- **Location:** posterolateral inferior ⅓ of vaginal introitus medial to labia minora at / below level
- **Size:** 1-4 cm

## Imaging Findings

### General

- unilocular cyst hyperintense on T2WI
- variable T1 hyperintensity depending on proteinaceous / mucinous contents

## Clinical

- **Rx:** administration of silver nitrate; marsupialization; surgical excision
