# Disease: ARTERIOVENOUS MALFORMATION OF UTERUS

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

UTERINE ARTERIOVENOUS FISTULA = UTERINE CIRSOID ANEURYSM

## Key Facts

- **Associated with:** dilatation & curettage, endometrial carcinoma, gestational trophoblastic

## Imaging Findings

### MR

- tortuous tubular signal voids in myometrium / parametrium / protruding into endometrial
- vascular lake with sluggish flow hyperintense on T2WI
- intensely enhancing lesion isointense to vessels on contrast-enhanced dynamic subtraction
