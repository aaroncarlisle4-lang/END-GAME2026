# Disease: HYBRID PET/CT IMAGING

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

combination of high-resolution PET scanner + CT scanner allows acquisition of 3- histologically heterogeneous group of cancers derived from cells of immune system

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 5% of all cancers in USA
- **Location:** skin, stomach, small intestine (most common)

## Imaging Findings

### General

- enlargement of lymph nodes / secondary lymphoid tissues
- may arise from almost any organ = extranodal lymphoma
- uptake lower for indolent compared to aggressive lymphoma (SUV > 10 likely to
- aggressive transformation (= change from low-grade to high-grade lymphoma) in 5-
- splenic > hepatic uptake indicates splenic involvement
- heterogeneous scattered uptake in bone marrow involvement

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** 6.0÷100,000 (= 20,150) deaths annually in 2013
