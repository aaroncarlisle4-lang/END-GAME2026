# Disease: KRUKENBERG TUMOR

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

metastatic ovarian adenocarcinoma most commonly originating in GI tract

## Key Facts

- **Age:** any age, most common in 5th-6th decade

## Imaging Findings

### General

- solid mass + intratumoral cysts; in 60-80% bilateral
- hypo- / hyperechoic mass ± cystic degeneration

### MR

- variable hypointensity on T2WI ← mucin-filled signet-ring cells + abundant collagen
- moderate to marked enhancement of solid components
