# Disease: VELAMENTOUS CORD INSERTION

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

umbilical cord insertion into membranes outside placental margin = attachment of cord to

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 0.09-1.8%, more frequent in multiple gestations

## Imaging Findings

### General

- segments of umbilical cord course between amnion + chorion unprotected by Wharton jelly /
- splayed umbilical vessels at periphery of placenta
