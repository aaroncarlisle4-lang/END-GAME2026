# Disease: PLACENTA PREVIA

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

abnormally low implantation of ovum with the placenta covering all / part of internal cervical

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 0.5% of all deliveries; 3-5% of all pregnancies complicated by 3rd trimester

## Imaging Findings

### MR

- abnormal position of placenta infringing on cervical os

## Clinical

- **Cx:** secondary to premature detachment of placenta from lower uterine segment
- **Rx:** precludes vaginal delivery + pelvic examination
