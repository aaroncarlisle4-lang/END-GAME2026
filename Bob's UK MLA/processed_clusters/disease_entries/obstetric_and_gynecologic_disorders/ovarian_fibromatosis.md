# Disease: OVARIAN FIBROMATOSIS

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

rare benign condition causing tumorlike enlargement of ovaries related to massive ovarian

## Imaging Findings

### General

- nonspecific enlargement of ovaries
- at least partial preservation of ovarian anatomy

### US

- enlarged heterogeneously echogenic ovary with areas of acoustic shadowing
- low flow at Doppler imaging

### MR

- hypointense areas on T1WI + T2WI
- "black garland" on T2WI ← fibrous tissue encasing peripheral aspect of ovary
