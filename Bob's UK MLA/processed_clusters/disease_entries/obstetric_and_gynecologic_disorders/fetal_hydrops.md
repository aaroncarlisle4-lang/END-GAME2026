# Disease: FETAL HYDROPS

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

excess of total body water → extracellular accumulation of fluid in tissues + serous ERYTHROBLASTOSIS FETALIS lysis of fetal RBCs by maternal IgG antibodies
