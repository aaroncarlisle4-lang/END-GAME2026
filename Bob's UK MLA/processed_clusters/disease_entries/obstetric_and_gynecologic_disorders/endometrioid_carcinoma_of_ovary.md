# Disease: ENDOMETRIOID CARCINOMA OF OVARY

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 8-15% of all ovarian cancers; 2nd most common malignant ovarian neoplasm (after
- **Associated with:** hyperplasia / carcinoma of the uterine endometrium in 20-33%
- **Path:** malignant mixed mesodermal tumor = carcinoma-sarcoma is grouped with endometrioid
- **Histo:** tubular glandular pattern with a pseudostratified epithelium resembling endometrial

## Imaging Findings

### General

- solid / complex (= cystic + solid) tumor
- bilateral in 15% of stage I cases

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** better than serous / mucinous carcinomas
