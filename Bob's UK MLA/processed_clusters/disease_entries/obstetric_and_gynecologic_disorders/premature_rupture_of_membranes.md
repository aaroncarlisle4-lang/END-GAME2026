# Disease: PREMATURE RUPTURE OF MEMBRANES

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

spontaneous rupture of chorioamnionic membranes before the onset of labor

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** overall 2.1-17.1%; PPROM 0.9-4.4%;

## Imaging Findings

### General

- decrease in the amount of amniotic fluid

### CT

- products of conception low-lying / in cervix
- blood in cervix / vagina ←signs of impending / ongoing spontaneous abortion
