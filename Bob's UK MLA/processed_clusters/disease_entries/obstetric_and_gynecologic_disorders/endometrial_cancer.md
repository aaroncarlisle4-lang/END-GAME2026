# Disease: ENDOMETRIAL CANCER

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Incidence:** 60,050 new cases with 10,470 deaths annually (2016)
- **Histo:** adenocarcinoma (90-95%), sarcoma (1-3%)
- **Peak age:** 55-62 years; 74% > age 50
- **Location:** predominantly in uterine fundus; 24% in isthmic portion
