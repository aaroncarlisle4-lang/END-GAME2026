# Disease: PERLMAN SYNDROME

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

rare fetal overgrowth disorder characterized by visceromegaly, renal lesions, distinctive

## Imaging Findings

### General

- cystic hygroma
- fetal macrosomia
- macrocephaly
- agenesis of corpus callosum
- choroid plexus cyst
- nephromegaly + hydronephrosis
- hepatomegaly
- cryptorchidism
- polyhydramnios

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** death within first few days of life
