# Disease: BECKWITH-WIEDEMANN SYNDROME

**Source section:** OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC DISORDERS

## Definition

EMG SYNDROME (Exomphalos = omphalocele, Macroglossia, Gigantism) common autosomal dominant overgrowth syndrome with reduced penetrance + variable

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 1÷13,700 to 1÷14,300 live births; M÷F = 1÷1
- **Histo:** disordered lobar arrangement, medullary dysplasia

## Imaging Findings

### General

- advanced bone age
- nephromegaly
- increased cortical echogenicity ← glomeruloneogenesis
- accentuation of corticomedullary definition
- medullary sponge kidney
- pyelocalyceal diverticula
- LGA fetus with growth along 95th percentile
- polyhydramnios (51%)
- thickened placenta
- long umbilical cord
