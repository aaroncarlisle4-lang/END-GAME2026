# Disease: BREAST DURING PREGNANCY & LACTATION

**Source section:** BREAST ANATOMY

## Definition

formation + secretion of milk (= fat + lactose + proteins) formation of colostrum around 16 weeks EGA secretion of copious milk around day 4 postpartum maintenance of milk supply around day 10 postpartum via autocrine control decreased milk production ← epithelial apoptosis

## Imaging Findings

### General

- diffuse, markedly dense, heterogeneously coarse, confluent nodular breast parenchyma
- marked decrease in adipose tissue
- slight diffuse hypoechogenicity of enlarged nonfatty fibroglandular component
- diffuse hyperechogenicity of breast parenchyma
- prominent ductal system + increased vascularity
- diffuse high SI on T2WI ← ↑ fraction of mobile water
- rapid enhancement + early plateau
