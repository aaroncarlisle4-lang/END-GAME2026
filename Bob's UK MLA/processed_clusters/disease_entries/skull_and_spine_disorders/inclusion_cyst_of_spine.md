# Disease: INCLUSION CYST OF SPINE

**Source section:** SKULL AND SPINE DISORDERS

## Definition

uni- / multilocular benign cystic tumor lined by stratified squamous epithelium containing cystic tumor lined by a membrane composed of epidermal elements of skin

## Key Facts

- **Path:** (a)   wall ± calcifications (in 31%) and papillary projections along inner surface
- **Prevalence:** 1-2% of intraspinal tumors; 0.7-1.8% of all CNS tumors
- **Location:** lumbosacral (60%), cauda equina (20%)
- **Mean age:** 34 (range, 3-71) years; M÷F = 1.35÷1.00

## Imaging Findings

- CT myelography facilitates detection
- well-demarcated isoattenuating mass ± hypoattenuating fat
- ± wall calcifications + papillary projections
