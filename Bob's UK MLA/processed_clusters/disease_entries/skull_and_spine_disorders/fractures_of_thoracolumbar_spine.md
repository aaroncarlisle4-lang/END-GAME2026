# Disease: FRACTURES OF THORACOLUMBAR SPINE

**Source section:** SKULL AND SPINE DISORDERS

## Definition

distance of line drawn between posterior margins of adjacent vertebral bodies + inability to maintain normal associations between vertebral segments while under area of transition between a stiff + mobile segment of spine damage of all 3 columns ← horizontally impacting force SEATBELT FRACTURE purely ligamentous disruption leading to lumbar subluxation / dislocation injury caused by three-point restraint type (combined lap and shoulder belt device)

## Key Facts

- **Location:** ⅔ at thoracolumbar junction
- **Frequency:** in 3% of all blunt chest trauma

## Clinical

- **Cx:** kyphotic progression → subsequent vertebral collapse
- **N.B.:** inverse relationship between osseous destruction and ligamentous injury!
- **Prognosis:** minor and stable injury; 10% mortality
