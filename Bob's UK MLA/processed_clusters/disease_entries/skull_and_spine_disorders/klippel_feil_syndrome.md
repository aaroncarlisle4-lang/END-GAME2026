# Disease: KLIPPEL-FEIL SYNDROME

**Source section:** SKULL AND SPINE DISORDERS

## Definition

BREVICOLLIS synostosis of two / more cervical segments

## Key Facts

- **Location:** cervical spine

## Imaging Findings

### General

- wide and flat vertebral bodies
- ± cervicothoracic / cervical / occipitoatlantal fusion:
- fusion of vertebral bodies and posterior elements
- ± hemivertebrae
- scoliosis
- rib fusion
- Sprengel deformity (25-40%)
- ear anomalies: absent auditory canal, microtia, deformed ossicles, underdevelopment of bony
