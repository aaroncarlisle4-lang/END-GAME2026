# Disease: SCHEUERMANN DISEASE

**Source section:** SKULL AND SPINE DISORDERS

## Definition

SPINAL OSTEOCHONDROSIS = KYPHOSIS DORSALIS JUVENILIS = VERTEBRAL disorder consisting of vertebral wedging + endplate irregularity + narrowing of intervertebral

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** in 31% (21%) of males (females) with back pain
- **Age:** onset at puberty
- **Location:** lower thoracic / upper lumbar vertebrae; in mild cases limited to 3-4 vertebral

## Imaging Findings

### General

- anterior wedging of vertebral body of > 5°
- increased anteroposterior diameter of vertebral body
- slight narrowing of disk space
- kyphosis of > 40°/ loss of lordosis; scoliosis
- Schmorl nodes = intravertebral herniation of nucleus pulposus = depression in contour of
- flattened area in superior surface of epiphyseal ring anteriorly = avulsion fracture of ring
- detached epiphyseal ring anteriorly
