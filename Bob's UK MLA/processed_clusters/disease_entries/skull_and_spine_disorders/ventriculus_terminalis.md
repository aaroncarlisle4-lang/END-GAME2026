# Disease: VENTRICULUS TERMINALIS

**Source section:** SKULL AND SPINE DISORDERS

## Definition

small ependyma-lined oval cyst at the transition from tip of conus medullaris to origin of filum

## Key Facts

- **Size:** 8-10 mm long, 2-4 mm in diameter
