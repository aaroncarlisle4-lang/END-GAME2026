# Disease: CURRARINO TRIAD

**Source section:** SKULL AND SPINE DISORDERS

## Definition

triad of anorectal malformation + sacrococcygeal osseous defect + presacral mass (= ASP rare syndrome characterized by autosomal dominant genetic inheritance (in > 50%)

## Key Facts

- **Path:** presacral mass consists of teratoma, anterior sacral meningocele > dermoid cyst,
- **Age:** < 16 years at diagnosis (in 80%)
