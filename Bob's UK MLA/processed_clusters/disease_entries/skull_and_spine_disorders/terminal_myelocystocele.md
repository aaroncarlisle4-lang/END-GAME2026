# Disease: TERMINAL MYELOCYSTOCELE

**Source section:** SKULL AND SPINE DISORDERS

## Definition

combination of posterior spina bifida + meningocele + tethered cord + hydromyelia + cystic

## Key Facts

- **Associated with:** anorectal + genitourinary + vertebral anomalies: anal atresia, cloacal

## Imaging Findings

### General

- spinal cord surrounded dorsally + ventrally by dilated subarachnoid space of the meningocele
- nerve root exit ventrally
- bifid spinal cord
- hydromyelia
