# Disease: BRACHIAL PLEXUS INJURY

**Source section:** SKULL AND SPINE DISORDERS

## Definition

most severe nerve injury of extremities

## Imaging Findings

### General

- displacement of spinal cord to contralateral side ← absence of normal nerve root traction
- pouchlike nerve root sleeve at site of avulsion
- asymmetric slightly deformed root sleeve + nerve roots
- obliteration of tip of root sleeve
- traumatic meningocele
- contrast extravasation collecting in axilla
- metrizamide in neural foramina

### MR

- focal T2-hyperintense cord changes ← edema of acute phase / myelomalacia in chronic
- focal T2-hypointense cord changes ← hemosiderin
- enhancement of morphologically normal intradural nerve root / root stump ← functional
- abnormal enhancement of paraspinal (multifidus) muscle ← muscle denervation = indirect
- thickening of postganglionic brachial plexus ← edema + fibrosis
