# Disease: MENINGEAL CYST

**Source section:** SKULL AND SPINE DISORDERS

## Definition

abnormal dilatation of meninges within sacral canal / foramina ± presacral component dilated nerve-root sleeve as normal variant OCCULT INTRASACRAL MENINGOCELE

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 5%
- **Location:** posterior rootlets (S2 + S3 most common)

## Imaging Findings

### General

- unilocular / multilocular cyst
- remodeling erosion of sacral canal / foramen ← CSF pulsations
- thinned cortical margins
- cyst communicates freely with subarachnoid space
- cyst wall continuous with arachnoid + dura
- cyst cavity occupies space between peri- and endoneurium
- bone remodeling + enlargement of neural foramen
- cyst does not communicate with subarachnoid space

## Clinical

- **Rx:** surgical decompression, sacral laminectomy, percutaneous drainage
