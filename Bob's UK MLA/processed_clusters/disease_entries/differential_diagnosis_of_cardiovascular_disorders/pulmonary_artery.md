# Disease: PULMONARY ARTERY

**Source section:** DIFFERENTIAL DIAGNOSIS OF CARDIOVASCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

systemic pulmonary supply via aortic branches

## Imaging Findings

### General

- diminished flow on left side (hypoplastic / stenotic pulmonary artery in 40%)
- diminished / increased blood flow to either lung
- increased flow to left lung ← jet phenomenon
- poststenotic dilatation of trunk + left pulmonary artery
