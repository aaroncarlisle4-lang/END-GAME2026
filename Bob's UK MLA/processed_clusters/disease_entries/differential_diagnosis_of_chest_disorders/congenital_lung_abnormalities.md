# Disease: CONGENITAL LUNG ABNORMALITIES

**Source section:** DIFFERENTIAL DIAGNOSIS OF CHEST DISORDERS

## Definition

SEQUESTRATION SPECTRUM collective name for congenital underdevelopment of one / more lobes of a lung separated

## Key Facts

- **Incidence:** 1÷10,000; R÷L = 1÷1

## Imaging Findings

### CT

- missing bronchus + lobe(s)
- absence of ipsilateral pulmonary artery
- bronchus terminates in dilated blind pouch
- absence of ipsilateral pulmonary tissue
