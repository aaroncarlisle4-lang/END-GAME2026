## Key Facts

- **Incidence:** 150,000 annually in USA on CXR
- **Histo:** scar, pleural plaque, post-infarction fibrosis, intrapulmonary lymph node
- **Prevalence:** 20% in lung cancer screening trial in heavy smokers
