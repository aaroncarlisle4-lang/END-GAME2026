# Disease: BONE SCLEROSIS

**Source section:** DIAGNOSTIC GAMUT OF BONE DISORDERS

## Definition

transformation of mesenchymal cells into cortical bone without intervening cartilaginous endosteal new bone formation

## Imaging Findings

### General

- accumulation of calcified cartilage matrix packing the medullary cavity
- focal densities / striations along trabecular bone
