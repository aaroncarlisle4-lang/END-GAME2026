# Disease: CMV-INDUCED RETINITIS

**Source section:** ORBITAL AND OCULAR DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** in ⅓ of AIDS patents NOT on HAART (highly active antiretroviral therapy)
- **Location:** unilateral → bilateral

## Imaging Findings

### General

- uveal enhancement
- retinal detachment
- calcifications of retina

## Clinical

- **Cx:** blindness ← retinal detachment within 3-6 months (in 33% prior to HAART)
