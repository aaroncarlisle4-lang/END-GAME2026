# Disease: ADENOID CYSTIC CARCINOMA

**Source section:** ORBITAL AND OCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

most common malignancy of lacrimal gland

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 29% of all lacrimal epithelial neoplasms; ~ 5% of all primary orbital neoplasms
- **Age:** 4th decade of life
- **Histo:** nonencapsulated bland-appearing epithelial cells arranged in nests / chords forming a

## Imaging Findings

### General

- irregular borders with distortion of globe + orbital contents (with more advanced disease)
- ± bone erosion + calcifications
- perineural invasion (lacrimal branch of ophthalmic nerve)

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** poor; 20% 10-year-survival rate
