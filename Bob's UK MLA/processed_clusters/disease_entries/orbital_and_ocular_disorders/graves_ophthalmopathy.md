# Disease: GRAVES OPHTHALMOPATHY

**Source section:** ORBITAL AND OCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

THYROID OPHTHALMOPATHY = ENDOCRINE EXOPHTHALMOS autoimmune disease unrelated to thyroid function with an increase in orbital pressure

## Key Facts

- **Etiology:** produced by long-acting thyroid-stimulating factor (LATS); probably immunologic
- **Age:** adulthood; 5% younger than 15 years; M÷F = 1÷4
- **Histo:** deposition of hygroscopic mucopolysaccharides + glycoprotein (early) + collagen
