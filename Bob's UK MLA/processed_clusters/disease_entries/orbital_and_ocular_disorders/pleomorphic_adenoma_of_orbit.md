# Disease: PLEOMORPHIC ADENOMA OF ORBIT

**Source section:** ORBITAL AND OCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

BENIGN MIXED TUMOR

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 57% of epithelial lesions in lacrimal gland
- **Age:** 4th to 5th decade of life
- **Location:** superotemporal orbit

## Imaging Findings

### General

- well-circumscribed usually homogeneously enhancing mass
- larger lesion heterogeneous ← cystic degeneration, hemorrhage, serous / mucous collections,
- smooth concavity of lacrimal fossa ← bone remodeling
- T1-hypointense + T2-hyperintense mass
