# Disease: PHTHISIS BULBI

**Source section:** ORBITAL AND OCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

"wood eye" = shrunken nonfunctioning + usually calcified eye

## Imaging Findings

### US

- shrunken eye
- usually calcified wall
- ± hyperechoic fibrous tracts within vitreous + resultant retinal detachment
