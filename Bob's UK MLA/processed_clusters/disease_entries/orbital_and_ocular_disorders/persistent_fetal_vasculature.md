# Disease: PERSISTENT FETAL VASCULATURE

**Source section:** ORBITAL AND OCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

PERSISTENT HYPERPLASTIC PRIMARY VITREUS rare congenital nonhereditary condition with persistence + hyperplasia of fibrovascular important source of intraocular nutrition until 8th month of gestation; arises from dorsal fibrillar ectodermal meshwork + mesodermal tissue consisting of embryonic hyaloid

## Key Facts

- **Age:** noticed at birth / within first few weeks of life
- **Location:** unilateral

## Imaging Findings

### General

- microphthalmia = small hypoplastic globe (61-92%):
- normal-sized globe (13%)
- buphthalmos (in up to 26%)
- retinal detachment ← vitreoretinal traction in 30%
