# Disease: SUPERIOR OPHTHALMIC VEIN THROMBOSIS

**Source section:** ORBITAL AND OCULAR DISORDERS

## Imaging Findings

### General

- filling defect in superior ophthalmic vein
- enlargement of both superior ophthalmic veins + cavernous sinus
- periorbital edema
- engorgement of extraocular muscles
- exophthalmos

## Clinical

- **Cx:** vision loss, thrombosis of cavernous sinus, sepsis
- **Rx:** anticoagulation + antibiotics
