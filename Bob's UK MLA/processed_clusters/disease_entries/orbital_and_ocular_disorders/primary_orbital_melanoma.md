# Disease: PRIMARY ORBITAL MELANOMA

**Source section:** ORBITAL AND OCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

UVEAL MELANOMA = Choroidal Melanoma

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 5-7÷1,000,000
- **Mean age:** 56 (range, 50-70) years; in 65% > 50 years of age; Whites÷African Americans =
- **Path:** mushroom-shaped tumor with broad choroidal base / flat mass / crescentic mass; range

## Clinical

- **Cx:** retinal detachment and extrascleral spread
- **Rx:** plaque brachytherapy, external beam radiation, transpupillary thermotherapy, surgical
- **N.B.:** small nonprogressive melanoma < 3 mm thick can be followed every 3-6 months
- **Prognosis:** 5-year survival rate for (a) small < 3 mm thick choroidal melanoma 85%, (b)
