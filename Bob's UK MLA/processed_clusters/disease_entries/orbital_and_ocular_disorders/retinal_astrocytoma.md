# Disease: RETINAL ASTROCYTOMA

**Source section:** ORBITAL AND OCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

RETINAL ASTROCYTIC HAMARTOMA low-grade benign neoplasm / hamartoma arising from the nerve fiber layer of retina / optic

## Key Facts

- **Etiology:** tuberous sclerosis (53%); neurofibromatosis type 1 (14%); sporadic (33%)
- **Path:** usually multiple + bilateral in tuberous sclerosis;
- **Histo:** spindle-shaped fibrous astrocytes with small oval nuclei
- **Age:** congenital
- **Location:** retina near optic disc; bilateral in 25%

## Imaging Findings

### General

- retinal mass ± enhancement ± calcifications
- typically unilateral (DDx to drusen)

## Clinical

- **Cx:** (1)  Central retinal vein occlusion + secondary hemorrhage
- **Rx:** not necessary
