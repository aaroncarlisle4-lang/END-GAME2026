# Disease: BONE MARROW

**Source section:** MUSCULOSKELETAL INFECTION

## Definition

hematopoietically active (by EGA of 20 weeks) with a rich + extensive vascular supply, reversal of yellow to red marrow

## Key Facts

- **Distribution:** conversion from red to fatty marrow throughout life

## Imaging Findings
