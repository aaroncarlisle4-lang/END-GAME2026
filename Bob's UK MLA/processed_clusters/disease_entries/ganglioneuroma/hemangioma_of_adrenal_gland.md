# Disease: HEMANGIOMA OF ADRENAL GLAND

**Source section:** GANGLIONEUROMA

## Definition

rare benign vasoformative stromal tumor of adrenal gland

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 1÷10,000 autopsies
- **Histo:** cavernous > capillary type

## Imaging Findings

### General

- often large mass (usually > 10 cm in diameter, up to 22 cm) owing to their indolent nature
- highly vascular mass with multiple peripheral nodular areas of marked enhancement after
- NO complete fill in of contrast material
- calcifications (28-87%) from previous hemorrhage / multiple CHARACTERISTIC phleboliths
- persistent enhancement on delayed imaging

### CT

- central low attenuation (necrosis / fibrosis)

### MR

- mass hypointense relative to liver on T1WI + central hyperintensity ← hemorrhage /
- usually markedly hyperintense on T2WI, especially in central portion
- variable appearance after hemorrhage, thrombosis, necrosis, fibrosis

## Clinical

- **Cx:** hemorrhage
