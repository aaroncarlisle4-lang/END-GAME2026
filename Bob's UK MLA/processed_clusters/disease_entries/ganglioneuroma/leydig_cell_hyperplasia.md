# Disease: LEYDIG CELL HYPERPLASIA

**Source section:** GANGLIONEUROMA

## Definition

rare benign condition characterized by an increased number of Leydig cells

## Key Facts

- **Location:** frequently bilateral; multifocal
- **Size:** 1-6 mm

## Imaging Findings

### General

- hypo- / hyperechoic, T2-hypointense intratesticular nodule(s)
- variable vascularity ± avid enhancement
