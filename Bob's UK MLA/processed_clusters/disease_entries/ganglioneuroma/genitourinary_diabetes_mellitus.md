# Disease: GENITOURINARY DIABETES MELLITUS

**Source section:** GANGLIONEUROMA

## Definition

defined as persistent proteinuria (> 500 mg of albumin/24 hours) + retinopathy + elevated

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 35-45% of IDDM; < 20% of NIDDM; M > F
- **Histo:** diffuse intercapillary glomerulosclerosis

## Imaging Findings

### General

- renal enlargement (= renal hypertrophy with glomerular expansion)
- progressive decrease in size
- diffuse cortical hyperechogenicity with gradual loss of corticomedullary differentiation
- resistive index > 0.7 (very late)
- contrast material may induce renal failure (= rise in serum creatinine level 1-5 days after
- enlarged postvoid residual urine volume

## Clinical

- **Cx:** vesicoureteral reflux, recurrent pyelonephritis, pyohydronephrosis, overflow
