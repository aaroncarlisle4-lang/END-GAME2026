# Disease: INFLAMMATORY PSEUDOTUMOR OF BLADDER

**Source section:** GANGLIONEUROMA

## Definition

PSEUDOSARCOMATOUS FIBROMYXOID TUMOR

## Key Facts

- **Age:** 38 (range, 15-74) years; M÷F = 11÷6
- **Path:** ulcerating bleeding mass
- **Histo:** loosely packed spindle cells within myxoid matrix
- **Location:** reported in every organ of body

## Imaging Findings

### General

- locally aggressive single exophytic / polypoid 2-8 cm bladder mass ± ulceration
- may invade through bladder wall with extravesical component
- ringlike peripheral enhancement + poor central enhancement ← necrosis

### MR

- heterogeneous mass with central hyperintense component surrounded by hypointense

## Clinical

- **Rx:** surgery, high-dose steroids, radiation therapy
