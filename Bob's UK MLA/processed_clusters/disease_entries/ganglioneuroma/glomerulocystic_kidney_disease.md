# Disease: GLOMERULOCYSTIC KIDNEY DISEASE

**Source section:** GANGLIONEUROMA

## Definition

GCKD = rare form of cystic renal disease characterized by cystic dilatation of Bowman

## Key Facts

- **Genetics:** often inherited autosomal-dominant disease in families with ADPKD / component of
- **Age:** neonate, young child
- **Histo:** uniform cystic dilatation of Bowman capsule around glomerulus

## Imaging Findings

### General

- normal-sized / hypoplastic kidneys

### US

- small round cysts in renal cortex
- cortical hyperechogenicity ← if cysts difficult to define
- numerous T2-hyperintense lesions predominantly in subcapsular portion of renal cortex
