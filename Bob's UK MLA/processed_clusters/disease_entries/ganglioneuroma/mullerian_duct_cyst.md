# Disease: MÜLLERIAN DUCT CYST

**Source section:** GANGLIONEUROMA

## Definition

failed regression of paramesonephric (= müllerian) duct which usually regresses by 3rd fetal

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 4-5% of male newborns; in 1% of men
- **Peak age:** 20-40 years
- **Location:** arises from region of verumontanum slightly lateral to midline
- **Associated with:** typically NO other congenital anomalies; renal agenesis (rare)

## Imaging Findings

### General

- large teardrop-shaped intraprostatic cyst usually with extension superolaterally above
- aspirate contains serous / mucous clear brown / green fluid (hemorrhage + debris), NOT
- commonly contains calculi

### MR

- increased signal on T1WI ← hemorrhage, protein

## Clinical

- **Cx:** infection, hemorrhage, carcinomatous transformation
