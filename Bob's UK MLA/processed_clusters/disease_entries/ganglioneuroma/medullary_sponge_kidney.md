## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 1÷5,000 persons; in 0.5% of IVPs; in 12-20% of patients with calcified kidney
- **Age:** 3rd-4th decade
- **Genetics:** mostly sporadic, occasionally autosomal dominant
- **Histo:** ectasia of medullary + papillary collecting ducts with small < 1 cm cysts in medulla
