# Disease: LITHIUM-INDUCED NEPHROTOXICITY

**Source section:** GANGLIONEUROMA

## Imaging Findings

### General

- typically normal-sized kidneys
- abundant uniformly distributed renal microcysts
- 1-2 mm microcysts (in 33-62%)

### CT

- multiple tiny hypoattenuating lesions
- multiple T2-hyperintense foci

## Clinical

- **Rx:** reversible with drug cessation
