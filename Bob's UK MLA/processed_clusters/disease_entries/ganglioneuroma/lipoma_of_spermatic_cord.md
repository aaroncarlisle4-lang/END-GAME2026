# Disease: LIPOMA OF SPERMATIC CORD

**Source section:** GANGLIONEUROMA

## Definition

EXTRATESTICULAR / PARATESTICULAR LIPOMA

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** most common extratesticular tumor

## Imaging Findings

### US

- well-defined homogeneous scrotal mass
- NO internal flow on color Doppler

### MR

- homogeneous high SI on T1WI (DDx: hematoma, proteinaceous cyst)
- hyperintense paratesticular mass on T2WI
- india-ink = black boundary artifact and signal suppression on out-of-phase imaging
- signal suppression on fat-suppressed images
