# Disease: HEMOLYTIC-UREMIC SYNDROME

**Source section:** GANGLIONEUROMA

## Definition

characterized by microangiopathic hemolytic anemia with typical features of DIC

## Key Facts

- **Age:** usually children < 2 years
- **Histo:** microangiopathy including endothelial swelling + thrombus formation in glomerulus +

## Imaging Findings

### General

- kidneys of normal / slightly increased size
- hyperechoic cortex
- diastolic flow absent / reversed / reduced (= increase in resistance to flow)
- return to normal waveforms predates return of urine output

### SCINTIGRAPHY

- lack of renal perfusion

### Intestines:

- mural stratification + narrowing of bowel lumen
- pericolonic fat stranding

## Clinical

- **Cx:** perforation, intussusception, infectious colitis
- **Prognosis:** complete spontaneous recovery (in 85%)
