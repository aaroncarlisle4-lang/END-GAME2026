# Disease: CLASSIFICATION OF CNS ANOMALIES

**Source section:** TRIGEMINAL NEUROPATHY

## Definition

NEUROCUTANEOUS SYNDROMES = NEUROECTODERMAL DYSPLASIAS development of benign tumors / malformations in organs of ectodermal origin (CNS, eye,

## Imaging Findings

### General

- punctate / nodular calcifications
- porencephalic cysts
- occasionally microcephaly
- typically punctate / stippled / curvilinear periventricular calcifications
- often hydrocephalus
