# Disease: VASCULAR DISEASE OF BRAIN

**Source section:** TRIGEMINAL NEUROPATHY

## Definition

carotid (CA) + vertebral artery (VA) injuries during generalized multitrauma / direct inversion of the normal attenuation relationship between gray and white matter (gray matter Starfield pattern

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 1.1-1.6% of all blunt trauma
- **Location:** vessel origin, carotid bulb, cavernous carotid segment
