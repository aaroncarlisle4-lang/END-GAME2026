# Disease: RETAINED FETAL LUNG FLUID

**Source section:** PNEUMONECTOMY CHEST

## Definition

NEONATAL WET LUNG DISEASE = TRANSIENT RESPIRATORY DISTRESS OF THE NEWBORN =

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 6%; most common cause of respiratory distress in newborn

## Imaging Findings

### General

- linear opacities + perivascular haze + thickened fissures + interlobular septal thickening
- symmetric perihilar radiating congestion
- mild hyperaeration
- mild cardiomegaly
- small amount of pleural fluid

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** resolving within 1-2-4 days (retrospective Dx)
