# Disease: POSTOBSTRUCTIVE PNEUMONIA

**Source section:** PNEUMONECTOMY CHEST

## Definition

chronic inflammatory disease distal to bronchial obstruction; despite the name coexistent

## Imaging Findings

### General

- frequently associated with some degree of atelectasis
- persists unchanged for weeks
- recurrent pneumonia in same region after antibiotic Rx
