# Disease: TRACHEOBRONCHOPATHIA OSTEOCHONDROPLASTICA

**Source section:** PNEUMONECTOMY CHEST

## Definition

rare benign disease characterized by multiple submucosal cartilaginous / osseous nodules

## Key Facts

- **Path:** foci of submucosal hyaline cartilage with areas of lamellar bone
- **Histo:** adipose tissue + calcified areas with foci of bone marrow; thinned normal overlying

## Imaging Findings

### CXR

- scalloped / linear opacities surrounding + narrowing trachea (best on lateral view)

### CT

- deformed thickened narrowed tracheal wall
