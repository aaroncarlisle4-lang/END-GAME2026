# Disease: THORACIC DUCT CYST

**Source section:** PNEUMONECTOMY CHEST

## Definition

exceedingly rare benign mass of posterior mediastinum

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** cystic space lined by hyalinized fibrous connective tissue surrounded by patchy
- **Location:** anywhere along thoracic duct
- **Size:** 3-22 cm

## Imaging Findings

### General

- well-circumscribed posterior mediastinal mass
- contiguity with cisterna chyli
- enhancement of a smooth thin wall

### CT

- homogeneous well-defined mass of water attenuation

### MR

- variable SI on T1WI; mildly hyperintense ← increased lipids + proteins
- variously heterogeneous hyperintense mass on T2WI

## Clinical

- **Cx:** rapid enlargement / spontaneous rupture after high-fat meal
- **Rx:** resection under preservation of thoracic duct pedicles to avoid postoperative chylothorax
