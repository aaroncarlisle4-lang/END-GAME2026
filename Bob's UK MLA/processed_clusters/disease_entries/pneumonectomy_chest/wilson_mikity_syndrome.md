# Disease: WILSON-MIKITY SYNDROME

**Source section:** PNEUMONECTOMY CHEST

## Definition

PULMONARY DYSMATURITY similarity to bronchopulmonary dysplasia in normal preterm infants breathing room air; rarely

## Imaging Findings

### General

- hyperinflation
- reticular pattern radiating from both hila
- small bubbly lucencies throughout both lungs (identical to bronchopulmonary dysplasia)

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** resolution over 12 months
