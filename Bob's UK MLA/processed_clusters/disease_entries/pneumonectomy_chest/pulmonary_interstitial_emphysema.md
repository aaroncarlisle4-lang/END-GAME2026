# Disease: PULMONARY INTERSTITIAL EMPHYSEMA

**Source section:** PNEUMONECTOMY CHEST

## Definition

PIE = complication of respirator therapy with PEEP

## Key Facts

- **Location:** right parahilar region

## Imaging Findings

### General

- meandering tubular + cystic lucencies following distribution of bronchovascular tree
- bilateral symmetrical distribution
- pseudocysts (localized form of PIE) = multiple circular well-defined air collections with
- lobar overdistension (occasionally)
- microcardia

## Clinical

- **Cx:** (1)  pneumothorax (77%) ← rupture of cyst
- **Rx:** (1)  High-frequency jet / oscillatory ventilation
