# Disease: STREPTOCOCCAL PNEUMONIA

**Source section:** PNEUMONECTOMY CHEST

## Definition

Streptococcus pyogenes, Gram-positive cocci appearing in chains

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 1-5% of bacterial pneumonias (rarely seen); most common in winter months
- **Associated with:** delayed onset of diaphragmatic hernia (in newborns)

## Imaging Findings

### General

- patchy bronchopneumonia
- lower lobe predominance (similar to staphylococcus)
- empyema

## Clinical

- **Cx:** (1)  Residual pleural thickening (15%)
