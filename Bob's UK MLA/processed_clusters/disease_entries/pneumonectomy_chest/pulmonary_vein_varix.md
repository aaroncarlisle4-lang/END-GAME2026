# Disease: PULMONARY VEIN VARIX

**Source section:** PNEUMONECTOMY CHEST

## Definition

abnormal tortuosity + focal dilatation of pulmonary vein just before entrance into left atrium

## Key Facts

- **Etiology:** congenital / associated with chronic pulmonary venous hypertension or mitral valve
- **Location:** medial ⅓ of either lung below hila close to left atrium

## Imaging Findings

### General

- well-defined lobulated round / oval mass without Ca2+
- change in size during Valsalva / Müller maneuver
- opacification at same time as LA (on CECT)
