# Disease: THYMIC HYPERPLASIA

**Source section:** PNEUMONECTOMY CHEST

## Definition

increase in size + weight of thymus with preservation of its organized microscopic features increase in number of active lymphoid germinal centers in thymic medulla associated with

## Key Facts

- **Age:** commonly in child, also in adult

## Imaging Findings

### General

- size:
- diffuse enlargement
- > 50% transient overgrowth (reducible with steroids)
- after recovery regrowth to original size within 9 months
- shape:
- smooth contour, normal vessels
- normal / oval shape with loss of its distinct bilobed appearance
- fine mixture of fat and lymphoid tissue

### MR

- homogeneously decreased signal on out-of-phase images ← fatty infiltration of normal /
- may take up FDG mimicking thymoma
- diffuse symmetric enlargement / normal size
