# Disease: MINERALIZING MICROANGIOPATHY

**Source section:** INTRAVENTRICULAR NEUROCYTOMA

## Definition

RADIATION-INDUCED LEUKOENCEPHALOPATHY sequelae of radiotherapy combined with methotrexate therapy for leukemia radiation-induced demyelination of periventricular white matter

## Key Facts

- **Incidence:** in 25-30% after > 9 months after treatment
- **Age:** childhood

## Imaging Findings

### CT

- thin reticular / serrated linear / punctate calcifications near corticomedullary junction,
- symmetric low-attenuation process in white matter near corticomedullary area
