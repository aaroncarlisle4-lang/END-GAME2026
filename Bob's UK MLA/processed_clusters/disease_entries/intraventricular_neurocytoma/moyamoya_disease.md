# Disease: MOYAMOYA DISEASE

**Source section:** INTRAVENTRICULAR NEUROCYTOMA

## Definition

uncommon occlusive cerebral arteritis classically involving supraclinoid internal carotid underlying cause for moyamoya image identified

## Key Facts

- **Etiology:** unknown; NO underlying cause identified
- **Age:** bimodal distribution in 1st decade + 4th decade of life
- **Path:** development of extensive tiny basal perforator and transdural collateral vessels
- **Histo:** endothelial hyperplasia + fibrosis without associated inflammatory reaction
