# Disease: METACHROMATIC LEUKODYSTROPHY

**Source section:** INTRAVENTRICULAR NEUROCYTOMA

## Definition

MLD = most common hereditary (autosomal recessive) leukodystrophy (dysmyelinating

## Key Facts

- **Age:** 2nd year of life

## Imaging Findings

### General

- progressive loss of hemispheric brain tissue

### CT

- symmetric low density of white matter adjacent to ventricles (esp. centrum ovale and
- progressive atrophy
- no contrast enhancement

### MR

- progressive symmetrical areas of hypointensity on T1WI
- hyperintensity on T2WI (increased water)
- early sparing of internal capsule + subcortical U-fibers

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** death within 4 years of onset
