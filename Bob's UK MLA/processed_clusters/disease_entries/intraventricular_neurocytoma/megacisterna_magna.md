# Disease: MEGACISTERNA MAGNA

**Source section:** INTRAVENTRICULAR NEUROCYTOMA

## Definition

normal variant of focal enlargement of subarachnoid space in posteroinferior aspect of

## Imaging Findings

### General

- cisterna magna ≥ 10 mm (on midsagittal image)
- free communication with 4th ventricle + cervical subarachnoid space (confirmed with CSF
- intact vermis + normal 4th ventricle
- absence of hydrocephalus
- occasionally enlarged posterior fossa
