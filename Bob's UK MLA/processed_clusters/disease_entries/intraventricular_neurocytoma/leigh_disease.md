# Disease: LEIGH DISEASE

**Source section:** INTRAVENTRICULAR NEUROCYTOMA

## Definition

SUBACUTE NECROTIZING ENCEPHALOMYELOPATHY autosomal recessive disorder characterized by deficiencies in pyruvate carboxylase +

## Key Facts

- **Location:** putamen (consistently), periaqueductal region, cerebral peduncles

## Imaging Findings

### General

- T2 prolongation of affected areas
- abnormally high lactate levels on MR spectroscopy
