# Disease: ISOLATED VERMIAN HYPOPLASIA

**Source section:** INTRAVENTRICULAR NEUROCYTOMA

## Definition

DANDY-WALKER VARIANT (nonpreferred term)

## Key Facts

- **Genetics:** usually no recurrence risk

## Imaging Findings

### General

- partial absence of inferior vermis (best on midsagittal image)
- remainder of vermis + cerebellar hemispheres + 4th ventricle + posterior fossa of normal size

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** favorable outcome in 75%
