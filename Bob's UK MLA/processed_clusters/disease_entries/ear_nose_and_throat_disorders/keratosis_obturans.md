# Disease: KERATOSIS OBTURANS

**Source section:** EAR, NOSE, AND THROAT DISORDERS

## Definition

expansile accumulation of keratin debris within EAC

## Key Facts

- **Age:** younger patient < 40 years
- **Associated with:** chronic sinusitis, bronchiectasis
- **Location:** usually bilateral process

## Imaging Findings

### General

- diffuse widening of EAC by epidermal plug
- ± smooth scalloping of surrounding bone
