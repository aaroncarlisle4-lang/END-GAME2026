# Disease: GRAVES DISEASE

**Source section:** EAR, NOSE, AND THROAT DISORDERS

## Definition

DIFFUSE TOXIC GOITER autoimmune disorder antibodies binding to TSH receptors (LATS) with inhibitory +

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 5÷10,000; most common form of hyperthyroidism (60-80%)
- **Peak age:** 3rd-4th decade; M÷F = 1÷7

## Imaging Findings

### General

- diffuse thyroid enlargement
- gland may be normal in size in early stage
- uniformly increased uptake
- incidental nodules superimposed on preexisting adenomatous goiter (5%)

### US

- global enlargement of 2-3 times the normal size
- normal / diffusely hypoechoic pattern
- "thyroid inferno" = diffuse hyperemia on color Doppler

## Clinical

- **Rx:** 131I treatments (for adults):
- **Cx:** 10-30% develop hypothyroidism within 1st year + 3% per year rate thereafter
