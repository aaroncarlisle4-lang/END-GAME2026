# Disease: ANGIOLIPOMA OF PAROTID GLAND

**Source section:** EAR, NOSE, AND THROAT DISORDERS

## Definition

benign nodular lesion similar to ordinary lipomas except for associated angiomatous

## Key Facts

- **Age:** rare before puberty

## Imaging Findings

### CT

- circumscribed (more common) / infiltrating mass
- marked enhancement around fatty components
