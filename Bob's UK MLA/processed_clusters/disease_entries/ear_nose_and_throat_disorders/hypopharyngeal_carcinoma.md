# Disease: HYPOPHARYNGEAL CARCINOMA

**Source section:** EAR, NOSE, AND THROAT DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** squamous cell carcinoma (95%)

## Imaging Findings

### General

- invasion of posterior ala of thyroid cartilage, cricothyroid space, soft tissue of neck in T4
- difficult assessment due to varying thickness of inferior constrictor + prevertebral muscles
- invasion of retropharyngeal space with extension into oro- and nasopharynx
- retropharyngeal adenopathy

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** poor ← early soft-tissue invasion
