# Disease: LARYNGEAL CHONDROSARCOMA

**Source section:** EAR, NOSE, AND THROAT DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Age:** 50-70 years; M >> F

## Imaging Findings

### General

- coarse / stippled intratumoral calcifications
- ± locally invasive

### MR

- very high SI of tumor matrix on T2WI (= hyaline cartilage)

## Clinical

- **Rx:** function-preserving laryngeal resection (local recurrence may be seen 10 years or more)
