# Disease: LARYNGEAL HEMANGIOMA

**Source section:** EAR, NOSE, AND THROAT DISORDERS

## Definition

SUBGLOTTIC HEMANGIOMA

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** cavernous / capillary type
- **Age:** < 6 months; M÷F = 1÷2
- **Location:** subglottic region

## Imaging Findings

### General

- strong contrast enhancement

### CT

- phleboliths (PATHOGNOMONIC for cavernous type)

### MR

- very high signal intensity on T2WI
- posterolateral subglottic airway narrowing (AP view)
- eccentric thickening of subglottic portion of trachea (AP view)
- subtle endoluminal mass arising from posterior wall below true cords (LAT view)

## Clinical

- **N.B.:** CECT required to depict full extent
- **Rx:** tracheostomy (waiting for spontaneous regression)
