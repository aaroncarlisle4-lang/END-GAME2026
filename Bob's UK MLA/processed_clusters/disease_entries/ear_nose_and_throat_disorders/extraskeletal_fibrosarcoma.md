# Disease: EXTRASKELETAL FIBROSARCOMA

**Source section:** EAR, NOSE, AND THROAT DISORDERS

## Definition

malignant tumor of fibroblasts of classic herringbone-like architecture

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 1-3% of sarcomas in adults; 11-12% of sarcomas in infants
- **Age:** 40 -70 years; M÷F = 3÷2
- **Location:** sinonasal cavity, larynx, neck

## Imaging Findings

### MR

- heterogeneous mass of predominantly low signal intensity with variable enhancement on
- heterogeneous high signal intensity on T2WI

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** infantile extraskeletal fibrosarcoma better than adult fibrosarcoma
