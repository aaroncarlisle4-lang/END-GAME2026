**Source section:** EAR, NOSE, AND THROAT DISORDERS

## Definition

severity of stenosis is primarily graded as a ratio of lumen diameter narrowing NOT

## Key Facts

- **Age:** beginning at birth slowly progressing to adult life
- **Location:** large + medium-sized elastic and muscular arteries
- **Histo:** deposition of fatty streaks + fibrous tissue; rarely shows intraplaque
