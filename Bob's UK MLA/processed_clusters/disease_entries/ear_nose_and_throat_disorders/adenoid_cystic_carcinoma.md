# Disease: ADENOID CYSTIC CARCINOMA

**Source section:** EAR, NOSE, AND THROAT DISORDERS

## Definition

CYLINDROMA

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 4-15% of salivary gland cancers; 1-3% of all head & neck malignancies
- **Histo:** (a) cribriform subtype, grade 1
- **Age:** 3rd-9th decade; maximum between 40 and 70 years; M=F
- **Location:** subglottis at junction with trachea (80%)

## Imaging Findings

### MR

- hypo- to hyperintense (high signal corresponds to low cellularity with a better prognosis)
- adenoid cystic carcinomas have the highest relative incidence of perineural invasion (up to 56%)
- extensive submucosal tumor spread of entire larynx
- invasion of cricoid cartilage, thyroid + esophagus
- regional neck nodes hardly ever involved

### Parotid gland (2-6% of tumors in this gland; arises from peripheral parotid ducts with

- infiltrating parotid mass

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** slow growing but relentless malignant course with repeat recurrences; the greater
- **Rx:** repeat surgical excision + radiation therapy
