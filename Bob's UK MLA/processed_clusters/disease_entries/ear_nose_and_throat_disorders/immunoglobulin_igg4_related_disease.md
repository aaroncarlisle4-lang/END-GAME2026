# Disease: IMMUNOGLOBULIN IGG4-RELATED DISEASE

**Source section:** EAR, NOSE, AND THROAT DISORDERS

## Definition

IGG4-RELATED SCLEROSING DISEASE (ISD) = HYPER-IGG4 DISEASE = systemic disease with multiorgan involvement and elevated IgG4 levels

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** diffuse lymphoplasmacytic infiltration (by T-lymphocytes and plasma cells staining

## Imaging Findings

### CT

- intermediate soft-tissue attenuation

### MR

- low T1 signal intensity (= isointense to muscle)
- homogeneous low to intermediate SI on T2WI ← increased cellularity + fibrosis
