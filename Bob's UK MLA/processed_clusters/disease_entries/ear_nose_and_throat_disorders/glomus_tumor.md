# Disease: GLOMUS TUMOR

**Source section:** EAR, NOSE, AND THROAT DISORDERS

## Definition

composed of cells closely resembling modified smooth muscle cells of glomus bodies of GLOMANGIOPERICYTOMA = SINONASAL-TYPE HEMANGIOPERICYTOMA

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** < 2% of all soft tissue tumors
- **Location:** distal extremities, head & neck (15-30%)
- **Path:** well-delineated unencapsulated cellular tumors
- **Histo:** closely packed cells (= high cellularity) forming short fascicles, sometimes exhibiting
- **Peak age:** 7th decade; M = F

- **Prognosis:** low tendency for metastasis (5%)
