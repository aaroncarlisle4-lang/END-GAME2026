# Disease: COGAN SYNDROME

**Source section:** EAR, NOSE, AND THROAT DISORDERS

## Definition

AUTOIMMUNE INTERSTITIAL KERATITIS rare autoimmune multisystem disease characterized by nonsyphilitic interstitial keratitis +

## Key Facts

- **Age:** young white adult

## Imaging Findings

### General

- aortitis → aortic insufficiency
- necrotizing vasculitis → coronary / iliac / renal artery stenosis

### MR

- membranous labyrinthine obliteration / narrowing
- enhancement of membranous labyrinth

### CNS

- ischemic change or infarction, meningoencephalitis, cerebral venous sinus thrombosis,
