# Disease: LARYNGEAL CYST

**Source section:** EAR, NOSE, AND THROAT DISORDERS

## Definition

rare benign pharyngeal cyst of vallecula / vocal cord / epiglottis

## Key Facts

- **Age:** any; most common in 6th decade

## Imaging Findings

### General

- smoothly hemispheric mass ± distortion of coated surfaces of pharyngeal / laryngeal
