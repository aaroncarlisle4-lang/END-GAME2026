# Disease: APICAL PETROSITIS

**Source section:** EAR, NOSE, AND THROAT DISORDERS

## Definition

PETROUS APICITIS = PETROSITIS infectious process caused by medial extension of acute otitis media into pneumatized petrous

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** chronic > acute apicitis
- **Etiology:** spread from middle ear + mastoid infection; requires presence of air cells in petrous

## Imaging Findings

### General

- opacification of aerated petrous apex + air-fluid levels
- osseous erosion
- fluid in ipsilateral middle ear + mastoid

### MR

- hyperintense signal on T2WI + hypointense on T1WI
- avidly enhancing mass in pneumatized petrous apex
- enhancing meninges ← adjacent meningeal inflammation

## Clinical

- **Cx:** meningitis; epidural / brain abscess (ring enhancement, restricted DWI); venous sinus
- **Rx:** intravenous antibiotics, myringotomy, surgery
