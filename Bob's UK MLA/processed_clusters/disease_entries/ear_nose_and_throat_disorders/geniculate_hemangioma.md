# Disease: GENICULATE HEMANGIOMA

**Source section:** EAR, NOSE, AND THROAT DISORDERS

## Definition

venous malformation in region of facial nerve

## Imaging Findings

### CT

- expansile honeycomb appearance
- intratumoral bone spicules
- bone forming / growth among bone trabeculae (= ossifying hemangioma)

### MR

- mass of heterogeneous signal intensity
- avid enhancement
