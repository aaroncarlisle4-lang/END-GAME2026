# Disease: LINGUAL THYROID

**Source section:** EAR, NOSE, AND THROAT DISORDERS

## Definition

solid embryonic rest of thyroid tissue, which remains ectopic along the tract of thyroglossal

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** in 10% of autopsies (within tongue < 3 mm); M << F

## Imaging Findings

### CT

- small hyperattenuating focus relative to muscle ← iodine content + moderate contrast

### MR

- solid avidly enhancing lesion mildly T1 hyper- / isointense relative to muscle

### NUC

- SPECIFIC high uptake of 123I and 99mTc

## Clinical

- **N.B.:** resection of ectopic thyroid will render patient athyroid
- **Cx:** in 3% malignant (papillary carcinoma) / goitrous transformation
