# Disease: CARDIAC VEINS

**Source section:** LYMPH FLOW DISORDERS

## Definition

distal portion of great cardiac vein

## Key Facts

- **Location:** within 10 mm of each other
- **Size:** 45 mm long; 10-12 mm in diameter
- **Prevalence:** 95%; single v. (60%), up to 3 veins

## Imaging Findings

### beginning of coronary sinus

- kink in vein → luminal obstruction of great cardiac v.
