# Disease: HUMAN CHORIONIC GONADOTROPIN

**Source section:** DIFFERENTIAL DIAGNOSIS OF OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC

## Definition

hCG = glycoprotein elaborated by placental trophoblastic cells beginning the 8th day after indirect agglutination test for hCG in urine; cross-reaction with other hormones / measures beta subunit of hCG in serum with a sensitivity as low as 1-2 mIU/mL
