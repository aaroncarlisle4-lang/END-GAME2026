# Disease: PELVIC PAIN

**Source section:** DIFFERENTIAL DIAGNOSIS OF OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC

## Definition

nonmenstrual pain of > 6 months duration

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 15% in women between 18 and 50 years; 10-40% of all gynecologic outpatient
- **Age:** typically < 35 years; Whites > Blacks
