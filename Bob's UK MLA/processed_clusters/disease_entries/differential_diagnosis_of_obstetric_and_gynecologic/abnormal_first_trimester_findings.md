# Disease: ABNORMAL FIRST TRIMESTER FINDINGS

**Source section:** DIFFERENTIAL DIAGNOSIS OF OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC

## Definition

VAGINAL BLEEDING IN FIRST TRIMESTER hemorrhage in cul-de-sac (= rectouterine pouch) normal pelvic US = transient state of early pregnancy during which no definite IUP is positive β-hCG without IUP

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 15-25% of all pregnancies, of which 50% terminate in abortion

## Imaging Findings

### General

- no placental blood flow
- synechia often thins during uterine stretching + disappears as pregnancy progresses
