# Disease: FETAL TUMOR

**Source section:** DIFFERENTIAL DIAGNOSIS OF OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC

## Definition

CONGENITAL TUMOR mass in fetus + newborn manifesting in first 3 months of life

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 1.7-13.5÷100,000 live births
- **Location:** typically along midline of body
- **Path:** all 3 germ cell layers:
- **Histo:** (a)  mature
- **Age:** first 4-6 weeks of infancy
- **Associated with:** congenital leukemia of Down syndrome (15-fold increase of leukemia)

## Imaging Findings

### General

- macrocephaly, hydrocephalus, polyhydramnios ← depressed swallowing ←
- hepatosplenomegaly → ↑ abdominal circumference
- hydrops ← fetal anemia / leukemic infiltrate of myocardium / visceral fibrosis with ↑

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** poor ← rapidly progressive course
