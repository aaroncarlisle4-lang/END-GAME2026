# Disease: ASSESSMENT OF FETAL WELL-BEING

**Source section:** DIFFERENTIAL DIAGNOSIS OF OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC

## Definition

sum of vertical depths of largest clear amniotic fluid pockets in the 4 uterine quadrants in utero Apgar score = assessment of fetal well-being external monitoring after injection of oxytocin / maternal breast stimulation

- reactive fetal heart rate tracing (normal) = at least 4 fetal heart accelerations (> 15 bpm

## Clinical

- **N.B.:** no heart accelerations in immaturity, during sleep cycle, with maternal sedative use
