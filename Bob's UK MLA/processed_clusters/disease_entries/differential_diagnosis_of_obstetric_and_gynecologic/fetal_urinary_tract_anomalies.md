# Disease: FETAL URINARY TRACT ANOMALIES

**Source section:** DIFFERENTIAL DIAGNOSIS OF OBSTETRIC AND GYNECOLOGIC

## Definition

AP diameter of renal pelvis > 5 mm at 15-20 weeks, ≥ 8 mm at 20-30 weeks, ≥ 10 mm at >

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 0.25%-1.00% liveborn infants (OB-US); 1÷100 to 1÷200 neonates (pediatrics)

## Imaging Findings

### General

- bladder volume: 1 mL at 20 weeks MA; 36 mL at 40 weeks MA
- filling + emptying of fetal urinary bladder occurs every 10-30 (range 7-43) minutes
- increased renal parenchymal echogenicity indicates renal abnormality in 80%
- fetal hydronephrosis
