# Disease: CRANIOVERTEBRAL JUNCTION

**Source section:** DIFFERENTIAL DIAGNOSIS OF SKULL AND SPINE DISORDERS

## Definition

congenital primary developmental anomaly with abnormally high position of vertebral acquired form of basilar invagination with bulging of C-spine and foramen magnum into anthropometric term referring to flattening of skull base

## Key Facts

- **Associated with:** Chiari malformation, syringohydromyelia in 25-35%
