# Disease: BIOMECHANICS OF SPINAL COLUMN

**Source section:** DIFFERENTIAL DIAGNOSIS OF SKULL AND SPINE DISORDERS

## Definition

primary structural support of human body (Francis Denis, 1983) vertebra retaining partial features of segments below and above; total number of vertebrae

## Key Facts

- **Incidence:** 3-21% of population
- **Location:** thoracolumbar (4%) + lumbosacral (15%) junction
