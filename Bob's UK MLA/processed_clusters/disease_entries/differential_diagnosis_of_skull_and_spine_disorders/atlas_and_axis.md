# Disease: ATLAS AND AXIS

**Source section:** DIFFERENTIAL DIAGNOSIS OF SKULL AND SPINE DISORDERS

## Definition

displacement of atlas with respect to axis ligamentous laxity in infants allows for movement of the vertebral bodies on each other,

## Key Facts

- **Location:** midline (97%); lateral through sulcus of vertebral artery (3%)

## Imaging Findings

### General

- absence of arch-canal line (LAT view)
- superimposed on odontoid process / axis body simulating a fracture (open-mouth
- plump rounded anterior arch overlapping the odontoid process making
- duplicated anterior margins (LAT view)
- unfused odontoid process > 12 years of age
- absence of odontoid process
- anterior arch of atlas hypertrophic + situated too far posterior in relation to axis body
- dislocation 8-10 days after onset of symptoms

## Clinical

- **Cx:** atlantoaxial instability
