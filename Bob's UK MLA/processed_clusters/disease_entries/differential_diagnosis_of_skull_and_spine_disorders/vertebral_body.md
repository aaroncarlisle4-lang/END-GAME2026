# Disease: VERTEBRAL BODY

**Source section:** DIFFERENTIAL DIAGNOSIS OF SKULL AND SPINE DISORDERS

## Definition

mostly benign equal to / lower than SI of muscle NEUROFORAMINAL WIDENING = DUMBBELL-SHAPED / HOURGLASS LESION normal small steplike recess at corner of anterior edge of developing vertebral body that "ghost vertebra" following stressful event during vertebral growth phase in childhood increase in opacity of vertebral body retaining its size and contours

## Key Facts

- **Location:** cervical spine
- **Associated with:** ankylosing spondylitis, ochronosis
