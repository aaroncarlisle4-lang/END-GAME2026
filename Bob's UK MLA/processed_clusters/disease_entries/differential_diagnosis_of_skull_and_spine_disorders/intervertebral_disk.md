# Disease: INTERVERTEBRAL DISK

**Source section:** DIFFERENTIAL DIAGNOSIS OF SKULL AND SPINE DISORDERS

## Definition

gaslike density due to liberation of nitrogen gas from surrounding tissues into clefts with an abnormal nucleus

## Key Facts

- **Location:** joint under traction, spine
- **Prevalence:** in 1-3% of all spinal radiographs; in up to 20% of plain radiographs; in up
- **Incidence:** 9% (on CT)
- **Associated with:** fusion of vertebral bodies

## Imaging Findings

### General

- acute protraction of shoulder in children
- common in degenerative disk of spine
- accentuated on supine X-ray during spine extension
- obscured on upright radiograph during spine flexion
