# Disease: BLAKE POUCH CYST

**Source section:** NERVOUS SYSTEM DISORDERS

## Definition

embryonic midline outpouching of superior medullary velum extending inferior + posterior to

## Key Facts

- **Age:** neonate

## Imaging Findings

### General

- retrocerebellar / infraretrocerebellar cyst = diverticulum of enlarged 4th ventricle
- enhancing structure along anterosuperior aspect of cyst inferior to vermis = displaced choroid
- mild indentation of inferior vermis / caudomedial aspects of cerebellar hemispheres ← mass
- tetraventricular hydrocephalus
- NO supratentorial morphologic abnormalities aside from hydrocephalus
- posterior fossa + cerebellum of normal size + shape

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** favorable (shunt-related complications possible)
