# Disease: CAPILLARY TELANGIECTASIA

**Source section:** NERVOUS SYSTEM DISORDERS

## Definition

CAPILLARY ANGIOMA nest of dilated capillaries separated by normal neural tissue; commonly "cryptic"

## Key Facts

- **Age:** typically in elderly

## Imaging Findings

### General

- poorly defined area of dilated vessels (resembling petechiae)
- best delineated with MR (due to hemorrhage) with focus of increased signal intensity on

## Clinical

- **Cx:** punctate hemorrhage (uncommon); gliosis + calcifications (rare)
- **Prognosis:** bleeding in pons (usually fatal)
