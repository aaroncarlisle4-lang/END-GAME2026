# Disease: BRAIN / CEREBRAL DEATH

**Source section:** NERVOUS SYSTEM DISORDERS

## Definition

1st-line test for cerebral perfusion; can be performed at the ICU bed site

## Key Facts

- **Path:** severe brain edema, diffuse liquefactive necrosis

## Imaging Findings

### General

- activity stops abruptly at the skull base = lack of activity in distribution of anterior and
- NO / faint activity in SSS on static image with 99mTc pentetic acid
- absence of activity in cerebrum on delayed images with 99mTc bicisate / 99mTc
- sagittal sinus not visualized
- common carotid arteries must be clearly visualized on 1st pass which confirms a good
- activity in face ("hot nose" sign) / scalp must not be mistaken as focal brain activity
