# Disease: AGENESIS OF CORPUS CALLOSUM

**Source section:** NERVOUS SYSTEM DISORDERS

## Definition

COMPLETE DYSGENESIS OF CORPUS CALLOSUM failure of formation of corpus callosum originating at lamina terminalis at 7-13 weeks from milder form of callosal dysgenesis (best seen on MR) depending on time of arrested growth

## Key Facts

- **Incidence:** 0.7-5.3%
- **Histo:** axons from cerebral hemispheres that would normally cross continue along medial
