# Disease: CANAVAN DISEASE

**Source section:** NERVOUS SYSTEM DISORDERS

## Definition

SPONGIFORM LEUKODYSTROPHY rare form of leukodystrophy as an autosomal recessive disorder, most common in Ashkenazi

## Key Facts

- **Incidence:** < 100 reported cases
- **Histo:** spongy degeneration of white matter with astrocytic swelling + mitochondrial
- **Age:** 3-6 months

## Imaging Findings

### General

- diffuse symmetric white matter abnormality
- may involve basal ganglia
- cortical atrophy

### CT

- low-density white matter

### MR

- white matter hypointense on T1WI + hyperintense on T2WI

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** death in 2nd-5th year of life
