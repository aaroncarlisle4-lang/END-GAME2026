# Disease: PAGET DISEASE

**Source section:** NEUROPATHIC OSTEOARTHROPATHY

## Definition

OSTEITIS DEFORMANS multifocal chronic skeletal disease characterized by disordered and exaggerated bone

## Key Facts

- **Etiology:** ? chronic paramyxoviral infection
- **Prevalence:** 3% of individuals > 40 years; 10% of persons > 80 years; higher prevalence in
- **Age:** Caucasian of Northern European descent > 55 years (in 3%); > 85 years (in 10%);
- **Histo:** increased resorption + increased bone formation; newly formed bone is abnormally soft
- **Location:** polyostotic in axial skeleton: skull, pelvis, spine
- **Path:** loss of excessive vascularity

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** < 10% 5-year survival
- **Cx:** sarcomatous degeneration: most commonly osteo- / chondrosarcoma / malignant fibrous
- **Rx:** calcitonin, biphosphonates, mithramycin
