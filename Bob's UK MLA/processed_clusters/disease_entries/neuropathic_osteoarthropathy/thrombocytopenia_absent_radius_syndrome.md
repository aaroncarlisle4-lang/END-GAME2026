# Disease: THROMBOCYTOPENIA-ABSENT RADIUS SYNDROME

**Source section:** NEUROPATHIC OSTEOARTHROPATHY

## Definition

TAR SYNDROME rare autosomal recessive disorder

## Imaging Findings

### General

- usually bilateral radial aplasia / hypoplasia
- uni- / bilaterally hypoplastic / absent ulna / humerus
- defects of hands, feet, legs

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** death in 50% in early infancy ← hemorrhage
