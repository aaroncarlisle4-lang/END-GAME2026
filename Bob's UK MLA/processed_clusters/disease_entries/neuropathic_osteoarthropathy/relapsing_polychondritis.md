# Disease: RELAPSING POLYCHONDRITIS

**Source section:** NEUROPATHIC OSTEOARTHROPATHY

## Definition

rare debilitating multisystem inflammatory + autoimmune disorder characterized by recurrent

## Key Facts

- **Etiology:** acquired metabolic disorder (? abnormal acid mucopolysaccharide metabolism) /
- **Histo:** loss of cytoplasm in chondrocytes; plasma cell and lymphocyte infiltration
- **Age:** 40-60 years (no age predilection)

## Imaging Findings

### General

- calcification of pinna of ear

### Chest

- manubriosternal / costochondral arthropathy (30%)

### Respiratory tract (in up to 70%)

- ectasia + collapsibility (← cartilaginous destruction) of trachea and mainstem bronchi
- bronchiectasis
- generalized + localized emphysema

### Cardiovascular (in 15-46%)

- aortic aneurysm (4-10%), mostly in ascending aorta, may be multiple / dissecting
- aortic / mitral valve insufficiency (8%)
- aortitis + systemic vasculitis (13%)
- obliterating vasculitis in medium-sized + large arteries

### Bone

- periarticular osteoporosis
- erosive changes in carpal bones resembling rheumatoid arthritis
- soft-tissue swelling around joints + styloid process of ulna
- erosive irregularities in sacroiliac joints
- disk space erosion + increased density of articular plates

## Clinical

- **Rx:** corticosteroids, immunosuppression
- **Prognosis:** 74% 5-year survival rate; 55% 10-year survival rate; median survival time of 11
