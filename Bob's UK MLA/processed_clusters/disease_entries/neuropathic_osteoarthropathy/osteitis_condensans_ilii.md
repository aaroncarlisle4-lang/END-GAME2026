# Disease: OSTEITIS CONDENSANS ILII

**Source section:** NEUROPATHIC OSTEOARTHROPATHY

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 2% of population
- **Age:** young multiparous women

## Imaging Findings

### General

- triangular area of sclerosis along inferior anterior aspect of ileum adjacent to SI joint (joint
- similar triangle of reparative bone on sacral side
- usually bilateral + symmetric; occasionally unilateral
- sclerosis dissolves in 3-20 years following stabilization of pubic symphysis
