# Disease: SHIN SPLINTS

**Source section:** NEUROPATHIC OSTEOARTHROPATHY

## Definition

SHIN SORENESS = MEDIAL TIBIAL STRESS SYNDROME = SOLEUS SYNDROME nonspecific term describing exertional lower leg pain

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 75% of exertional leg pain
- **Location:** posterior / posteromedial tibial cortex

## Imaging Findings

### General

- normal / longitudinal periosteal new bone
- normal radionuclide angiogram + blood-pool phase (DDx to stress fracture)
- linear longitudinal uptake on delayed images

### MR

- marrow edema / hemorrhage
- periosteal fluid
