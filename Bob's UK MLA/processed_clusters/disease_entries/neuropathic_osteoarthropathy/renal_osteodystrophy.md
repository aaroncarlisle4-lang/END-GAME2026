## Key Facts

- **Location:** most apparent in areas of rapid growth such as knee joints
- **Age:** < 1 year
- **Etiology:** renal tubular defect
- **Prevalence:** 1-2% of world's population
- **Peak age:** 45-65 years; M÷F = 1÷3 if < 40 years; M÷F = 1÷1 if > 40 years
