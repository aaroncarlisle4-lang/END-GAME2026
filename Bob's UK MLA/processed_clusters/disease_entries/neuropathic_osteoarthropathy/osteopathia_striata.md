# Disease: OSTEOPATHIA STRIATA

**Source section:** NEUROPATHIC OSTEOARTHROPATHY

## Definition

VOORHOEVE DISEASE disorder of secondary spongiosa of X-linked dominant inheritance / sporadic occurrence

## Key Facts

- **Genetics:** unknown
- **Location:** all long bones symmetrically affected

## Imaging Findings

### General

- dense linear longitudinal striations in diaphyses + metaphysis of long tubular bones: typically
- radiating fan-shaped densities of "sunburst" appearance from acetabulum into ileum
