# Disease: ORODIGITOFACIAL SYNDROME

**Source section:** NEUROPATHIC OSTEOARTHROPATHY

## Definition

OROFACIODIGITAL SYNDROME heterogeneous group of defects, probably representing varying expressivity, involving face,

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 1÷50,000 live births
- **Etiology:** autosomal trisomy of chromosome No. 1 with 47 chromosomes; X-linked dominant
- **Sex:** nuclear chromatin pattern female (lethal in male)
- **Associated with:** renal polycystic disease

## Imaging Findings

### General

- cleft in palate + jaw bone
- hypoplasia of mandible (micrognathia) + occiput of skull
- hypodontia
- clinodactyly, syndactyly, brachydactyly (metacarpals may be elongated), polysyndactyly,
