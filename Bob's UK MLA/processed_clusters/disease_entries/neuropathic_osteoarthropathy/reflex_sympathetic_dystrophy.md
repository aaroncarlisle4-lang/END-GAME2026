# Disease: REFLEX SYMPATHETIC DYSTROPHY

**Source section:** NEUROPATHIC OSTEOARTHROPATHY

## Definition

CAUSALGIA = SHOULDER-HAND SYNDROME = POSTTRAUMATIC OSTEOPOROSIS SUDECK DYSTROPHY serious + potentially disabling condition with poorly understood origin + cause

## Key Facts

- **Location:** hands and feet distal to injury

## Imaging Findings

### General

- periarticular soft-tissue swelling
- patchy osteopenia (50%) as early as 2-3 weeks after onset of symptoms (DDx: disuse
- generalized osteopenia = ground-glass appearance with endosteal + intracortical excavation:
- subperiosteal bone resorption
- lysis of juxtaarticular + subchondral bone
- preservation of joint space (DDx: rheumatoid / septic arthritis)
- ↑ flow + ↑ blood pool + ↑ in periarticular uptake on delayed images in affected part
- ↓ flow / delayed uptake (15-20%)

## Clinical

- **Rx:** sympathetic block, α- / β-adrenergic blocking agents, nonsteroidal antiinflammatory drugs,
