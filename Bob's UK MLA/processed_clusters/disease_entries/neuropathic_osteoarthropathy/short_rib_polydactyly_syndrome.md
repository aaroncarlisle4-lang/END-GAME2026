# Disease: SHORT-RIB POLYDACTYLY SYNDROME

**Source section:** NEUROPATHIC OSTEOARTHROPATHY

## Definition

group of autosomal recessive disorders characterized by short limb dysplasia, constricted SALDINO-NOONAN SYNDROME MAJEWSKI TYPE

## Imaging Findings

### General

- severe micromelia
- pointed femurs at both ends (type I); widened metaphyses (type III)
- narrow thorax
- extremely short horizontally oriented ribs
- distorted underossified vertebral bodies + incomplete coronal clefts
- polydactyly
- cleft lip / palate

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** uniformly lethal
