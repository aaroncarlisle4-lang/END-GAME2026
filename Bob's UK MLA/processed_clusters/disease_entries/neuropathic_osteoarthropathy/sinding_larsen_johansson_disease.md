# Disease: SINDING-LARSEN-JOHANSSON DISEASE

**Source section:** NEUROPATHIC OSTEOARTHROPATHY

## Definition

osteochondrosis of inferior pole of patella, often bilateral (NOT osteonecrosis / epiphysitis /

## Key Facts

- **Age:** adolescents (often 10-14 years)

## Imaging Findings

### General

- peripatellar soft-tissue swelling
- calcification / ossification of patellar tendon
- patella alta deformity = abnormally elevated position of patella with respect to femur (LAT
- small bone fragments at lower pole of patella (LAT view)

### MR

- hypointense area on T1WI + hyperintense on T2WI in inferior pole of patella + proximal
- thickening of patellar tendon without tear / avulsion
- small bone fragment inferior to lower pole of patella
- patella alta
- joint effusion
- bone marrow edema of patella (MRI!)
