# Disease: SYNOVIAL CYST

**Source section:** NEUROPATHIC OSTEOARTHROPATHY

## Definition

BAKER CYST synovial cyst in the posterior aspect of knee joint communicating with posterior joint

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** lined by synovial cells
- **Prevalence:** 19% in general orthopedic patients; 61% in patients with rheumatoid arthritis

## Imaging Findings

### General

- communication with bursa (documented on arthrogram)
- well-outlined hypointense collection on T1WI + hyperintense on T2WI
- septa in 50%
- smooth contour
- smooth contour extending along fascial planes (usually between gastrocnemius +
- leakage into calf tissues
