# Disease: SOFT-TISSUE OSTEOMA

**Source section:** NEUROPATHIC OSTEOARTHROPATHY

## Definition

OSTEOMA OF SOFT PARTS (extremely rare)

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** mature lamellar bone with well-defined haversian system; bone marrow, myxoid,
- **Location:** head (usually posterior part of tongue), thigh

## Imaging Findings

### General

- ossified mass

### NUC

- intense tracer accumulation, greater than adjacent bone
