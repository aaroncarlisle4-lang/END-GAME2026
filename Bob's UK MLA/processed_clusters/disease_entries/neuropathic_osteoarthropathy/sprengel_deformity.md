# Disease: SPRENGEL DEFORMITY

**Source section:** NEUROPATHIC OSTEOARTHROPATHY

## Definition

failure of descent of scapula ← fibrous / osseous omovertebral connection

## Key Facts

- **Associated with:** Klippel-Feil syndrome, renal anomalies

## Imaging Findings

### General

- elevation + medial rotation of scapula
