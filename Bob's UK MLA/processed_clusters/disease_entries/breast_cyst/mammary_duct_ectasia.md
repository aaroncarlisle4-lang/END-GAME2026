# Disease: MAMMARY DUCT ECTASIA

**Source section:** BREAST CYST

## Definition

PLASMA CELL MASTITIS = VARICOCELE TUMOR OF BREAST = MASTITIS OBLITERANS = rare aseptic inflammation of subareolar area

## Key Facts

- **Mean age:** 54 years; may develop in young children
- **Location:** subareolar, often bilateral + symmetric; may be unilateral + focal

## Imaging Findings

### General

- dense triangular mass with apex toward nipple
- duct dilatation = distended ducts connecting to nipple
- periphery blending with normal tissue
- multiple often bilateral dense round / oval a few mm long calcifications with lucent center +
- oval / elongated calcified ring around dilated ducts with very dense periphery (=
- fairly uniform linear, often rod-shaped / sausage-shaped / needle-shaped calcifications
- nipple retraction / skin thickening may occur
