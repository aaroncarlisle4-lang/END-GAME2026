# Disease: FIBROCYSTIC CHANGES

**Source section:** BREAST CYST

## Definition

MAZOPLASIA = MASTITIS FIBROSA CYSTICA = CHRONIC CYSTIC MASTITIS = CYSTIC DISEASE = MAMMARY PROLIFERATIVE DISEASE proliferation of round cells of LCIS type growing along terminal ducts in permeative low-grade intraductal proliferation with partial / incompletely developed features of FIBROADENOMATOID MASTOPATHY benign proliferative lesion

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** most common diffuse breast disorder; in 51% of 3,000 autopsies
- **Age:** 35-55 years
- **Etiology:** exaggeration of normal cyclical proliferation + involution of the breast with
- **Path:** lobulocentric lesion derived from TDLU with distortion and effacement of underlying
- **Histo:** epithelial and myoepithelial proliferation of ductules + lobules with nuclear
- **Mean age:** 32 years; black woman
