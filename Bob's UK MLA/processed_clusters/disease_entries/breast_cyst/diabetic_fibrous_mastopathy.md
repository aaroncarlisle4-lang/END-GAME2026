# Disease: DIABETIC (FIBROUS) MASTOPATHY

**Source section:** BREAST CYST

## Definition

rare fibroinflammatory breast disease

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** stromal sclerosis with an increased number of spindle cells, scattered epithelial cells
- **Age:** young woman, 20 years after onset of diabetes

## Imaging Findings

### General

- nonspecific discrete mass / regional asymmetry of dense tissue

### US

- single / multiple bilateral masses hypoechoic to SQ fat
- hypovascular / avascular on color flow imaging
- posterior acoustic shadowing
