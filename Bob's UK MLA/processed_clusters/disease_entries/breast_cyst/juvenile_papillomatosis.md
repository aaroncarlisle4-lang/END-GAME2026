# Disease: JUVENILE PAPILLOMATOSIS

**Source section:** BREAST CYST

## Definition

distinctive rare benign clinical-pathologic entity that characteristically involves young

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** low but increasing
- **Path:** 1-8 cm tumor composed of many aggregated cysts of < 2 cm with interspersed dense
- **Histo:** multiple macrocysts lined by flat duct epithelium / epithelium with apocrine metaplasia,
- **Mean age:** 23 (range, 12-48) years

## Imaging Findings

### General

- usually negative mammogram / asymmetric density
- ± microcalcifications
- marked enhancement with benign enhancement profile on MR

### US

- ill-defined hypoechoic masses filled with multiple cysts of variable size clearly

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** development of synchronous (4%) / metachronous (5-15%) breast cancer after 8-9
- **Rx:** complete surgical excision requiring negative margins
