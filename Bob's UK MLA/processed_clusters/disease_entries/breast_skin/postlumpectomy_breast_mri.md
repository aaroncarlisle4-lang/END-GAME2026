# Disease: POSTLUMPECTOMY BREAST MRI

**Source section:** BREAST SKIN

## Imaging Findings

### General

- persists in 25% at 6 years after breast conservation Rx
- seen for up to 18 months
- slow initial + persistent delayed enhancement
- persists up to 5 years
- nodular non-masslike enhancement (NMLE) > 5 mm around a seroma cavity
- segmental / ductal / clumped NMLE
- masslike enhancement
- rapid initial + washout kinetics NOT consistent with fat necrosis
- increasing size of formerly "probably benign" lesion
- focal / multifocal masses with rapid initial + delayed washout kinetics
- irregular / spiculated margins (84-91% PPV)
- rim enhancement
