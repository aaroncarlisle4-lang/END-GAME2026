# Disease: TOXIC AUTONOMOUS NODULE

**Source section:** OLFACTORY NEUROBLASTOMA

## Definition

PLUMMER DISEASE = TOXIC ADENOMA hyperthyroidism caused by one / two hyperfunctioning nodules independent of normal

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** adenoma

## Imaging Findings

### General

- radioiodine uptake (RAIU) mildly to moderately elevated
- concentration of radiopharmaceutical to a far greater degree than surrounding extranodular
