# Disease: PERSISTENCE OF STAPEDIAL ARTERY

**Source section:** OLFACTORY NEUROBLASTOMA

## Definition

rare congenital vascular anomaly due to failure of regression of the embryonic stapedial

## Imaging Findings

### General

- enlarged / separate tympanic segment of facial nerve canal
- abnormal linear soft-tissue density crossing over cochlear promontory
- absent / hypoplastic ipsilateral foramen spinosum
- absence of normal origin of middle meningeal artery
