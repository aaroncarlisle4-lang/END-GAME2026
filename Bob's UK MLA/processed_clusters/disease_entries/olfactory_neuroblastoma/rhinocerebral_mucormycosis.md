# Disease: RHINOCEREBRAL MUCORMYCOSIS

**Source section:** OLFACTORY NEUROBLASTOMA

## Definition

paranasal sinus / orbital infection caused by nonseptated fungi Rhizopus arrhizus and

## Imaging Findings

### General

- propensity for vascular invasion by fungal hyphae ← production of elastase
- nodular thickening involving nasal septum + turbinates
- mucoperiosteal thickening + clouding of ethmoids
- focal areas of bone destruction

## Clinical

- **Spread:** fungus first involves nasal cavity, then extends into maxillary / ethmoid sinuses / orbits
- **Cx:** cerebral infarction, hemorrhage, mycotic aneurysm
- **Prognosis:** high mortality rate
