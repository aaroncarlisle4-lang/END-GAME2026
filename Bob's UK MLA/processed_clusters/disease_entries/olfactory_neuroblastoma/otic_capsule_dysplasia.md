# Disease: OTIC CAPSULE DYSPLASIA

**Source section:** OLFACTORY NEUROBLASTOMA

## Definition

cochlear cavity malformation saccular defect / cavity in otic capsule in the position normally occupied by cochlea cystic cochleovestibular malformation complete lack of partitioning of cochlea classic Mondini malformation normal basilar turn + varying degrees of hypoplasia of middle and apical turns Michel aplasia = Michel anomaly = agenesis of osseous + membranous labyrinth (rare) decrease in the diameter of IAC ← hypoplasia / aplasia of cochlear nerve (portion of ENLARGED VESTIBULAR AQUEDUCT SYNDROME

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 2nd most common imaging finding in children with sensorineural hearing loss
- **Associated with:** underdeveloped lateral semicircular canal
- **Age:** manifests around 3 years
- **Location:** bilateral in 50-66%
