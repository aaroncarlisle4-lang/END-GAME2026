# Disease: TEMPOROMANDIBULAR JOINT DYSFUNCTION

**Source section:** OLFACTORY NEUROBLASTOMA

## Definition

internal derangement = abnormal relationship of disk to condyle

## Key Facts

- **Incidence:** affects up to 28% of population

## Imaging Findings

- flattening of disk

- disk displacement:
- anterior (in up to 34% of asymptomatic volunteers), anterolateral, anteromedial, lateral,
- anterior displacement of posterior band in front of condyle with abnormal stretching of
- anterior displacement of meniscus without reduction at maximum mouth opening
- "stuck disk" = fixed position of disk relative to glenoid fossa and articular eminence during
- "locked jaw" = posterior band displaced beyond 1 o'clock position
- joint effusion
- osteoarthritis: flattening, osteophytes, erosions, sclerosis
