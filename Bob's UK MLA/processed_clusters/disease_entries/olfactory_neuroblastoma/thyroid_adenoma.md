# Disease: THYROID ADENOMA

**Source section:** OLFACTORY NEUROBLASTOMA

## Definition

COLLOID NODULE = ADENOMATOUS HYPERPLASIA DEGENERATIVE INVOLUTED NODULE monoclonal tumor arising from follicular epithelium

## Key Facts

- **Path:** single lesion with well-developed fibrous capsule

## Imaging Findings

### General

- round / oval mass of low attenuation with enhancement
- often multiple nodules by US / scintigraphy / surgery
- mostly hypofunctioning, rarely hyperfunctioning
- solid form = incompletely encapsulated, poorly demarcated nodules merging with
- cystic form (= colloid cyst) = anechoic areas in nodule ← hemorrhage / colloid
- calcific deposits
- mass with increased / decreased echogenicity
- "halo" sign = complete hypoechoic ring with regular border surrounding isoechoic solid

## Clinical

- **Rx:** surgical hemithyroidectomy (→ 15-30% risk of malignancy if diagnosed on FNAB)
