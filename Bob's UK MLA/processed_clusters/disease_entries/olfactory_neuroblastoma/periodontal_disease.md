# Disease: PERIODONTAL DISEASE

**Source section:** OLFACTORY NEUROBLASTOMA

## Imaging Findings

### CT

- rimlike enhancement of fluid collection (abscess), cellulitis, myositis
- cortical dehiscence commonly on lingual aspect of mandible
- permeative bone changes, osseous destruction, periosteal reaction

## Clinical

- **Cx:** descending necrotizing mediastinitis
- **Rx:** tooth extraction, abscess drainage, IV antibiotics
