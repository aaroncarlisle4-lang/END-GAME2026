# Disease: PERITONSILLAR ABSCESS

**Source section:** OLFACTORY NEUROBLASTOMA

## Definition

complication of untreated / incompletely treated tonsillitis

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** ⅓ of all soft-tissue abscesses of head & neck
- **Location:** between tonsillar capsule and pillar

## Imaging Findings

### General

- tonsillar enlargement: √ "kissing tonsils" = medial apposition of enlarged tonsils
- mass effect on oropharynx
- linear striated enhancement of palatine tonsil + posterior pharyngeal soft tissue
- central liquefaction + surrounding rimlike enhancement
- true tonsillar abscess (rare)
