# Disease: PINDBORG TUMOR

**Source section:** OLFACTORY NEUROBLASTOMA

## Definition

CALCIFYING EPITHELIAL ODONTOGENIC TUMOR

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 0.17-1.8% of all odontogenic tumors
- **Age:** 20-60 years; M÷F = 1÷1
- **Histo:** epithelial cells in a fibrous stroma
- **Associated with:** crown of impacted tooth
- **Location:** premolar / molar region of mandible (⅔), maxilla (⅓)

## Imaging Findings

### General

- perichondral lucencies
- radiolucent with scattered calcified components

### CT

- diffuse highly attenuated foci = intralesional calcifications

### MR

- predominantly low signal intensity on T1WI
- high signal intensity on T2WI
- heterogeneous contrast enhancement
