# Disease: OTITIS MEDIA

**Source section:** OLFACTORY NEUROBLASTOMA

## Definition

CHRONIC OTOMASTOIDITIS long-term damage to middle ear

## Key Facts

- **Age:** infants + young children > adults

## Imaging Findings

### General

- partial / total fluid-attenuation opacification of middle ear ± fluid level
- opacification of mastoid air cells
- middle ear effusion
- granulation tissue (no displacement / destruction)
- cholesterol granuloma
- cholesteatoma

## Clinical

- **Cx:** (1) Otomastoiditis
