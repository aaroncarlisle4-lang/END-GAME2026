## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 2nd most common benign tumor of parotid gland; 5%-10% of all benign salivary
- **Age:** 5-6th decade; M > F
- **Histo:** CHARACTERISTIC double layer of oncocytes (= epithelial cells) resting on a dense
- **Associated with:** history of smoking
- **Location:** usually solitary + unilateral; bilateral + multifocal (±metachronous) in 10-60%

## Imaging Findings

### General

- well-circumscribed single / multiple cystic / solid lesion in parotid region usually 3-4 cm in

### MR

- hypointense compared with fat / surrounding parotid tissue on T2WI

### US

- oval hypoechoic well-defined tumor
- frequently multiple anechoic areas
- increased vascularity

### NUC

- increased uptake with 99mTc, 201Tl, FDG

## Clinical

- **Cx:** sporadically malignant transformation of epithelial component
- **Rx:** surgical resection
