# Disease: PENDRED SYNDROME

**Source section:** OLFACTORY NEUROBLASTOMA

## Definition

autosomal recessive disorder of chromosome 7 characterized by dyshormonogenic goiter

## Imaging Findings

### General

- multinodular goiter
- absence of interscalar septum of modiolus (= central bony spiral of cochlea) in 20-75%;
- enlargement of vestibular duct in 80-100%
