# Disease: SINONASAL PSEUDOTUMOR

**Source section:** OLFACTORY NEUROBLASTOMA

## Key Facts

- **Location:** nasal cavity, nasopharynx, skull base, temporal bone, maxillary sinus, larynx,

## Imaging Findings

### General

- more aggressive appearance than orbital pseudotumors:
- osseous erosion, remodeling, sclerosis (common)
- ± perineural spread along maxillary, mandibular, hypoglossal nn.

## Clinical

- **Cx:** internal carotid occlusion
- **Prognosis:** good after complete resection
- **Rx:** less responsive to steroid therapy than orbital pseudotumors
