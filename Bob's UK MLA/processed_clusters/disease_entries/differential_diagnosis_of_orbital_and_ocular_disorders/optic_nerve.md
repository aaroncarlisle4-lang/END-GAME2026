# Disease: OPTIC NERVE

**Source section:** DIFFERENTIAL DIAGNOSIS OF ORBITAL AND OCULAR DISORDERS

## Definition

lens-shaped thickening single / multiple nodules along nerve-sheath complex usually due to tumor uniform enlargement of nerve-sheath complex

## Imaging Findings

### General

- fusiform thickening of optic nerve-sheath complex
- excrescentic thickening of optic nerve-sheath complex
- tubular enlargement of optic nerve-sheath complex
