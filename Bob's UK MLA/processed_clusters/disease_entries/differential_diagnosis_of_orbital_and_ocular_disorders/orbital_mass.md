# Disease: ORBITAL MASS

**Source section:** DIFFERENTIAL DIAGNOSIS OF ORBITAL AND OCULAR DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Location:** single muscle (in adults); multiple muscles (in children)

## Imaging Findings

### General

- avidly enhancing orbital soft-tissue mass with involvement of extraocular muscles + lacrimal glands
- enlarged extraocular muscle
- positive response to steroids
