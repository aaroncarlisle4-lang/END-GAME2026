# Disease: SALIVARY GLANDS

**Source section:** NECK

## Definition

main excretory duct of parotid gland

## Key Facts

- **Location:** mainly in upper + lower poles of parotid

## Imaging Findings

### US

- homogeneous texture varying from markedly hyperechoic to slightly hyperechoic (compared
- short axis-to-long axis ratio > 0.5 (in 60%)
- short axis diameter < 5-6 mm
- hyperechoic hilum
- normal duct sonographically not visible
- may be connected to parotid / sublingual glands by glandular processes
- duct sonographically not visible
