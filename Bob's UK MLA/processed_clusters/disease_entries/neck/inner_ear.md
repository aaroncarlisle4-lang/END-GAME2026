# Disease: INNER EAR

**Source section:** NECK

## Definition

central and most capacious ovoid part of bony labyrinth posterior to cochlea horizontal bony septum dividing IAC into superior and inferior compartments

## Key Facts

- **Location:** petrous portion of temporal bone
- **Size:** ≤ 0.1-0.2 mm

## Imaging Findings

### General

- juts into epitympanum
- vertical orientation transverse to long axis of petrous part of temporal bone
- forms convexity of arcuate eminence
- nearly parallel to posterior surface of petrous bone
- filled with endolymph
