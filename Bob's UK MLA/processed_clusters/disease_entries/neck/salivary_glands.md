# Disease: SALIVARY GLANDS

**Source section:** NECK

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 90-95% occur in parotid gland, 5% in submandibular + sublingual glands; only

## Imaging Findings

### General

- hypoechoic enlargement of gland
- multiple small oval hypoechoic areas
- enlarged lymph nodes with increased blood flow
