# Disease: OSSICULAR CHAIN

**Source section:** NECK

## Key Facts

- **Location:** anterolateral to incus and stapes

## Imaging Findings

### General

- "ice cream cone" configuration on axial CT
- lateral process with ligament may be visible on CT
- sometimes visible on CT as thin linear structures (most commonly lateral malleal lig.)
