# Disease: LYMPH NODES OF THE NECK

**Source section:** NECK

## Definition

level VI node (not routinely excised during radical neck dissection)

## Imaging Findings

### CT

- iso- or hypoattenuating to muscle

### MR

- low to intermediate signal intensity on T1WI
- intermediate to high signal intensity on T2WI

### US

- hypoechoic in comparison with muscle
- ovoid shape with echogenic fatty hilum
- greater enlargement + inflammation of surrounding tissue
- diffuse enlargement + little surrounding inflammation
- normal to slightly enlarged in size
- ± enhancement ← increased vascularity

## Clinical

- **Cx:** progression to suppurative adenopathy
