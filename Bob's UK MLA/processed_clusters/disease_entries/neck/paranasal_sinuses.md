# Disease: PARANASAL SINUSES

**Source section:** NECK

## Definition

anteriormost ethmoid air cell in front of the attachment of middle turbinate to ethmoidal air cell above + posterior to infundibulum + hiatus semilunaris, located INFRAORBITAL ETHMOIDAL AIR CELL = MAXILLOETHMOIDAL / anterior ethmoid air cells inferolateral to ethmoidal bulla, on lateral wall of most posterior ethmoid air cell pneumatized into sphenoid bone ± surrounding the pneumatization of frontal recess by various anterior ethmoid cells single anterior ethmoid cell within frontal recess above agger nasi cell tier of > 2 anterior ethmoid cells above agger nasi cell single massive cell above agger nasi pneumatizing into frontal sinus isolated air cell along anterior wall of frontal sinus not abutting the agger nasi cell

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 78-99%
- **Location:** anterior, lateral + inferior to frontoethmoidal recess = on lateral nasal wall
- **Prevalence:** present in > 90%
- **Size:** 28 x 24 x 20 mm in adults, rapid growth until late teens
