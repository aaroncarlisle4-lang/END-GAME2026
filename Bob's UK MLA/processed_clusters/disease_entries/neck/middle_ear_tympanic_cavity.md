# Disease: MIDDLE EAR / TYMPANIC CAVITY

**Source section:** NECK

## Definition

air-filled cavity within petrous portion of temporal bone containing ossicular chain carotid wall mastoid wall including from lateral to medial small triangular fragile 2-layered membrane in upper region loosely attached to larger robust tightly attached region consisting of 3 layers (skin + fibrous tissue + rounded promontory

## Key Facts

- **Location:** above malleolar folds; forms superior ⅛ of drum circumference

## Imaging Findings

### General

- may be faintly discerned on CT
- wall prominence posteriorly + superiorly
- wall prominence inferior and anterior to (1)
- wall prominence anterior to (2)
- inferior to (3)
- convexity inferior + slightly anterior to (4)
- recess on posterior wall
- recess on posterior wall lateral to (6)
- situated between sinus tympani + facial nerve recess
