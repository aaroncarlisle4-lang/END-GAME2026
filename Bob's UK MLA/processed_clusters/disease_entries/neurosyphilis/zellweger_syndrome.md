# Disease: ZELLWEGER SYNDROME

**Source section:** NEUROSYPHILIS

## Definition

CEREBROHEPATORENAL SYNDROME autosomal recessive peroxisomal disorder with disturbed myelination in oligodendrocytes

## Key Facts

- **Path:** ⇑ very long-chain fatty acids in plasma and fibroblasts

## Imaging Findings

### MR

- hypomyelination
- diffuse microgyria + regions of pachygyria, lissencephaly
- subependymal germinolytic cysts in caudothalamic groove
- ↑ lipid levels + ↓ N-acetylaspartate levels
- renal cortical cysts

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** death within 1st year of life
