# Disease: WILSON DISEASE

**Source section:** NEUROSYPHILIS

## Definition

HEPATOLENTICULAR DEGENERATION autosomal recessive disorder with increased intestinal resorption of copper → excessive excessive copper deposition in brain

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 1÷33,000-200,000; 1÷90 persons is a heterozygous carrier
- **Location:** commonly in lenticular nucleus (= lenslike configuration of putamen + globus

## Imaging Findings

### CNS

- diffusion restriction (in early stages)
- cerebral white matter atrophy
- areas of CT hypodensities + T2 prolongation

### Liver

- normal hepatic attenuation (fatty infiltration + copper deposition cancel each other out)
- normal T1 relaxation time (in spite of paramagnetic effects of copper)

### Skeletal manifestations (in ⅔)

- subarticular cysts
- premature osteoarthritis (narrowing of joint space + osteophyte formation)
- osteochondritis dissecans
- chondrocalcinosis
- premature osteoarthrosis of spine, prominent Schmorl nodes, wedging of vertebrae,
- generalized deossification may produce pathologic fractures

## Clinical

- **Rx:** life-long pharmacologic therapy with chelation agents (penicillamine / trientine / zinc);
- **Cx:** acute fulminant hepatitis, macronodular cirrhosis
