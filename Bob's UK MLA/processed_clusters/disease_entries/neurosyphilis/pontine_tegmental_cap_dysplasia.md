# Disease: PONTINE TEGMENTAL CAP DYSPLASIA

**Source section:** NEUROSYPHILIS

## Definition

sporadic malformation with unknown genotype

## Imaging Findings

### General

- flattened ventral pons
- vaulted pontine tegmentum ("cap")
- partial absence of middle cerebellar peduncles
- vermian hypoplasia
- molar tooth-like aspect of pontomesencephalic junction
- absent inferior olivary prominence
