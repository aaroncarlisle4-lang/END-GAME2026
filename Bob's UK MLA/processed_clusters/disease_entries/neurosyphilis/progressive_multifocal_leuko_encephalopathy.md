# Disease: PROGRESSIVE MULTIFOCAL LEUKO-ENCEPHALOPATHY

**Source section:** NEUROSYPHILIS

## Definition

rapidly progressive fatal demyelinating disease in patients with impaired immune system

## Key Facts

- **Prevalence:** 4% of AIDS patients
- **Etiology:** reactivation of ubiquitous JC papovavirus
