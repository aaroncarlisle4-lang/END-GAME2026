# Disease: POSTERIOR REVERSIBLE ENCEPHALOPATHY SYNDROME

**Source section:** NEUROSYPHILIS

## Definition

(PRES) = REVERSIBLE POSTERIOR LEUKOENCEPHALOPATHY SYNDROME = heterogeneous collection of disorders associated with a breakdown in cerebral autoregulatory

## Key Facts

- **Location:** white matter of posterior half of brain

## Imaging Findings

### General

- hypodense white matter on NECT
- lesion hypointense on T1WI + hyperintense on T2WI ← vasogenic edema (= fluid
- lesion hypo- to isointense on DWI + hyperintense on ADC map (due to a net effect of elevated
- NO contrast enhancement

## Clinical

- **Cx:** infarction (lesions develop high DWI signal + normal ADC signal)
- **Rx:** rapid control of blood pressure / withdrawal of offending drug
