# Disease: PICK DISEASE

**Source section:** NEUROSYPHILIS

## Definition

FRONTOTEMPORAL DEMENTIA rare form of presenile dementia similar to Alzheimer disease; may be inherited with

## Imaging Findings

### General

- focal cortical atrophy of anterior frontal + anterior temporal lobes
- dilatation of frontal + temporal horns of lateral ventricle

### PET

- hypometabolism in frontal + anterior temporal lobes + anterior cingulate gyrus
- temporal lobe involvement extends to anterior aspects (NOT in Alzheimer disease)
- frontal-predominant form = sparing of temporal lobes
