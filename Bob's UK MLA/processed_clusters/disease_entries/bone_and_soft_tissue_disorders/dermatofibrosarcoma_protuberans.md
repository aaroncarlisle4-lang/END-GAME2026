# Disease: DERMATOFIBROSARCOMA PROTUBERANS

**Source section:** BONE AND SOFT-TISSUE DISORDERS

## Definition

uncommon spindle cell tumor arising in dermis typically spreading into subcutaneous tissue +

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 6% of all soft-tissue sarcomas
- **Age:** mostly adolescence
- **Location:** trunk (50%)

## Imaging Findings

### CT

- well-defined nodular subcutaneous lesion without calcification

### MR

- unmineralized small nodule / large mass
- nonspecific signal intensity:
- hypointense on T1WI
- hyperintense relative to fat on T2WI
- ± heterogeneous foci of hemorrhage, myxoid change, necrosis
- linear extension along skin surface
- moderate enhancement
