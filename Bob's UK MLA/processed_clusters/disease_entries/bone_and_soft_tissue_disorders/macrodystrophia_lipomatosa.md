# Disease: MACRODYSTROPHIA LIPOMATOSA

**Source section:** BONE AND SOFT-TISSUE DISORDERS

## Definition

rare nonhereditary congenital form of localized gigantism = neural fibrolipoma with

## Key Facts

- **Path:** striking increase in adipose tissue in a fine fibrous network involving periosteum, bone

## Imaging Findings

### General

- long + broad splayed phalanges with endosteal + periosteal bone deposition
- overgrowth of soft tissue, greatest at volar + distal aspects
- slanting of articular surfaces
- lucent areas of fat (DIAGNOSTIC)

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** accelerated maturation possible; growth stops at puberty
