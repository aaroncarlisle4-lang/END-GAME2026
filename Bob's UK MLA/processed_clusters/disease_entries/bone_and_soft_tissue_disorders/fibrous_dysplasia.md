## Definition

FIBROUS OSTEODYSTROPHY = OSTEODYSTROPHIA FIBROSA = OSTEITIS benign developmental anomaly of mesenchymal precursor of bone → slowly progressive Mazabraud syndrome: expands along longitudinal axis of bone (rarely over entire length) Leontiasis ossea [leon, Greek = lion] CHERUBISM [kerubh, Hebrew = winged angel] autosomal dominant disorder of variable penetrance; probably form of giant cell nonhereditary phakomatosis
