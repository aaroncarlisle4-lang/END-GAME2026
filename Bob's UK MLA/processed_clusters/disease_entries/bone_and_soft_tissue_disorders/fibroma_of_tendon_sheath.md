# Disease: FIBROMA OF TENDON SHEATH

**Source section:** BONE AND SOFT-TISSUE DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** paucicellular tumor with scattered spindle-shaped myofibroblasts embedded in dense
- **Mean age:** 31 (range, 20-50) years; M÷F = 2÷1
- **Location:** tendon sheath of upper extremity (fingers, hands, wrists) in 82%
- **Size:** usually < 3 (range, 1-5) cm

## Imaging Findings

### MR

- small hypointense nodule on all pulse sequences compared to muscle
- areas of high signal intensity ← myxoid change
- no / moderate / marked enhancement
- attachment to tendon sheath

## Clinical

- **Rx:** local excision (24% recurrence rate)
