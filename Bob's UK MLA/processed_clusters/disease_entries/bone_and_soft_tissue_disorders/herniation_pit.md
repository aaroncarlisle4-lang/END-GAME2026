# Disease: HERNIATION PIT

**Source section:** BONE AND SOFT-TISSUE DISORDERS

## Definition

SYNOVIAL HERNIATION PIT = CONVERSION DEFECT ingrowth of fibrous + cartilaginous elements from adjacent joint through perforation in cortex

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** fibroalveolar tissue
- **Age:** usually in older individuals
- **Size:** usually < 1 cm in diameter; may enlarge over time

## Imaging Findings

### General

- well-circumscribed round lucency
- reactive thin sclerotic border
- hyperintense area on T2WI (= fluid signal intensity)
- bone scan may be positive
