# Disease: HYPERTROPHIC OSTEOARTHROPATHY

**Source section:** BONE AND SOFT-TISSUE DISORDERS

## Definition

HYPERTROPHIC PULMONARY OSTEOARTHROPATHY (HPO) = MARIE- paraneoplastic syndrome

## Imaging Findings

### General

- cortical thickening
- lamellar periosteal proliferation of new bone, at first smooth then undulating + rough
- soft-tissue swelling ("clubbing") of distal phalanges
- "parallel track" / "double stripe" / "tramline" sign = patchy linear diffusely increased
- increased periarticular uptake ← synovitis
- scapular involvement in ²/³
- mandible ± maxilla abnormal in 40%

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** treatment of underlying condition leads to remission of symptoms often within 24
