# Disease: BENIGN CORTICAL DEFECT

**Source section:** BONE AND SOFT-TISSUE DISORDERS

## Definition

developmental intracortical bone defect

## Key Facts

- **Age:** usually 1st-2nd decade; uncommon in boys < 2 years of age; uncommon in girls < 4 years

## Imaging Findings

### General

- well-defined intracortical round / oval lucency
- usually < 2 cm long
- sclerotic margins

## Clinical

- **Cx:** pathologic / avulsion fracture following minor trauma (infrequent)
