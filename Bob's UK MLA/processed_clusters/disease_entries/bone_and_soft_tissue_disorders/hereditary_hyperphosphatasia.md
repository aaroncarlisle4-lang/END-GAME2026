# Disease: HEREDITARY HYPERPHOSPHATASIA

**Source section:** BONE AND SOFT-TISSUE DISORDERS

## Definition

"JUVENILE PAGET DISEASE" rare autosomal recessive disease with sustained elevation of serum alkaline phosphatase,

## Key Facts

- **Histo:** rapid turnover of lamellar bone without formation of cortical bone; immature woven
- **Age:** 1st-3rd year; usually stillborn

## Imaging Findings

### General

- deossification = decreased density of long bones with coarse trabecular pattern
- metaphyseal growth deficiency
- wide irregular epiphyseal lines (resembling rickets in childhood), persistent metaphyseal
- bowing of long bones + fractures with irregular callus
- widened medullary canal with cortical thinning (cortex modeled from trabecular bone)
- skull greatly thickened with wide tables, cotton wool appearance
- vertebra plana
- diagnosis suspected in utero in 20%

## Clinical

- **Cx:** pathologic fractures; vertebra plana universalis
