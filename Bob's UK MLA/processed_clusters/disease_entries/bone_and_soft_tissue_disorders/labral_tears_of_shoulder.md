# Disease: LABRAL TEARS OF SHOULDER

**Source section:** BONE AND SOFT-TISSUE DISORDERS

## Definition

anterior-to-posterior lesion of the superior labrum centered at biceps tendon attachment

## Key Facts

- **Location:** anteroinferior labrum > entire anterior labrum > isolated tear of anterosuperior

## Imaging Findings

### General

- absence / detachment of labrum
- frayed labrum with irregular margin
