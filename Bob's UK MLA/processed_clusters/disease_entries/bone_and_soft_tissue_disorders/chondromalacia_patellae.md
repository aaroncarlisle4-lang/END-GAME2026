# Disease: CHONDROMALACIA PATELLAE

**Source section:** BONE AND SOFT-TISSUE DISORDERS

## Definition

pathologic softening of patellar cartilage leading to defects of surface (chondrosis) /

## Imaging Findings

### General

- focal areas of increased T2 / PD signal intensity of cartilage
- partial / full-thickness cartilage loss
- underlying marrow / bone reactive changes
