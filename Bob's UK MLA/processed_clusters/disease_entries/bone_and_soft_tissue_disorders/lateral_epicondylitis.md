# Disease: LATERAL EPICONDYLITIS

**Source section:** BONE AND SOFT-TISSUE DISORDERS

## Definition

TENNIS ELBOW = TENDINITIS (epicondylitis is a misnomer) chronic overuse syndrome involving origin of extensor carpi radialis brevis tendon

- **Age:** 35-50 years
