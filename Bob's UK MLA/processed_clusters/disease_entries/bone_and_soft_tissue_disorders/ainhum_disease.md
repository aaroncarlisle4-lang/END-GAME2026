# Disease: AINHUM DISEASE

**Source section:** BONE AND SOFT-TISSUE DISORDERS

## Definition

DACTYLOLYSIS SPONTANEA

## Key Facts

- **Etiology:** unknown
- **Histo:** hyperkeratotic epidermis with fibrotic thickening of collagen bundles below; chronic
- **Frequency:** up to 2%
- **Age:** usually in males in 4th + 5th decades; Blacks (West Africa) + their American descendants;

## Imaging Findings

### General

- sharply demarcated progressive bone resorption of distal / middle phalanx with tapering of
- osteoporosis

## Clinical

- **Rx:** early surgical resection of groove with Z-plasty
