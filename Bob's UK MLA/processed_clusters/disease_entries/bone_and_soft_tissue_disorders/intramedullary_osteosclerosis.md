# Disease: INTRAMEDULLARY OSTEOSCLEROSIS

**Source section:** BONE AND SOFT-TISSUE DISORDERS

## Definition

rare condition increased bone formation within medullary cavity of long bones of lower

## Key Facts

- **Age:** adulthood; F > M
- **Location:** uni- / bilateral in ≥ 1 long bone of lower extremity; most frequently in tibia

## Imaging Findings

### General

- osteosclerosis limited to medullary cavity with minimal / no cortical thickening
