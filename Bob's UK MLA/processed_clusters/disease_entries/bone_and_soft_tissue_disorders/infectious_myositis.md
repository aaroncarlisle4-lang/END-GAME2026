# Disease: INFECTIOUS MYOSITIS

**Source section:** BONE AND SOFT-TISSUE DISORDERS

## Definition

acute / subacute / chronic infection of skeletal muscle

## Key Facts

- **Location:** usually single muscle; multiple in 11-43%; quadriceps m. > gluteal m. > iliopsoas

## Imaging Findings

### CT

- enlarged hypoattenuating muscle
- effacement of surrounding fat planes
- intramuscular fluid collection
- nonenhancement of necrotic tissue
- rim-enhancement of abscess
