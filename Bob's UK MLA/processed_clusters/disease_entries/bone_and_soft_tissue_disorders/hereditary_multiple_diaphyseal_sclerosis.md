# Disease: HEREDITARY MULTIPLE DIAPHYSEAL SCLEROSIS

**Source section:** BONE AND SOFT-TISSUE DISORDERS

## Definition

RIBBING DISEASE autosomal recessive disorder of intramembranous ossification similar to progressive

## Key Facts

- **Age:** after puberty, typically in middle-aged adults
- **Location:** typically tibia / femur
- **Distribution:** either unilateral / asymmetric + asynchronous bilateral involvement of long

## Imaging Findings

### General

- cortical thickening of periosteal + endosteal surfaces
- epiphyses characteristically spared
- ± narrowing of medullary canal
- disease may progress slowly and then stabilize
