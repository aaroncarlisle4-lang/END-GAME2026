# Disease: JUVENILE XANTHOGRANULOMA

**Source section:** BONE AND SOFT-TISSUE DISORDERS

## Definition

relatively benign non-LCH dendritic cell disorder of early childhood

## Key Facts

- **Age:** < 1 year (majority)
- **Location:** head, neck, upper trunk

## Imaging Findings

### Soft tissue

- heterogeneously hypoechoic hypovascular nodule
- infiltrative nonenhancing mass of intermediate T1 signal

### Bone

- well-defined medullary lytic lesion with sclerotic margins
