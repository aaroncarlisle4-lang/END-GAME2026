# Disease: KLIPPEL-TRÉNAUNAY SYNDROME

**Source section:** BONE AND SOFT-TISSUE DISORDERS

## Definition

rare sporadic (nonhereditary) combined capillary-venous malformation of trunk + extremities

## Key Facts

- **Incidence:** > 1,000 cases reported
- **Age:** usually manifest at birth; M÷F = 1÷1
- **Location:** one lower limb in 75-95% (10-15 x more common than upper extremity); bilateral

## Imaging Findings
