# Disease: ELASTOFIBROMA DORSI

**Source section:** BONE AND SOFT-TISSUE DISORDERS

## Definition

degenerative / reactive fibrous pseudotumor forming as a reaction to chronic mechanical

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** in 24% of women + 11% of men > 55 years (autopsy study); in 2% of CT
- **Histo:** enlarged irregular serrated elastic hypereosinophilic fibers, collagen, scattered

## Imaging Findings

### CT

- heterogeneous poorly defined lesion of soft-tissue attenuation similar to muscle

### MR

- well-defined heterogeneous intermediate-SI lesion similar to muscle on T1WI + T2WI
- interspersed streaks of fat intensity in fascicular pattern
- heterogeneous enhancement

## Clinical

- **Rx:** local excision for symptomatic lesions
