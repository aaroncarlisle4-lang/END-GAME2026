# Disease: ANTERIOR TIBIAL BOWING

**Source section:** BONE AND SOFT-TISSUE DISORDERS

## Definition

WEISMANN-NETTER SYNDROME congenital painless nonprogressive bilateral anterior leg bowing

## Key Facts

- **Age:** beginning in early childhood

## Imaging Findings

### General

- anterior bowing of tibia + fibula, bilaterally, symmetrically at middiaphysis
- thickening of posterior tibial + fibular cortices
- minor radioulnar bowing
- kyphoscoliosis
- extensive dural calcification
