# Disease: BONE CONTUSION

**Source section:** BONE AND SOFT-TISSUE DISORDERS

## Definition

BONE BRUISE acute bone injury in children

## Key Facts

- **Path:** subcortical trabecular microfractures → intraosseous hemorrhage + edema without

## Imaging Findings

### General

- invisible
- high T2 signal intensity = marrow edema + hemorrhage

## Clinical

- **Prognosis:** healing in 12-16 weeks (much longer than for a typical cortical fracture)
- **Cx:** insufficiency fracture ← return to normal physical activity before complete healing
