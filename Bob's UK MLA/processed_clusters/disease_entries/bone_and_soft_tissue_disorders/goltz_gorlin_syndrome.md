# Disease: GOLTZ-GORLIN SYNDROME

**Source section:** BONE AND SOFT-TISSUE DISORDERS

## Definition

FOCAL DERMAL HYPOPLASIA X-linked dominant connective tissue genodermatosis

## Key Facts

- **Genetics:** defect in PORCN gene encoding a key protein involved in developmental Wnt
- **Location:** antecubital + popliteal fossa
- **Age:** present at birth

## Imaging Findings

### General

- scoliosis
- generalized osteopenia
- osteopathia striata = bilateral symmetric vertical metaphyseal striations of long bones with
- skeletal defects:
- "lobster claw" deformity = split hands and feet
- clinodactyly, oligodactyly, polydactyly, syndactyly
- regional asymmetric underdevelopment
- spina bifida
- rib and clavicular abnormalities
