# Disease: EPITHELOID SARCOMA

**Source section:** BONE AND SOFT-TISSUE DISORDERS

## Key Facts

- **Frequency:** 1-2% of all soft-tissue sarcomas
- **Age:** 10-35 years; M>> F
- **Location:** forearm, hand, finger

## Imaging Findings

### General

- soft-tissue mass ± speckled calcifications
- cortical thinning / osseous erosion
