# Finding: Low Alpha-fetoprotein

**Source section:** ELEVATED AMNIOTIC FLUID AFP (AF-AFP

**Differential count:** 6

## Differentials

- Absence of fetal tissues: eg, hydatidiform mole
- Fetal demise
- Misdated pregnancy
- Normal pregnancy
- Patient not pregnant
