# Finding: Pediatric Indications for Bone Scan

**Source section:** Indications for Bone Imaging
**Quality score:** 0.633
**Differential count:** 10

## Differentials

- Back pain
- Diskitis
- Pars interarticularis defect: SPECT imaging adds sensitivity
- Osteoid osteoma: can be used intraoperatively to ensure removal of nidus
- Sacroiliac infection
- Nonaccidental trauma
- < 10% 99mTc-tin colloid / free 99mTc-pertechnetate (a good preparation is 95% bound)
- Agent should not be used prior to 30 min after preparation
- Avoid injection of air in preparation of multidose vials (oxidation results in poor Tc bond)
- Kit life is 4-5 hours after preparation
