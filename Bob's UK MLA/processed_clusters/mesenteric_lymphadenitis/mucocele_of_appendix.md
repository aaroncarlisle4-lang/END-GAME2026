# Finding: MUCOCELE OF APPENDIX

**Source section:** MESENTERIC LYMPHADENITIS
**Quality score:** 0.7
**Differential count:** 12

## Differentials

- Simple mucocele (20%) = retention cyst = cystic dilatation of lumen ← obstruction by fecolith, foreign body, endometriosis, adhesions, postoperative scarring following appendectomy, volvulus, carcinoid, appendiceal carcinoma, carcinoma of cecum
- Focal / diffuse mucosal hyperplasia (5-25%)
- Mucinous cystadenoma ← hyperplasia with epithelial atypia (50-63%) = mucin-
- Mucinous cystadenocarcinoma with stromal invasion (11-20%)
- Cystic fibrosis with accumulation of thick mucus
- asymptomatic (25%), ± acute / chronic RLQ pain
  - palpable mass in right lower quadrant (in up to 50%)
  - altered bowel habits, weight loss, vomiting
  - ± elevated CEA, CA 19-9, CA 125
- Rupture → pseudomyxoma peritonei
- Torsion → gangrene + hemorrhage
- Herniation into cecum → bowel obstruction
- Intussusception
- Right ureteral obstruction
- usually asymptomatic, may appear as acute appendicitis
