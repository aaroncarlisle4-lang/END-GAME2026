# Finding: Genital Prolapse

**Source section:** MESENTERIC LYMPHADENITIS
**Quality score:** 0.5
**Differential count:** 6

## Differentials

- bulging vaginal mass outside external genitalia
  - dyspareunia, urinary retention, back pain
- Rx:  pessary, pelvic floor exercises, hysterectomy, vaginal repair
- Cx:  insidious progressive ureteral obstruction
- UTERINE PROCIDENTIA
- symptoms of bowel obstruction, vaginal pressure, dyspareunia, low back pain
  - difficult to diagnose on physical examination
- often missed at physical examination
  - impaired emptying → need for digital assistance

## Context
