# Finding: Pulmonary Amyloidosis

**Source section:** AMYLOIDOSIS OF THE CHEST
**Quality score:** 0.833
**Differential count:** 7

## Differentials

- NODULAR PARENCHYMAL TYPE (most common)
- usually asymptomatic, incidental discovery on CXR
- Size:   5-15 cm
- Site:   subpleural / peripheral
- Distribution:   concentrated in lower lobes
- DIFFUSE ALVEOLAR SEPTAL TYPE (least common) ◊  Most commonly associated with systemic amyloidosis
- usually asymptomatic with normal CXR
  - cough + dyspnea with abnormal CXR
