# Finding: D. INTRAPERITONEAL

**Source section:** ABNORMAL INTRAABDOMINAL AIR
**Quality score:** 0.7
**Differential count:** 12

## Differentials

- Gas-forming peritonitis
- Rupture of abscess
- Laparotomy / laparoscopy (58%) absorbed in 1-24 days dependent on initial amount of air introduced and body habitus (80% in asthenic, 25% in obese patients) ◊  After 3 days free air should be followed with suspicion!
- Leaking surgical anastomosis
- Peritoneal dialysis
- Feeding tube placement
- Endoscopic perforation
- Enema tip injury
- Use of gynecologic instruments
- Vigorous respiratory resuscitation
- Diagnostic pneumoperitoneum
- Diagnostic peritoneal lavage
