- True cyst (epithelial lining): glandular cyst, embryonal cyst, mesothelial inclusion cyst
- Parasitic cyst: hydatid cyst
- Hemorrhagic complication / degeneration of a tumor:
- Neuroblastoma (rare)
- Cortical adenoma with low density
