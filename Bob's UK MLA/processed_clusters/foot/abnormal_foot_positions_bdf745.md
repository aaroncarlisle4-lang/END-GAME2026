# Finding: Abnormal Foot Positions

**Source section:** FOOT
**Quality score:** 0.633
**Differential count:** 10

## Differentials

- FOREFOOT
- Varus = adduction =  axis of 1st metatarsal deviated medially relative to axis of talus
- Valgus = abduction
- Inversion = supination =  inward turning of sole of foot
- Eversion = pronation =  outward turning of sole of foot
- HINDFOOT talipes = any deformity of the ankle and hindfoot [talus, Latin = ankle; pes, Latin = foot]
- Equinus =  hindfoot abnormality with reversal of calcaneal pitch so that the heel cannot touch the ground
- Calcaneal foot =  very high calcaneal pitch so that forefoot cannot touch the ground
- Pes planus = flatfoot =  low calcaneal pitch + (usually) heel valgus + forefoot eversion
- Pes cavus =  high calcaneal pitch (fixed high arch)

## Context

=  axis of 1st metatarsal deviated laterally relative to axis of talus
