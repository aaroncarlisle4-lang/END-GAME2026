# Finding: Intraocular Calcifications

**Source section:** Spectrum of Ocular Disorders
**Quality score:** 0.733
**Differential count:** 7

## Differentials

- Retinoblastoma (> 50% of all cases)
- Astrocytic hamartoma
- Choroidal osteoma
- Optic drusen
- Scleral calcifications (a)  in systemic hypercalcemic states (HPT, sarcoidosis, hypervitaminosis D, in chronic renal disease) (b)  in elderly: scleral plaques at insertion of extraocular muscles, esp. medial + lateral rectus mm.
- Retrolental fibroplasia
- Phthisis bulbi
