# Finding: Cardiac Scleroderma

**Source section:** PRESBYESOPHAGUS
**Quality score:** 0.9
**Differential count:** 9

## Differentials

- arrhythmia
- abdominal pain, diarrhea
  - multiple episodes of pseudoobstruction
- dysphagia (50%), heartburn (30%) ›  functional:
- decreased small bowel motility with delayed intestinal transit time → stasis → bacterial overgrowth → diarrhea → malabsorption
- constipation (common), may alternate with diarrhea
- Dermatomyositis (similar radiographic findings)
- Sprue (increased secretions, segmentation, fragmentation, dilatation most significant in midjejunum, normal motility)
- Obstruction (no esophageal changes, no pseudodiverticula)
- Idiopathic intestinal pseudoobstruction (usually in young people)
