## Differentials

- Pericardial effusion with obstruction of thoracic duct

  - diarrhea, vomiting, abdominal pain, steatorrhea
  - hypoproteinemia ← protein loss into intestinal lumen Path:   dilatation of lymph vessels in mucosa + submucosa + abundance of foamy fat-staining macrophages (DDx to Whipple disease: negative for PAS)
