# Finding: Fat-containing Breast Lesion

**Source section:** BREAST LESION
**Quality score:** 0.533
**Differential count:** 4

## Differentials

- Lipoma
- Galactocele =  fluid with high lipid content (last phase)
  - during / shortly after lactation
- Oil cyst= traumatic lipid cyst = fat necrosis
  - site of prior surgery / trauma
- Focal collection of normal breast fat

## Context

◊  Fat contained within a lesion usually proves benignity!
