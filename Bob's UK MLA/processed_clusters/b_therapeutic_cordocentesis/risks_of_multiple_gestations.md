# Finding: Risks of Multiple Gestations

**Source section:** B. THERAPEUTIC CORDOCENTESIS
**Quality score:** 0.9
**Differential count:** 9

## Differentials

- Anemia    2.5-fold

- Perinatal mortality    4-6-fold ◊  Risk increases with number of fetuses, monozygosity, monochorionicity
- Perinatal mortality 5-10 times that of singleton pregnancy (91-124÷1,000 births) ›  9% for dichorionic diamniotic twins ›  26% for monochorionic diamniotic twins

- Increased risk of congenital anomalies: 23÷1,000 births = twice as frequent as in singletons; 3-7 times more frequent in mono- than in dizygotic twins
