# Finding: Duodenal Duplication Cyst (5%

**Source section:** DUODENAL VARICES
**Quality score:** 0.633
**Differential count:** 7

## Differentials

- obstructive symptoms, palpable abdominal mass
  - hemorrhage ← peptic ulceration
  - jaundice ← biliary obstruction
  - pancreatitis ← ectopic pancreatic tissue
- bowel obstruction / constipation
  - passage of feces through vagina
- abdominal mass, bowel obstruction / constipation
  - GI hemorrhage
- constipation + fecal soiling
- intractable excoriation of perianal skin (with chronic perianal fistula); palpable retrorectal / retroanal mass
- Anterior meningocele, sacrococcygeal teratoma, retrorectal abscess, pilonidal cyst, sacral bone tumor
- Tailgut cyst (no smooth muscle layer)

## Context

Path:  noncommunicating spherical cyst; may contain ectopic gastric mucosa in 21%, small bowel mucosa, pancreatic tissue Location:   1st + 2nd portion of duodenum Site:  on mesenteric side of anterior wall √  mass in concavity of duodenal C-loop √  compression + displacement of 1st / 2nd portion of duodenum superiorly + anteriorly Cholangiography: √  may communicate with pancreatic ductal system through the aberrant duct of an accessory lobe Cx:  pancreatitis ← perforation of duplication cyst DDx:  pancreatic cyst, pancreatic pseudocyst, choledochal cyst, choledochocele, duodenal intramural tu
