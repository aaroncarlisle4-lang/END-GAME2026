# Finding: Congenital Duodenal Obstruction

**Source section:** DUODENUM
**Quality score:** 0.567
**Differential count:** 8

## Differentials

- bile-stained vomiting delayed until after first feeding and increasing progressively
- Duodenal atresia / severe stenosis
- Annular pancreas
- Midgut volvulus
- Duodenal web
- Ladd bands
- Preduodenal portal vein
- Duodenal duplication cyst Severe duodenal obstruction is characterized by 2 air-fluid levels on the upright view ("double bubble sign"), the largest below the left hemidiaphragm (= gastric distention), the smaller on the right side of the spine (= dilated proximal duodenum).
