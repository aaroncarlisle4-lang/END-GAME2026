# Finding: MYOCARDITIS

**Source section:** MITRAL REGURGITATION
**Quality score:** 0.267
**Differential count:** 5

## Differentials

- Infection: viral (Coxsackie B, other enteroviruses, herpesvirus, adenovirus, parvovirus B19, Epstein-Barr virus); bacterial (diphtheria, Streptococcus); parasitic (Trypanosoma cruzi)
- Autoimmune process
- Cardiotoxic drugs: anthracyclines, trastuzumab, catecholamines, chemotherapy
- Hypersensitivity reaction
- Myocardial infarction / mechanical injury / radiation Rx Histo:   lymphocytic infiltration associated with myocyte injury in the absence of ischemia
  - usually asymptomatic; acute chest pain
  - atrial / ventricular arrhythmias
  - rapid recent onset of heart failure
  - cardiogenic shock, sudden cardiac death (12%)
  - CK-MB (creatine kinase - myocardial b fraction specific for heart muscle) with high specificity but limited sensitivity
  - troponin I more often elevated than CK-MB
